Main screen

Initial entrance

Swatch Welcome to Color Cafe. Let us warm your day.

After exiting a menu

Swatch Don't be blue. We're here for you.

Buy

Items for sale

ITEM Short for ButlerJuice +100HP

ITEM Spaghetti woven by master coders Party +30HP

B.ShotBowtie

ARMOR A handsome bowtie.

RoyalPin

ARMOR Luxurious brooch.

Initial buying

Swatch Our menu is specially prepared.

Making a purchase

If item goes to inventory

Swatch Queen thanks you for your patronage.

If item goes to storage

("it'll" overflows the textbox as in the game.)

Swatch Thanks, it'll be in your STORAGE.

Backing out of a purchase

Swatch Take your time. We'll be waiting.

Not enough D$

Swatch You can't afford it. ... maybe next time?

Storage full

Swatch Seems your bags are full. Shall we assist?

Sell

Swatch Queen... Makes us buy trash for her.

Initial item sale screen

Swatch An ITEM to delight our Queen?

Initial weapon sale screen

Swatch A WEAPON to protect our Queen?

Initial armor sale screen

Swatch ARMOR to adorn our Queen?

Selling with empty inventory

Choosing "Sell Items/Pocket Items" when you have no items, or "Sell Weapons/Armor" when you have no unequipped weapons/armor.

Swatch It seems you don't have anything.

Making a sale

Swatch I'm sure she's going to enjoy this.

Selling the last of your items/armor/weapons

Swatch That's all. Need any help with your bags?

You are returned to the initial Sell screen.

Backing out of a sale

Swatch Changed your mind? We'll wait.

Trying to sell special items

Special items include:

  • The Manual (unavailable by this point)
  • Jevilstail & Devilsknife
  • Dealmaker & Puppet Scarf
  • Thorn Ring
Swatch There are some things we cannot buy.

Empty slot

Swatch Black is the color of sorrow.

Talk

Swatch Chatting would be our pleasure.

About yourself

Swatch Ah, forgive me for not introducing myself sooner, young masters. Swatch I am Swatch, Our Lady Grace's head butler. Swatch When we are not attending to her needs, I and the Queen's other staff, Swatch Use this room as our humble yet colorful gift-shop-slash-cafe. Swatch Please, shop to your heart's content. We are eager to serve your any wish.

Mission Statement

Swatch Does a woman of Our Lady Grace's caliber not deserve - Swatch A coven of powerful men and women to dote upon her every movement? Swatch To have rap anthems written to her kindness -- Swatch Which are then bitcrushed beyond recognition to make them more 'computer'? Swatch To have melons, smashed, with karate chops, repeatedly, for no apparent reason? Swatch To have barrels, thrown, from the rafters -- Swatch And make us smash them all with karate chops before the timer runs out? Swatch But the barrels, are full of melons, and we have to smash those too? Swatch Yes. The answer is... yes.

Rouxls Kaard

Before crossing the acid tunnel

Swatch Rouxls? I am afraid I am not familiar with a guest of that moniker... Swatch Ah, wait. Are you perchance referring to that unbearable - Swatch Err, UNIQUE Man who barged in here and demanded to be made "Butler Supremeth?" Swatch I am afraid that person... is now banned from this establishment. Swatch And may have suffered injuries as a result of his, err... rather swift ejection. Swatch Please do not mention him again. Swatch We are still cleaning up the worms he left on the floor.

After crossing the acid tunnel

Swatch Oh, you defeated that simpleton? Wonderful, wonderful. Swatch After he stole our flower display for one of his "boo-kettes," Swatch Tasque Manager told me she saw him in the hallway, Swatch Where, opening with "Ah, thou muste be 50`% Likelye to be Queene", Swatch He thrust the vase upon her, and asked to be her "minion for life." Swatch ... he was promptly electrocuted to the ground. Swatch Well. Enough of the unpleasantries. Would you like a toasted marshmallow?

Outfit

Available before entering the mansion basement. Gets replaced by Basement.

Swatch Ah, my outfit. Yes, the monochrome look is quite suitable, isn't it? Swatch Black suit, colored glasses. Many people have failed to imitate this look. Swatch Some even going so far as to attempt to impersonate me... Swatch In order to get the... Oh, nevermind. Macaron?

Basement

Available afer entering the basement and before defeating Spamton NEO. Replaces Outfit and gets replaced by Basement Robot.

Swatch What? Basement? There's no basement here. Swatch You need to relax. Have a drink, and talk softly... Swatch You're bothering the other customers.

Basement Robot

Available after defeating Spamton NEO. Replaces Basement.

Swatch ... So that's what happened. Swatch ... Swatch That robot was the embodiment of a Lightner's dream. Swatch A dream I helped create... once. Swatch Splashing color from the motion of their hand. Swatch The Lightner filled it with their own hope, giving it an incredible power. Swatch ... but, in the end, nothing ever came of it. Swatch And it was left in the basement with the rest of the corrupted data. Swatch ... But that crooked salesman! Somehow, he learned about the robot, Swatch And thought he could use its power to fulfill his own twisted dreams. Swatch What a pity. To think he was once a valued customer...

Exit

Swatch Please come again. We'll be waiting for you with rose-tinted glasses...
