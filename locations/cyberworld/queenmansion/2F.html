Dining hall doors

The double doors located at the far end of the large dining hall. Exclusive to the normal route, as the doors are open and can be entered without commentary on the Weird Route.

First time

It's locked... What's with this giant empty room anyway!?

The monitors on either side of the doors turn on, showing Queen.

Welcome To My Extravagant Dining Bonanza

48 tables bearing platters spawn in the air and fall onto the dark spots on the floor.

Only One Of These Delicious Platters Has The Key I Am Sure Searching Will Be An Addicting Game of Chance

The monitors turn off.

H-how the hell are we gonna search all these!?

Control is returned to the player, who can begin checking individual platters.

Subsequent times

(The door is locked.)
