Initial cutscene

Upon entering the mansion and advancing into the foyer, Susie and Ralsei break away from the party line to stand in front of the statue of Rouxls.

Huh...? Isn't this...

Susie turns to Ralsei. A slight pause.

Uhh, that guy from before?

She turns to look at Rouxls again.

Why is there a statue of him? It's... not a statue, Susie. Huh? You see, each Dark Fountain creates a different "world." A "world" whose Darkners reflect the will of its fountain. But, though those Darkners can exist in their own worlds... They might not "belong" if they go to another one.

A pause.

Huh...

Susie turns to Ralsei.

So if we just bring him back to the castle town... ...

She turns back to Rouxls. A slight pause.

Do we, uh, have to do that? Umm, yes. Whatever.

Susie and Ralsei get back in line behind Kris, and control is returned to the player.
