Treasure chest

Exclusive to the normal route.

Located behind a Queen portrait near the bottom of the first room to the east.

When closed

(You found a ReviveMint!)

Proceeds to the generic message for obtaining a regular item. If either the inventory or storage is not full, the Revive Mint is obtained and the chest remains open. Otherwise, if both the inventory and storage are full, the chest closes back up; subsequent interactions will repeat this narration until space is made for this object.

When open

(There's nothing here.)

Swatchlings

Exclusive to the normal route.

Present in the pottery balancing room before the pottery balancing challenge has been completed.

First seven times

Swatchling Please do not race the pottery.

Eighth time onwards

Swatchling Yes, we see you. You are the master of balance.

Queen statues

Exclusive to the normal route.

Located in the storage room to the east, past the traffic crossing.

(You became lost in the craftsmanship.)

Hacker

Exclusive to the normal route.

Present in the storage room, only if all three blue checkmarks were collected in Cyber Field.

Before tripping the hidden switch

The Hacker is walking back and forth through the room. While walking past one vase, his head briefly turns from a regular cursor to an pointing hand, as if about to click on something interactive.

Hacker I'm the hacker. I got the feeling there's a secret backdoor in this room... but... Hacker Where could it be...? Hmmmmmmmmm....

After tripping the hidden switch

The Hacker stands towards the far right of the room.

First time

Hacker I'm retiring from hacking the main frame. Hacker I decided to use my powers for the good of society. Hacker I'm going to reverse engineer the code to Super SMashing Fighters. Hacker So I can put my favorite cartoon characters into the game.

Subsequent times

Hacker Hacking is hard work. Whoof. I need to drink a jpeg of water. (He's using the app that lets you drink water from your phone.)

Hidden pathway switch

Exclusive to the normal route.

Located in the storage room; its position is indicated by the Hacker, if present.

A thud sounds as something opens up.

(A secret switch...!) (You heard something opening.)

Walking left reveals a path leading to the hidden area leading to the basement.

Storage room door

Exclusive to the Weird Route, as this door is open and can be passed through in the normal route.

Located in the hidden area, next to the B1F door.

(It's locked.)

Basement area lock

Exclusive to the normal route.

Located in the hidden area, blocking the way to the basement door.

Before purchasing the KeyGen

It has a lock that seems impossible to open.

After purchasing the KeyGen

(Kris used the KEYGEN.)

The KeyGen is removed from the inventory, and the forcefield disappears with a mysterious jingle. The party can now reach the B1F door.

Basement door

Exclusive to the Weird Route, as this door is open and can be passed through in the normal route (with special cutscenes for the first entry and first exit).

Located in the hidden area, next to the storage room door.

The door is locked and cannot be entered, but may be interacted with. The drawing of Queen on the door is scratched out.
