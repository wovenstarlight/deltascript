Castle doors

Available to interact with only in Chapter 1. From Chapter 2 onwards, the doors can be walked through into the entrance hall on 1F without commentary.

First time

Hey, Kris, I really think we should catch up with Susie. We can come back here after our adventure is over... ... and then I can bake you a yummy cake!

Subsequent times

Kris, perhaps we should save the world first...? It seems a bit important.

Little Ball

Present during Chapter 2. Located towards the left side.

Standard

Little Ball (It's a bit scary moving to a new place. But, as a ball...) Little Ball (There's nothing to sphere except sphere itself.)

If 9 or more Cyber World Darkners were recruited

Is accompanied by Nubert, whose wig the Little Ball is wearing.

Little Ball (With this windfall, I've ceased to be spherical.) Little Ball (Nubert... you're one-of-a-kind.)

Nubert

Present at the end of Chapter 2, if 9 or more Cyber World Darkners were recruited. Located towards the left side, accompanying Little Ball.

Nubert Nubert's had a wild ride. Time to settle down. Nubert Give my riches to the weak...

Royal Coat Rack

Present during Chapter 2. Located to the top right.

Before going to Cyber World

First time

Royal Coat Rack Prince Ralsei, I will keep your hat a-racked until you need it!

Subsequent times

Royal Coat Rack Until then, please allow me to pretend to be a wizard! Hi-ya!

After returning from Cyber World

Is accompanied by Trashy.

Royal Coat Rack Ho ho!! Who is this guy!! He's scary!!

Trashy

Present at the end of Chapter 2. Located near the top right, accompanying the Royal Coat Rack.

Trashy Oh boy, oh boy!! I'm Trashy!! Haha!! Trashy I bet I'm gonna make a lot of new friends!!

Shuttah

Present during Chapter 4, if recruited in Chapter 3. Is taking photos of Tasque, Poppup, Maus, and C. Round (the first three only present if recruited in Chapter 2), all wearing ribbons.

Shuttah Ooo la la. We are doing the wildlife fashion shoot! Shuttah So adirable, so admorable!

Kris, Susie, and Ralsei line up in front of Shuttah and strike poses as Shuttah takes a photo of them and the wildlife. Control then returns to the player.

Shuttah takes a photo of the wildlife.

Shuttah So plorable, so pliable.
