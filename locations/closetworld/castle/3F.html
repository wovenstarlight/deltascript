Tape

Blocking the way to the left end of the corridor at the start of Chapter 4.

Before the tea party in Kris and Susie's combined room

Hey, what's past here...? Um... instead of looking over there... How about seeing the special surprise in your rooms?

After the tea party

Susie breaks through the tape to explore the green door leading to Ralsei's room. The tape is destroyed and cannot be interacted with again.

Tenna's door

Available to interact with only under specific conditions - if Kris is separated from the party, or if Tenna didn't survive Chapter 3. Otherwise, the door can be walked through without commentary.

When Kris is alone

Because Susie and Ralsei have gone ahead into Ralsei's room. Exclusive to Chapter 4.

(Your friends are still in Ralsei's room...)

If Tenna didn't survive Chapter 3

If Tenna was defeated violently, or if the Shadowguys and Pippins weren't recruited and thus didn't help patch him up at the end of the chapter. The door is boarded up. (Gets overridden by the above dialogue when applicable.)

(It seems to be closed...)

Queen's door

Exclusive to Chapter 4. Available to interact with only when Susie and Ralsei have gone ahead into Ralsei's room, leaving Kris alone. Otherwise, the door can be walked through without commentary.

(Your friends are still in Ralsei's room...)
