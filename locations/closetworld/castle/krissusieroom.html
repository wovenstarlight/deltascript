Initial cutscene

Plays upon first entrance into the room.

Kris, Ralsei, and Susie enter the room. Susie scratches her head.

... H-huh? Our rooms... they're... combined?

Ralsei steps forward, turning to face the others.

Umm, just for today! I thought we could use the space...

Ralsei spreads his arms happily.

... For a tea party!

Susie turns to him.

Heh, a tea party?

Ralsei watches as Kris and Susie sit on the center and right chairs.

Hit me, I guess.

Ralsei walks over and pours Susie a cup of tea. She takes a sip and looks surprised.

Hey, this is pretty good! Thanks! I got help from Swatch...

Ralsei turns to Kris.

Kris, would you like any?

Ralsei looks away, going red.

Umm, okay! There's still cake, too, if you'd fancy some... Umm, okay! Here you go...

Ralsei pours tea for Kris.

Susie leans forward and flicks sugar cubes into Kris's cup.

There. Umm, and here's the tea.

Ralsei pours tea for Kris.

Susie takes another sip while Ralsei puts down the teapot and sits in the left chair.

It's kinda funny... I always thought tea parties were for wimps. I mean, don't get me wrong. They still are. But... Just feels like when I'm with you guys... I don't even... need to think about that.

Susie drinks as Ralsei laughs.

Aww, Susie! Guess your stupid attitude's been rubbing off on me. Like... I don't want to say I've been getting soft. But... I just always... HAD to be tough, you know? Now it just... feels like I... have options.

Ralsei laughs again; Susie goes to take a drink.

Aww, Susie!

Susie's eyes widen, cup still held up, as Ralsei continues.

You've changed so tremendously since I met you. No, rather, you've finally just been able to... Be... yourself. I feel... haha, I just feel so proud of you, Susie.

Susie puts down her cup.

Hey, you've changed too, dude. I... I have? Before... you were all stuck up about being good, right? Now... you're actually kinda normal sometimes. I suppose I just... started appreciating you more, Susie. For example, I thought your Rude Buster was scary before... But now, I think it's really b-- Umm, bad-bum! Just say "bad ass", idiot!

Ralsei and Susie both laugh.

You gonna eat a piece or what? Huh? No, that's for you two, haha.

Susie pushes a plate of cake over to a startled Ralsei.

Dude, you made it. You should have some. Umm... okay!

Ralsei tries the cake and looks surprised.

...! Oh! Th... this is really yummy! Why the heck are you so surprised? I... I've actually never eaten cake before... Since, I was making it for you two, after all. ... I can't believe it tastes this good! Haha, dude, you're such a weirdo sometimes. Just have the rest. N-no, I couldn't... Umm, okay! Just, um... later. (I'm, a bit embarrassed how much I liked it...) Haha, Kris, chill. He'll make you more later. Umm, I'd be happy to! Anyways, that was kinda fun. Thanks Ralsei.

The party stands and lines up again.

(Kris, we should do something for Ralsei sometime...)
