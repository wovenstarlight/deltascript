You may have bested me. But... You'll soon face someone... More fearsome... More tyrannical than you could EVER imagine. Queen? We already met her.

WHAT!?

The party members turn to the left as Queen walks in and looks into King's cell. A pause.

Wow Nice Hamster Wheel

So you, too, have come to humiliate me... No I Am Serious It's Cool

Queen walks to the right, stopping just beside King. The party members back out of her way. A pause. Then Queen begins laughing.

That Water Bottle However

King begins drinking from the bottle.

DO NOT SPEAK ILL OF THE ROYAL WATER BOTTLE!

He stops drinking and returns to his spot by the bars, looking over his shoulder at Queen; she turns left, holding a glass of acid.

It's Nothing Compared To The One At My Mansion

Hmph. Materialistic as always.

Queen turns to face him, the glass gone.

Materialism Evaluation Denied I Simply Give Myself: The Treatment I Deserve

Queen turns away from the bars and laughs.

You Slept Curled Up On A Dirty Throne Every Day

ENOUGH, WENCH!!!

What purpose have you here... Queenie Beanie. Just Thought I Would Send A Message, Kingy Wingy That You Don't Have To Worry About Lancer

I Will Be Looking After Him From Now On After All... I Am...

His Mother

When was that decided!?

Queen turns to the left.

Arbitrarily Right Now

I Think It Is Time To Abort This Conversation ... As you wish.

Oho, quite sorry, mine Kinge... Queene hath a nyew Man about Towne now!!

Sigh... That was my son's....

LESSER father.

Cannot Calculate Someone Less Than You

BEGONE!!!! Bye Bye Kingy Wingy

By The Way Did You Want Anything From The Store ... cashews. Order Processed

Chapter 4

When first approaching, if Tenna has been brought to Castle Town

Once they get close, the party gathers in front of the bars and the music goes silent. The bars and the party fade out of sight, clearly showing King inside his cell; Lancer is balanced atop his head.

Don't cry, papa! This jokester will make your belly laugh like jelly!

King turns his head to the left, Lancer turning with him.

A new court jester, eh...? Hm. Let us see it.

Cheery music starts playing as Tenna enters from the left, pointing from left to right.

Tenna Hey, KINGY-DOODLE!! IT'S ME!!! THE NEW FUNNY... MR. ANT TENNA!!

Tenna starts repeatedly throwing his hands in the air.

Tenna I can do dances!! Prances!! Practical jokes!! CLEAN YOUR SON'S EARS!!!

Tenna stops, then moves forward with an air horn in hand that he squeaks twice in King's face. Facing silence, he shifts a little further forward and squeaks the air horn once more, but once again gets no response. After a slight pause, he points at Lancer and King, holding a mic to his face.

Tenna Hey, WHAT did the playing card say to the television!?

A pause. King turns his head to look into his cell, Lancer turning along with him.

Hm... "Leave."

The music cuts off abruptly as King looks at Tenna and speaks this last line. Tenna reels, stunned. After a long pause, he straightens up, resting a hand on his tie with a wide smile.

Tenna Ah. He, uh, he did, huh!

Tenna takes a step back, adjusting his tie and flushing slightly.

Tenna Haha, tough audience today, uh, folks!

He turns away, hands behind his back and head hanging low.

Tenna Maybe I was too modern, too relevant...

He begins to walk away… but slips and falls head over heels.

Tenna Wh-whoops!!! I tripped!!

A pause. Then Lancer and King both burst into laughter. Tenna bolts to his feet and clutches his head, screen dark and thrashing about in distress.

Tenna HEY!!! Don't laugh at my misfortune!!! I..!!

He freezes when King speaks.

Good work, son. I LIKE this one.

After a slight pause, Tenna goes up on his tiptoes with his hands pressed together by his head in delight, wobbling slightly.

Tenna A-heh! I mean, uh, they like me! They really like me!

Bowing repeatedly, Tenna leaves to the left; Lancer follows after him, King watching them leave. Once they're gone, King looks away into the cell. The cell bars and the party members reappear, and with the Castle Town music restarting and the camera panning to focus on the party, control is returned to the player, who may now speak directly to King or walk away.
