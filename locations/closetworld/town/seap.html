Main screen

Initial entrance

Seam Hee hee... Welcome, travellers.

After exiting a menu

Seam Take your time... Ain't like it's better spent.

Buy

Items for sale

Dark Candy

Available initially. If you recruited 9 or more Cyber World Darkners, gets replaced by the CD Bagel after returning from Cyber World. If you didn't recruit 9 or more Cyber World Darkners, gets replaced by Darker Candy from Chapter 4 onwards.

ITEM Star-shape candy that heals 40HP

Darker Candy

Available from Chapter 4 onwards if you didn't recruit 9 or more Cyber World Darkners. Replaces Dark Candy.

ITEM Star-shape candy that heals 120HP

CD Bagel

Available after sealing the Cyber World's Fountain if you recruited 9 or more Darkners there. Replaces Dark Candy.

ITEM Music with each bite heals 80HP

Darkburger

ITEM Mysterious hamburger heals 70HP

Amber Card

ARMOR Defensive charm

Spookysword

WEAPON Black-and orange

Initial buying

Seam What do you like to buy?

Making a purchase

If item goes to inventory

Seam Thanks for that.

If item goes to storage

("it'll" overflows the textbox as in the game.)

Seam Thanks, it'll be in your STORAGE.

Backing out of a purchase

Seam What, not good enough?

Not enough D$

Seam Not enough money.

Storage full

Seam You're carrying too much.

Sell

Seam What kind of junk you got?

Initial item sale screen

Seam Alright, give me an ITEM.

Initial weapon sale screen

Seam What WEAPON will you give me?

Initial armor sale screen

Seam What ARMOR will you give me?

Selling with empty inventory

Choosing "Sell Items/Pocket Items" when you have no items, or "Sell Weapons/Armor" when you have no unequipped weapons/armor.

Seam You don't have anything!

Making a sale

Seam Thanks for that.

Selling the last of your items/armor/weapons

Seam That's it for that.

You are returned to the initial Sell screen.

Backing out of a sale

Seam No?

Trying to sell special items

Special items include:

  • The Manual
  • Jevilstail & Devilsknife
  • Dealmaker & Puppet Scarf
  • Thorn Ring
  • Twisted Sword
  • Blue Ribbon
Seam Y'think I WANT that?

Empty slot

Seam That's nothing.

Talk - Chapter 2

Before Cyber World, if you don't have Jevil's Shadow Crystal

First time

This dialogue also plays after Cyber World in certain cases:

  • First time overall in Chapter 2, if you didn't defeat Spamton NEO.
  • Second time overall in Chapter 2, if you defeated Jevil but not Spamton NEO.
Seam Hmm... So we are now in the Castle Town. Seam Well, now. It makes no difference to me. Seam As long as I stay in my shop, these walls won't change. Seam ... But. Seam In a way, I suppose it's just a little bit amusing to see you again. Seam I'm sure you three will have an interesting journey. Seam Me? I'll just be here offering the same old junk. Seam As you find stronger treasure, I'm sure you'll have little reason to return here. Seam But if you have some time, I'll offer you a biscuit and a hint!

Subsequent times

Before seeing Susie's room
Seam The little prince has something to show you in his castle. Seam Well now, are you going to turn down a royal invitation?
After visiting Susie's room
Seam If I were you, I would head home to the LIGHT WORLD. Seam Of course, I'm not you. So, I'm staying right here. Seam By the by, I can't offer any advice when you're not here... Seam So I'm not sure my hints will be much use after all! Ha ha!

Before Cyber World, if you have Jevil's Shadow Crystal

The Crystal can be obtained either by previously having defeated Jevil on this save file, or defeating him on a different file and retrieving the Crystal from the cliff to the west of Castle Town in this save file.

First time

Seam So we are here now, in a new world. Seam And right off the heels of defeating that clown... Incredible. Seam ... Oh? What's that? It seems like he gave something to you. Seam That's right... You must not have noticed it. Seam That Crystal... It's nearly invisible, but you've been holding it this whole time. Seam Here, I'll take it off your hands and appraise it. Seam ... Seam Incredible! To think he had a Shadow Crystal... Seam Shadow Crystals, so called because you can only see their shadow. Seam ... Seam Call it a premonition, but I get the feeling you may find more of these... Seam ... If you continue to defeat strong adversaries like him, that is. Seam If you can gather more Shadow Crystals, bring them here! Seam I'm sure I can stitch together something incredible for you...

The Shadow Crystal is removed from the inventory.

Subsequent times

If Seam hasn't taken Jevil's Crystal yet

Occurs when the below conditions are met:

  • You continued a Chapter 1 save file where you hadn't defeated Jevil or started Chapter 2 with a fresh save file.
  • You have at least one Chapter 1 save file where you defeated Jevil.
  • You talked to Seam at least once before this in Chapter 2 and then retrieved Jevil's Shadow Crystal from the cliff.
Seam Ah, yes, that's right. It didn't occur to me until just now, but... Seam You three defeated that clown last time, didn't you?

Continues from "Oh? What's that?" in the above section.

After Seam has taken Jevil's Crystal
Seam If you can defeat more strong adversaries, you may find more Shadow Crystals. Seam Try your best! It may be tough, but I'm sure it will be worth it!

After Cyber World, if you defeated Jevil but not Spamton NEO

Seam ... It seems that you didn't get another Shadow Crystal. Seam Hm. I understand. It's no small feat to fight an opponent that has one. Seam ... Seam Well then, forget it all. Seam You're a Lightner. Don't take your life, well, lightly!

After Cyber World, if you defeated Spamton NEO but not Jevil

If this is the first interaction overall

Seam ... What is that? It appears you have a Shadow Crystal. Seam ... Seam Unfortunately, I believe that you are missing one from your previous adventures. Seam ... Seam But, are you sure? Are you sure you didn't defeat that clown...? Seam Perhaps... You just haven't remembered that you had yet. Seam That's right, as long as you ever defeated that enemy in the past... Seam Then perhaps, even now, that Crystal might turn up somewhere close... Perhaps!

If you've obtained Jevil's Shadow Crystal from the cliff, dialogue continues into the next section.

If this is not the first interaction overall

Seam Aha! Another Shadow Crystal! You found it... Seam You can't see it, so perhaps you didn't notice... Seam Didn't you defeat him? Didn't you defeat Jevil? Seam Here, I'll take that Crystal. Now, you have collected 2 of them! Huzzah!

After Cyber World, if you defeated both Jevil and Spamton NEO

First time

Seam Well, what have we here! You managed to find another Shadow Crystal... Seam Oh, is that so? That salesman had found the crystal in the old machine...? Seam I wonder... if perhaps, long ago, that old machine was very important to someone. Seam A certain Lightner, perhaps... or maybe it's just a hunch. Haha! Seam At any rate, it's truly unfortunate a Darkner decided to steal that power... Seam Or, is it fortunate? Because of that, you found a new Crystal. Seam Here, let me hold onto that for you. Ha ha ha!

Second time

Seam You have collected 2 Shadow Crystals. Seam But don't let down your guard. I feel your next opponent may be... hm. Seam In reality, it may be impossible to win. Seam ... Unless you use the power of the Shadow Mantle. Seam Here, it may look like an old scrap of cloth, but... Seam ... eh? Seam I can't find it. Did someone... take it...? Seam Hahaha! Well now, there goes your one chance of victory!

Subsequent times

Seam You have collected 2 Shadow Crystals. Seam Unfortunately, without the Shadow Mantle, at 2 you may remain. Seam Fate is approaching... and it is not on your side.

After Cyber World, if you got neither Shadow Crystal

First time

Seam There are many fresh faces in town, I see. Seam It warms this old fluff to see such hope. Seam I was even surprised to see the Queen again. Seam Same as ever... Well, aren't we all.

Subsequent times

If you recruited 9 or more Cyber World Darkners
Seam Those young men have made an interesting shop. Seam They're leaving the bagel business, so I have the rest of their remaining stock.
If you recruited 8 or fewer Cyber World Darkners
Seam Those three young musical men are thinking about setting up a shop here. Seam I'm sure you'd appreciate having an option other than me, ha ha.
Seam ... By the by, it's growing late, isn't it? Seam You should hurry home once you finish your business here. Seam You don't want to get caught when the sun goes down...

Talk - Chapter 4

Standard

Plays when no Shadow Crystal dialogues (see below sections) take precedence.

First time

"First time" here is in reference to the first time no Shadow Crystal dialogue is triggered, not to the first interaction overall in this chapter.

Seam I've got a funny story. You see, the Addisons wanted to buy out my store. Seam So I bet them for it on a game of cards. Seam Hand after hand, they lost and lost, stubbornly doubling their bet each time. Seam After a while, they'd lost their store, their money, and even some of their clothes... Seam Hahaha! Seam Of course, in the end, I laughed and gave it all back. Seam But it was fun to give them a little scare. Seam ... Although, not as fun as if they could actually win.

Subsequent times

Seam ... It's funny you insist on hanging around here. Seam I won't sell nor say anything new, I'm afraid. Seam I'll brew a cup of tea and get out the blankets, but leave when you please.

If you didn't get the Knight's Shadow Crystal

Seam Ah, I know what you're going to say. Now, now, there's no shame in it. Seam You fought the Knight, didn't you? Well, we all know there's no winning there...

If you got the Shadow Mantle

Seam Even with the SHADOW MANTLE, you might have had a chance... Seam But even then, getting the SHADOW CRYSTAL would be... challenging.

If you didn't get the Shadow Mantle

Seam If only you had the SHADOW MANTLE, your luck might have gone another way. Seam But, it's over now, isn't it? No mantle, no crystal.
Seam Don't have a long face, now. We all knew nothing would come of this. Seam But it was fun... to pretend it might, for a little while. Seam Thank you.

If you got all 3 Shadow Crystals, first time

From Jevil, Spamton NEO, and the Knight.

If you gave Seam both of the previous Shadow Crystals

Seam Ah, I know what you're going to say. Now, now, there's no shame in it. Seam You fought the Knight, didn't you? Well, we all know there's no winning there... Seam ... what!? You GOT the Shadow Crystal!?
If you got the Shadow Mantle
Seam ... I see, so you were able to recover the Shadow Mantle. Seam That's odd. Kris... where did you find it?
If you didn't get the Shadow Mantle
Seam ... And without even using the Shadow Mantle!? Seam Hee hee... You three! You might be even sicker than that old clown! Seam Oh, it must've been a terrific match! If only I could've witnessed it...

If you didn't give Seam both of the previous Shadow Crystals

Seam What!? Is that... a Shadow Crystal!? Seam Meaning, you must have...
Seam ... Well, never mind that! You got the Crystal, that's all that matters! Seam Now you've got three. Only two more, and we should have enough... Seam ... to make something I think you'll quite enjoy. Now good luck... and I'll be taking that Shadow Crystal.

If you got all 3 Shadow Crystals, subsequent times

Overrides regular dialogue.

Seam It's strange. By all reasonable means, the strength of your next opponent... Seam Should be enough to already ring the bell on your defeat. Seam But, for some reason... I can't help but think you may win. Seam What is this feeling? ... Hope? Hahaha! Whatever it is, it's quite funny!

Exit

Seam See you again... Or not. Ha ha ha ha...
