Choosing a fusion

Selecting a viable fusion results a popup along the bottom of the screen asking "Really fuse it?", with options reading "Yes" and "No".

Choosing "Yes"

First time
Malius Huh-hah! Let's feel my technique!
Subsequent times
Malius Let's pick up the pace.

Two small bags appear on the counter beside Malius, with icons denoting them as items/armor/weapons. Malius strikes the bags thrice with their hammer head, making squeaky noises; the first two strikes make the bags increasingly squished, and on the third strike, only one bag is left behind, labelled with the appropriate icon.

(You got <ITEM>!)

The bag disappears and the requested fused item is added to the inventory.

Choosing "No"

The popup disappears, going back to the list of fusions.
