Zapper

Present as a guard outside the Star Changing Room's door after Rounds 1 and 2.

Before gaining access to the Star Changing Room for the most recent round

Initial

Plays only the first time Zapper is spoken to after entering this room.

Zapper Oh, it's you... Let me check if you're on the guest list. Zapper ...

Judgement

Follows the initial dialogue on the first interaction, or plays independently on subsequent interactions. Depends on the most recently obtained board rank.

If you got S-Rank or higher

Instances of "#" are replaced with the last obtained rank and board number, respectively.

Zapper ... I see! #-rank on Board #. Please enjoy our luxury facilities.

Zapper shifts to the side, granting access to the Star Changing Room door.

If you got A-Rank or lower, but purchased the counterfeit S-Rank for this round

Instances of "#" are replaced with the last obtained rank and board number, respectively.

Zapper ... Hmm. Says here you got only #-Rank on Board #. Try harder next time. Zapper ... What? What's that? You have proof of an S-rank right there? Zapper This... Did the S always look this cool and angular? Zapper ... I guess it seems like something Tenna would do... Alright, come on in. (The counterfeit S-rank was used up.)

Zapper shifts to the side, granting access to the Star Changing Room door. The S-Rank stuck to Kris's head disappears.

If you got A-Rank, B-Rank, or C-Rank

Instances of "#" are replaced with the last obtained rank and board number, respectively.

Zapper ... Hmm. Says here you got only #-Rank on Board #. Try harder next time.
If you got Z-Rank
Zapper Oh no, it says here you're here on the ban list. Zapper Yep, right there, above... well, try harder next time.

After gaining access to the Star Changing Room for the most recent round

Zapper Please enjoy.

Backstage doors

Available to interact with only after the Bonus Round, if the Sword Route's second stage wasn't successfully completed.

Ramb

Before completing a stage of the original game for the first time

That is, before you complete the original game's Desert Board after Round 1; or before you complete the original game's Island Board after Round 2, if you didn't play the original game's Desert Board.

First time

Ramb stands in front of the backstage doors.

Ramb Ah, Kris. Look at you, a dressing room big shot! Cheers, cheers.

Ramb shifts to the side, looking conspiratorial.

Ramb So how do you fancy the game, then? Right-o. It ain't much, innit, luv. Ramb Now, I KNEW you wouldn't like it, I KNEW. So listen up... Ramb Y'see, that game you been playin'? It's not, EXACTLY, the original. Ramb Ol' Tenna's done a little somethin' here, a little somethin' there... Ramb And made the whole adventure some big ol' blasted line from A to B. Ramb Primo. Boring. Stuff. Ramb But if I know Kris, and boy do I know Kris... that's not what YOU want, innit? Ramb Freedom. That's what YOU want, chum, freedom! Ramb Well, if I can go hush-hush for a minute here... Ramb Kris, luv. THAT'S what we got backstage. Ramb The ORIGINAL game. Where YOU decide what to do. Ramb All right, enough explaining, more gaming! Ramb I'll keep an eye out for Tenna, you go have some REAL fun, alright, luv?

Ramb moves out of the way of the doors.

Ramb There you go! And, I'm sure you know, but... secret to Tenna, right, luv?

Ramb Ah, Kris. Stick around here long enough, and you'll learn... Ramb Freedom's just a thing for big shots.
