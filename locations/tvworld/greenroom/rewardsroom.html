Lanino

Present only after Round 1's Desert Board is completed.

Initial

Lanino Oh, you three! It is I, Lanino. The Green Room was, BUSY, so I'm in here. Lanino Need any, ahem, assistance from a brave and independent moon?

The party lines up in front of Lanino.

Lanino Certainly. Tomorrow's forecast will be SUN, followed by a light... Lanino ... a light drizzle...

Lanino bursts into tears.

Lanino OH, ELNINA! My SWEETEST CLOUD! She would have LOVED a light drizzle!

Lanino stops crying with a sneeze, now looking downcast.

Lanino Everything reminds me of her! EVERYTHING! Lanino The water cooler... the water warmer... I tried counting sheep for a nap...

Lanino bursts into tears again.

Lanino And all I could imagine was her face! Lanino Without her moisture, my heart and skin have become so... so, dry.

Lanino straightens up, eyes wide.

Lanino Th... that's right! I don't need her! I don't need anyone!

Lanino grins gleefully.

Lanino We'll see how far she gets without ME coordinating her OUTFITS!

Lanino straightens up, eyes wide.

Lanino Th... that's right! I'm the most popular circle in the sky!

Lanino grins gleefully.

Lanino Everyone knows GUYS WITH PAULDRONS are HOT STUFF! I've got this!

Lanino's expression softens to a reassured smile.

Lanino Thanks, Kris! You shone a little light on my life!

Dialogue ends.

After reassuring Lanino
