Lancer

After Round 1

Lancer stands in front of the leftmost doors, facing the rest of the room. Upon interacting with him, the party gathers up in front of him, and Lancer hops in place.

Susie! Other people! You looked so cool gaming! Lancer! You're here for real?! Uh... not real?

Susie turns to the side, her smile gone.

... Hey, wait, if you're in this Dark World, aren't you gonna...

Lancer hops in place again, and she turns back to him, smiling again.

Don't worry, I've still got some time before I petrify!

Lancer does a spin.

Meanwhile, I got a job at the gaming factory. I got my own gamepad and I've only broken it 4 times! Now, I gotta go press buttons for pennies... See you in the funny stages!

With a reversed splat SFX, Lancer squeezes backwards through the gap in the doors behind him.

After Round 2

In the bottom right of the room, between the rightmost TV and cactus, Lancer is on his back on the ground, spinning in place.

Susie! Blue Kris! I'm doing my Hurricane Attack. Want to help!?

If Kris wasn't spinning, they begin spinning, and won't stop until choosing this dialogue option again or leaving the Green Room's main area. Otherwise, if Kris was spinning, they stop.

Obviously. Oooh, carry me! Carry me!

With the item get beeping SFX, Lancer disappears.

(You picked up LANCER into your LANCERs.)

A long pause.

Now, put me down! Put me down!

With the item get beeping SFX, Lancer reappears, still spinning.

(You dropped LANCER from your LANCERs.) Spin, spin, spin, spin, spin, spin, spin, spin...
