Ramb

If the first two stages of the Sword Route were completed, the backstage area is accessible and Ramb is located within.

Before completing the third stage and obtaining the Shadow Mantle

A half-petrified Ramb stands in the corridor leading to the original game's console, blocking the way through and awash in light coming from its room.

Ramb Kris... Kris, that's you, isn't it, luv? Ramb Good show, good show... Ramb Tenna's been up to this and that, that and this... All sorts of boring stuff. Ramb But Kris! Oh, Kris! You and I know... The REAL drama happens BACKSTAGE! Ramb That's why... I got it ready for you, y'know. Ramb One last game. Ramb And I'll keep watch. Ramb Well, just as long as... haha. Just as long as I can. Ramb Now, Kris, I hate to trouble you, I really do, but... Ramb Y'see, I can't really move myself now. Ramb If you could just shove me out of the way... Ramb Yes, that's right! After completing the third stage and obtaining the Shadow Mantle

Ramb is nowhere to be found.

Original game console

(Looks like a game console.) (Play it?)

Without the Odd Controller

The console turns on. The intro sequence of the original game plays, showing a shadowed logo that appears to be the word "MANTLE", but the sequence is abruptly cut short. Instead, the screen shows an error message reading "NO CONTROLLER". After a moment and a burst of static, the console shuts off.

With the Odd Controller

The console turns on. The intro sequence of the original game plays, showing a shadowed logo that appears to be the word "MANTLE", but the sequence is abruptly cut short. Instead, the screen shows an error message reading "NO CONTROLLER".

Kris kneels and plugs the Odd Controller into the console. The game screen begins flashing until, with a high-pitched noise, the SOUL appears on screen. With a series of softening beeps, the Desert Board (after Round 1) / Island Board (after Round 2) / City Board (after the Bonus Round) is overlaid on the screen in steps, with HERO_SWORD appearing over the SOUL, and gameplay begins.

Dialogue ends.
