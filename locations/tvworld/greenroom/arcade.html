Door to Monster Movie

Before unlocking the game

A sunglasses-wearing Pippins stands in front of the doors, jumping up and down. Upon interacting with them, your current money and POINTs totals are displayed above the dialogue box.

Pippins Hey, hey! This here's an extra special game... It's LOCKED, you know! Pippins That is... unless you pay 500 POINTs.

If you have 500 POINTs or more

Pippins stops jumping. 500 POINTs are deducted from your score.

(You handed over 500 POINTs.)

The party lines up in front of Pippins.

Pippins Alright, alright! Now that we've got those...

Pippins turns around and steps up to the doors.

Pippins Lock opening noise... Lock opening noise...

A beep. Pippins turns back to the party and steps to the side.

Look at that, would ya! The lock's all gone! Not a trace! You'd hardly believe it was ever there at all! Well, see ya! ... no refunds!

Pippins speeds offscreen to the right, presumably leaving the Arcade. The game is now unlocked.

If you have less than 500 POINTs

Pippins stops jumping. The sound of POINTs being deducted plays, but none are actually taken.

(You handed over 500 POINTs.)

The party lines up in front of Pippins.

Pippins Haha, thanks for the points... sucker!

Pippins moves to the side, revealing no lock on the doors to the game. But after a moment, Pippins moves back in front of the doors.

Pippins ... is what I would say if you had enough POINTs! Pippins Gee, try being responsible and saving 'em up, would ya!?

Nothing happens; dialogue ends.

 Pippins Seriously? A REAL gamer would pay ANY transaction!

Nothing happens. Dialogue ends.
