Main screen

Initial entrance

Seam Hee hee... Welcome, travellers.

After exiting a menu

Seam Take your time... Ain't like it's better spent.

Buy

Items for sale

Dark Candy

ITEM Star-shape candy that heals 40HP

Darkburger

ITEM Mysterious hamburger heals 70HP

Amber Card

ARMOR Defensive charm

Spookysword

WEAPON Black-and orange

Initial buying

Seam What do you like to buy?

Making a purchase

Seam Thanks for that.

Backing out of a purchase

Seam What, not good enough?

Not enough D$

Seam Not enough money.

Storage full

Seam You're carrying too much.

Sell

Seam What kind of junk you got?

Initial item sale screen

Seam Alright, give me an ITEM.

Initial weapon sale screen

Seam What WEAPON will you give me?

Initial armor sale screen

Seam What ARMOR will you give me?

Selling with empty inventory

Choosing "Sell Items" when you have no items, or "Sell Weapons/Armor" when you have no unequipped weapons/armor.

Seam You don't have anything!

Making a sale

Seam Thanks for that.

Selling the last of your items/armor/weapons

Seam That's it for that.

You are returned to the initial Sell screen.

Backing out of a sale

Seam No?

Trying to sell the Manual

Seam Y'think I WANT that?

Empty slot, or trying to sell the Devilsknife/Jevilstail

Seam That's nothing.

Talk

Seam Don't have anything better to do.

About yourself

Seam The name's Seam. Pronounced "Shawm." Seam And this is my little Seap. Ha ha ha ha... Seam Over the years, I've collected odds and ends. Seam 'Course, I've no attachment to any of it. It's just a hobby of mine. Seam Around here, you learn to find ways to pass the time... ... or go mad like everyone else.

Lightners

Seam Long ago, the Darkners lived in harmony with the Lightners. Seam They were like Gods to us. Our protectors. Our creators. Those who gave us purpose... Seam Then, one day we were all locked away in this prison... And the Lightners never returned. Seam Embittered, the King took up arms, and aims to take revenge upon the Lightners that left us behind. Seam 'Course, even among his troops, some still distantly hope the Lightners will return...

Kingdom

Available before defeating Jevil. Gets replaced by About JEVIL.

Seam Historically, this land was ruled by the Four Kings, from CARD CASTLE to the East. Seam But, recently, a strange knight appeared... And three of the kings were locked away. Seam The remaining king put him and his strange son into power. Seam This land hasn't seen THIS much chaos since... Seam Ha ha ha... Well, you don't need to know about THAT.

About JEVIL

Available afer defeating Jevil. Replaces Kingdom.

Seam He... was a funny little man. Once, the court jester. I, the court magician. Seam A friend...? Could I consider him a friend...? Perhaps, perhaps not. Seam But he was the only one who matched me in the games we used to play. Seam One day, he met a strange someone... and since then, he began to change. Seam He started saying bizarre things that didn't completely make sense - Seam But didn't completely not make sense, either. Seam Soon, he began to see the world as a game, and everyone as its participants. Seam As the court mage, and his only companion, I was forced to lock him away... Seam Or, rather, lock US all away, in his own words. Seam Since that time, the strange words he's said have stuck inside my cotton... Seam And my view of this world has become darker, yet darker. Seam Reflecting on these old memories, I think, perhaps - I miss playing games with him. Seam And I wonder, if I hadn't been asked to lock him up... Seam Would I have found a little more purpose in my life...?

We're legendary

Available before talking to Jevil for the first time. Gets replaced by Strange Prisoner.

Seam Ha ha ha ... So you are the "heroes" who are going to seal our Fountain? Seam Ha, good luck. It makes no difference to me. Seam Neither Light nor Dark hold a future for a Darkner in my condition.

Strange Prisoner

Available after talking to Jevil for the first time. Replaces We're legendary and gets replaced by We Won. Appears in yellow initially, then changes to white after being selected for the first time.

First time

Seam Eh...? What are you talking about...? A prisoner with an odd manner...? Seam ... No, you couldn't possibly be talking about HIM...? Seam ... I see. After all the trouble I went through to lock him up, you want to release him? Seam ... Seam Hmm, now that you mention it, it could be troublesome if he was left alone... Seam Yes, perhaps it's better if you Heroes dealt with him now...? Seam After all, it's not as if whatever happens, will matter in the end. Seam Perhaps a little chaos might be fun. Seam So then, take this. (BROKEN KEY A was added to your KEY ITEMS.) Seam If you're so driven, find the other two pieces, and get someone to fix them. Seam I'll even give you a hint - Walk where the stars don't shine. Seam Anyhow, if you succeed, come back here and tell me! Good luck!! Seam If you can call THIS luck... No, it's more like a curse...!

Broken Key A is added to the inventory.

Before opening the door

Seam Trouble opening the door...? Seam Ha ha ha, well, whether you can or not, it doesn't matter to me. Seam But here's a hint - walk where the stars don't shine.

After opening the door but before defeating Jevil

Seam You already opened the door...? Seam Ha ha ha... I almost wish I could watch! Tell me what happens! Seam Ha ha ha... though, I suppose if you don't come back, I'll understand, too!

We Won

Available after defeating Jevil. Replaces Strange Prisoner.

Seam You defeated him!? You REALLY defeated him!? Seam I see... perhaps you three may truly be "Heroes" after all... Seam But JEVIL was just a taste of what you'll face from now on. Seam One day soon... Seam You too, will begin to realize the futility of your actions. Seam Ha ha ha... Seam At that time, feel free to come back here. I'll make you tea... Seam And we can toast... to the end of the world!

Exit

Seam See you again... Or not. Ha ha ha ha...
