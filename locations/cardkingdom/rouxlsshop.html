Main screen

Initial entrance

Before fighting K. Round for the second time

Rouxls Welcome, welcome, my Heroick Comrades!

After fighting K. Round for the second time

Rouxls Welcome, welcome, my Heroick Comrades!

After exiting a menu

Rouxls Anything else?

Buy

Items for sale

Rouxls Roux

ITEM Fragrant sauce heals 50HP

Brave Ax

WEAPON Heroic & Cool

Dainty Scarf

WEAPON Homemade Healing up

Amber Card

ARMOR Defensive charm

Initial buying

Rouxls What shalt thou buy?

Considering a purchase

Buyeth for $[VALUE] ?

Making a purchase

Before fighting K. Round for the second time

Rouxls Thanks, worm.

After fighting K. Round for the second time

Rouxls Thanks, hero!

Backing out of a purchase

Rouxls Perhaps another ware?

Not enough D$

Rouxls Your funds are lacking.

Storage full

Rouxls Thou doth carry too much!

Sell

Rouxls Showeth me your treasure!

You are given the options "Selleth Items", "Selleth Weapons", "Selleth Armor", and "Returnst".

Initial item sale screen

Rouxls What ITEM shalt thou proffer?

Initial weapon sale screen

Rouxls What WEAPON shalt thou proffer?

Initial armor sale screen

Rouxls What ARMOR shalt thou proffer?

Selling with empty inventory

Choosing "Selleth Items" when you have no items, or "Selleth Weapons/Armor" when you have no unequipped weapons/armor.

Rouxls You have none!

Making a sale

Rouxls I shall make use of this!

Selling the last of your items/armor/weapons

Rouxls That seems to be all, no?

You are returned to the initial Sell screen.

Backing out of a sale, empty slot, or trying to sell the Devilsknife/Jevilstail

Rouxls What?

Trying to sell the Manual

Rouxls Er, NO thank you.

Talk

Rouxls Ah, what is thine Intereste?

About yourself

Rouxls I amst the Duke of Puzzles, Rouxls Kaard. Rouxls My hobbies includest calligraphy, lawkeeping, bugkeeping, cages... Rouxls ... and long walks in the dungeon.

Before fighting K. Round for the second time

Rouxls Artest thou one of my admirers, worm?

After fighting K. Round for the second time

Rouxls By the way, no hard feelingst about earlier, right?

Why sell to us

Before fighting K. Round for the second time

Rouxls Hahaha!! So I might amasseth funds, Worms! An army of funds... Rouxls So that I may crushest thou like Fermented Grapes, into a most delicious and adult beverage - Rouxls Vengeance!! Ahahaha!!! Rouxls Oh, how I amuse myself!!!

After fighting K. Round for the second time

Rouxls Haha, why of course I wouldst sell to my compatriarts, the Heroes Of Legend! Rouxls Earlier art but a Test of your Heroick Resolve! Rouxls Rouxls is ALWAYS on the winning side!! Thou can bet on that!!!

The King

Before fighting K. Round for the second time

Rouxls Our Lorde King art a cruel and terrifying Monarcke! Rouxls However, he is also Brilliant, for he decidedesteth to appoinst ME, as Duke of Puzzles, Rouxls After pretty mucheth everyone else was firedst or placed inside a cage. Rouxls Truly, a benevolent ruler!

After fighting K. Round for the second time

Rouxls Yes, the King! A terrible Man! An awful Monarcke! Rouxls Never likedeth that Guye, really! Rouxls I just envisionethed, perhaps... If I acceptedst this High Ranking Positione... Rouxls I could assist to overthroweth him from the Inside! Rouxls ... What!? Cannot thou conceiveth of my plan?!?

The King's Son

Before fighting K. Round for the second time

Rouxls His Son, on the other hand, is more troublesome than a fistful of fleas! Rouxls He alwayst asks me the Worste Queries, Rouxls And calleth me his "Lesser Dad." Rouxls Our relation is NONE, you little water-beetle! I placate you for your Father's sake! Rouxls I do not Wisheth to hear your MP3's! I would rather listen to the sweet song of Death! Rouxls ... Rouxls How is he, anyhow?

After fighting K. Round for the second time

Rouxls Perhaps my wording art a bit Harshe earlier... The King's offspring is a Darling! Rouxls A strange and irritating darling! Rouxls When his Father does not cook, his Son yells like a baby bird, until I feed him worms! Rouxls Is this what it means to be High Ranking!?

Exit

Before fighting K. Round for the second time

Rouxls See thou in the Dungeon, Worms!

After fighting K. Round for the second time

Rouxls Come by any time, my good Worms!
