Flavor text

Initial encounter

Appears with 1 or 2 other Ponmen.

Ponman drew near!

Standard

Ponman advances one step at a time. Ponman listens politely, despite having no ears. Ponman seems hypnotized by your idle animation. Ponman gazes enigmatically. Smells like a pawn shop.

Tired

Ponman can't keep its eye open.

Unused "sleepy" variant

Intended for when the "Lullaby" act was used on another Ponman in the encounter, making this one Tired. (Unused because it got assigned to the wrong variable.)

The enemies became SLEEPY from Ralsei's lullaby!

Low HP

Ponman looks dilated.

Spareable

Unused. (Got assigned to the wrong variable.)

Ponman is sleeping soundly.

Check

PONMAN - AT 8 DF 0 Its nucleus doubles as an eyespot.

Goodnight

You whispered goodnight to Ponman. It fell asleep...

Ponman falls asleep, gaining 100% Mercy, though this can be lost if it's awoken by being attacked.

{% include acticon chara="Ralsei" -%} Lullaby

Ralsei sang a soft and entrancing lullaby!

Ralsei sings to the enemies.

If Susie is in the party

PONMAN fell asleep! SUSIE fell asleep! The enemies became TIRED!

If Susie is not in the party

PONMAN fell asleep! The enemies became TIRED!

Ponman falls asleep, gaining 100% Mercy, though this can be lost if it's awoken by being attacked. Any other enemies become Tired.

{% include acticon chara="Ralsei" -%} Warning

Available before Susie joins the party for real.

Susie's attacks will miss from now on.

When against a lone Ponman

You and Ralsei warned Ponman about Susie. The enemy went on guard...

When against multiple enemies

You and Ralsei warned the enemies about Susie. Everyone went on guard.

Dialogue

No dialogue; Ponmen do not talk.
