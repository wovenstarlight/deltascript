General flavor text

Initial encounter

Two bad guys blocked the way!

Standard

Susie and Lancer are making fun of you. Susie and Lancer are gloating about how great their team is. Susie and Lancer are having an evil laugh contest. Susie and Lancer are coming up with victory celebration plans. Smells like teamwork.

Susie

Susie Heh, scared? Susie Weasel out of THIS! Susie Talk your way out of THIS! Susie Think fast!

Lancer

Lancer Hey!! Hey Susie!! Watch me, watch me!! Lancer Prepare for smashing, you luxurious melons!!! Lancer Horror! Pain! The power of REAL bad guys! (Nice, huh?) Lancer This bike is fueled by EVIL! ...and friendship!

Lancer Ow, my guts hurt!

If only Lancer has been attacked

Susie Hey, stop ganging up on him!

Susie Hey, stop hitting him!
