--- title: Dark World items description: >

Descriptions and usage/toss text for Dark World items.

For overworld usage, reactions from characters on whom the item is not being used occur only if that character is also in the party at time of use.

Usage messages including "[Character]" can substitute the names "Kris"/"Susie"/"Ralsei"/"Noelle", depending on who the item was used on.

Any heal amounts listed on this page are the base values, not adjusted by heal-buffing equipment like the Blue Ribbon.
