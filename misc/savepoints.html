Eye Puzzle

You bathed your body in the light. A power shines within you, breaking through the darkness. The pain you had melted away... (HP fully restored.)

If Kris's HP is below 30

The "spores" don't do enough damage to lower Kris's HP this much unless you reenter the room and get hit multiple times.

For some reason, you punished yourself with the spores. The light relieves you... (HP fully restored.)

If Kris's HP is at max

You bathed your body in the light. A power shines within you, breaking through the darkness. Any pain you may have had melted away... (HP fully restored.)

Castle Town

Before Ralsei joins the party

In front of you, a castle looms beneath the empty town. A black geyser emerges from it, piercing endlessly into the sky. The power of this place shines within you.

After Ralsei joins the party

Ralsei, the lonely prince, is now your ally. The power of fluffy boys shines within you.

Field - Great Door

Before Susie "joins" the party

With the door closed behind you, your adventure will truly begin... The power of adventure shines within you.

After Susie "joins" the party

The door is still closed. The power of backtracking shines within you.

Field - Seam's Shop

If Susie is in the party

Susie, the violet tormentor, is now your ally. The power of mean girls shines within you.

If Susie is not in the party

Susie, the violet tormentor, left your party immediately. The power of mean girls does not shine within you.

Field - Great Board

Picked Susie's team name

The power of The $!$? Squad shines within you.

Picked Lancer's team name

The power of The Lancer Fan Club shines within you.

Picked Ralsei's team name

The power of The Fun Gang shines within you.

Error

Displays if your team name choice wasn't stored properly.

Forest - Entrance

The blocky foliage grows thick above your head... The power of the forest shines within you.

Forest - Bake Sale

Chapter 2

Castle Town

Before retrieving the Card Kingdom residents

You look upon the castle you first saw yesterday... You are filled with the power of immediate nostalgia.

After retrieving the Card Kingdom residents and before visiting the Cyber World

The castle town has transformed from the power of friendship. You are filled with the power of friendship-based architecture.

After returning from the Cyber World

Cyber Field - Entrance

If you haven't reached Cyber City yet

A green field extends before you... And, in the distance, a city shines brightly. You are filled with the power of a new adventure.

If you've entered Cyber City before

A green field extends before you... You've seen this already. You are filled with the power of pointless backtracking.

Cyber Field - Arcade Machine

The game is wrecked. You are filled with the power of videogame violence.

Cyber Field - Music Shop

Crash! Boom! Bang! There's a terrific noise coming from the building nearby... You are filled with the power of noise music.

Cyber City - Entrance

If Kris is alone

You have neither fluffy boys nor mean girls with you now. Reluctantly, you are filled with the power from the cat sign.

If Noelle is in the party

Noelle is with you now. You are still filled with the power from the cat sign.

If Susie and Ralsei are in the party

You are mostly filled with the power of fluffy boys and mean girls. The cat sign still comprises about one percent of it.

Cyber City - First Alleyway

If Noelle is in the party

Noelle has joined your party. You are filled with the power of not knowing what a sugarplum is.

If Noelle is not in the party

But Susie and Ralsei are!

Noelle is no longer in your party. All of you are filled with the power... Of not knowing what a sugarplum is.

Cyber City - Music Shop

If you haven't entered Queen's Mansion yet

Energetic music wafts from the boxes. You are filled with the power of musical bagels.

If you've been to Queen's Mansion before

You are filled with the power of silence.

Cyber City - Second Alleyway

After defeating Berdly, a certain scent hangs in the air... You are filled with the power of chicken nuggets.

Queen's Mansion - Guest Hall

You have escaped from your second personalized room of the day. You are filled with the power of vacation homes.

Queen's Mansion - Entrance

Looking at these legs fills you with a certain power.

Queen's Mansion - 3F

Susie has gone into a book hole with Berdly. You are filled with the power of book holes.

Queen's Mansion - Acid Tunnel

Everyone is together, but the wind blows coldly... You are filled with a certain power.

Weird Route differences

Beginning

At the start of the Weird Route, as early as freezing the tutorial Virovirokun, all save point narration disappears. You only get the save menu with no accompanying commentary.

After forcefield puzzle

Up until you first enter Queen's Mansion, if Noelle is in the party, any save point will display the following narration:

You and Noelle were filled with power.

Castle Town, after scaring Noelle in the hospital

This accompanies the Castle Town save point after you've closed the Cyber World's Dark Fountain, and overrides the normal text associated with it, including the warning about not being able to return.

Precondition: In the hospital, when Kris joins Noelle outside Rudy's room, you must scare Noelle by stepping towards her (moving right) thrice.

After a long day, you have returned to the castle town. But, you still feel the power in your hands...
