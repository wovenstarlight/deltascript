Treasure chests

Closed chest containing a regular item

(You opened the treasure chest.) (Inside was <Item>.)

If the inventory isn't full

(<Item> was added to your ITEMs.)

The item is added to the inventory; the chest is now open.

If the inventory is full but storage isn't full

(<Item> was added to your STORAGE.)

The item is added to the inventory; the chest is now open.

If both the inventory and storage are full

(You have too many ITEMs to take <Item>.)

The treasure chest closes back up.
