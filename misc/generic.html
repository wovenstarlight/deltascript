Obtaining an item/equipment/money

Regular items

If the inventory isn't full

(<Item> was added to your ITEMs.)

The item is added to the inventory.

If the inventory is full but storage isn't full

(<Item> was added to your STORAGE.)

The item is added to the inventory.

If both the inventory and storage are full

(You have too many ITEMs to take <Item>.)

Nothing happens.

Key items

If the key item inventory isn't full

(<Key Item> was added to your KEY ITEMs.)

The key item is added to the inventory.

If the key item inventory is full

(You have too many KEY ITEMs to take <Key Item>.)

Nothing happens.

Armor

If the armor inventory isn't full

(<Armor> was added to your ARMORs.)

The armor is added to the inventory.

If the armor inventory is full

(You have too many ARMORs to take <Armor>.)

Nothing happens.

Weapons

If the weapon inventory isn't full

(<Weapon> was added to your WEAPONs.)

The weapon is added to the inventory.

If the weapon inventory is full

(You have too many WEAPONs to take <Weapon>.)

Nothing happens.

Money

(If D$1 is being awarded, the below narration will read "1 Dark Dollars".)

(<#> Dark Dollars was added to your MONEY HOLE.)

$<#> is added to your total money.

POINTs

(If 1 POINT is being awarded, the below narration will read "1 POINTs".)

(<#> POINTs was added to your SCORE.)

$<#> is added to your score, up to a maximum of 9999 POINTs.

Treasure chests

When closed

Standard

The chest opens up.

(You opened the treasure chest.) (Inside was <Object>.)

Proceeds to the generic message for obtaining a regular item, key item, armor, weapon, or money. If the relevant inventory (and storage, for regular items) is not full, the item is obtained and the chest remains open. Otherwise, if the relevant inventory/storage is full, the chest closes back up; subsequent interactions will repeat this narration until space is made in the relevant inventory for this object.

Contains nothing

{%- comment %} #mynothing {% endcomment %} (You opened the treasure chest.) (Inside was nothing.)

Open chest

(The chest is empty.)

Unused fallback

For if the chest is somehow neither open nor closed.

(It won't open.)

Vending machines

Present in Chapter 3.

Initial menu

(It's a vending machine.)

The menu on the right offers varying options depending on the machine, often including "Buy". The last option is always "Exit".

Buy

Initial buying

Also used when backing out of a purchase.

(Select an item.)

Making a purchase

Dark Dollars/POINTs are deducted from your total money/score, as applicable.

If item goes to inventory
(Item bought.)
If item goes to storage
(Item placed in STORAGE.)

Not enough currency

If the machine takes POINTs
(Not enough points.)
If the machine takes Dark Dollars
(Not enough money.)

Inventory/storage full

(Not enough space.)

Exit

Dialogue/narration ends silently, and control is returned to the player.
