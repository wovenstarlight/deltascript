Layout

The POWER menu has 3 sections, each with labels across the top.

CHAR

The top section, stretching across the upper third of the menu.

The left side of this section shows icons for each party member. The currently-selected party member has the SOUL over their icon, bracketed by red arrows, and their name is displayed above all the party member icons.

The right side of this section shows the currently-selected party member's title, which varies depending on the chapter and plot progression.

STATS

The bottom left section.

Displays 6 stat fields, in a format similar to the one below. Attack, Defense, Magic, and Guts are stats possessed by all characters; the first three's values vary depending on the weapon and armor equipped to each party member, while Guts depends on the character and chapter. The remaining two fields are purely cosmetic and vary depending on the character, chapter, and plot progression; see each character's section below for their respective values.

SPELLS

The bottom right section.

Displays spells belonging to the currently-selected party member, along with their TP costs. Pressing [CONFIRM] on the party member you're hovering over allows you to go through the spell list and view a description for each spell.

Coldness and Boldness are both variable values.

  • Coldness: 47 initially. Increases by 7 for each use of Ice Shock. Maxes out at 100.
  • Boldness: Equivalent to 3× plot - 222, where plot tracks the progression of the plot. The calculated Boldness values are as follows:
    • -12 initially
    • -6 after the first mouse puzzle
    • 3 after the gold Berdly statue appears
    • 9 after the second mouse puzzle
    • 12 after the third mouse puzzle
    • 15 after the second Berdly fight
    • 18 after the first half of the car ride with Queen
    • 33 after the Spamton fight
    • 48 after the second half of the car ride with Queen
    • 60 after the puzzle spelling out "DECEMBER"
    • 63 after the party reunites
    • 75 after the party is captured by Queen
