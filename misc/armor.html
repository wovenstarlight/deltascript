Notes

Armor can normally be equipped by all party members, unless specified otherwise.

Attempting to remove armor

Most characters allow you to remove their equipment (leaving that armor slot blank, instead of replacing it with another armor) without saying anything. The exception is Susie, who reacts as follows:

Susie Hey, hands off!

Susie's armor is not removed.
