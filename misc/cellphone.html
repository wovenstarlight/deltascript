In the Light World

Standard

Selecting "CELL" in the menu gives the following options:

  • Call Home
  • (Not) Sans's Number (Chapter 1 only, after talking to Sans)

Chapter 4, after the closet cutscene at Noelle's house

Selecting "CELL" in the menu gives the following narration:

(You checked your phone's contacts and recent dials.) (... but everything has been deleted.)

Call Home

Also labelled "Call Toriel" under certain circumstances.

If no one is at home

Ring... No one picked up.

If everyone at home is busy

(Ring, ring...) (Everybody seems too busy to pick up the phone...)

While inside the Dreemurr residence

Standard text for making calls while at Kris's home. May be overridden by chapter-specific dialogue.

In Kris's room, the upper floor hallway, or the bathroom

Chapter 4 v0.0.098 onwards: after breakfast, since Toriel is waiting outside the house, this dialogue gets overridden by the text for everyone being busy.

Ring... Hello? Dreemurr residence... Who might this be...? ... Kris? Sigh... Do not make me come over there. Click...

In the kitchen / living room

Ring... (The phone is ringing, but you can't get it.) (You're already on the phone, after all...)

Chapter 1

After leaving the Card Kingdom, at which point school is over for the day.

First time

Also plays when leaving the school, if you haven't already called Toriel.

(Ring, ring...) K... Kris!? Whatever have you been doing!? I sat in the car waiting for you after school for half an hour...! I called and called, but you never picked up... You had your poor mother worried sick! Kris, I am afraid I am going to have to punish you... ... huh? ... A friend? You were spending time with... a friend? ... Kris... I will make an exception this time. You can continue your adventures a little longer. But when you return home... You are going to have to go to bed early. See you soon, honey. (Click...)

Second time

(Ring, ring...) Kris, honey, what is it? What? No, I am not giving you a ride home. Our house is not far. It is just at the top of town. Try walking. It builds character, honey. (Click...)

Subsequent times

(Ring, ring...) Kris, I am grading papers. I can talk to you when you get back, alright? Our house is only at the top of town, you know. (Click...)

Chapter 2

In the school hallways, after obtaining the trash orb

At this point only, the number is labelled Call Toriel instead of Call Home.

First time
(Ring, ring...) Oh, excuse me, I seem to be receiving a phone call. Kris...? What is it, honey?
If you haven't overheard Alphys and Toriel's conversation
What? You are "normal?"
If you overheard Alphys and Toriel's conversation
What? You made a "trash orb"?
... Could you explain what you mean by that? (Kris, d-don't give the phone to me!!!) Oh, you are going to the library later? Well, thank you for telling me this time! Have a nice time! (Click...)
Subsequent times
(Ring, ring...) Kris, do not worry about the library. (Click...)

After being banished from Castle Town to do schoolwork

First time
(Ring, ring...) Ah, Kris! You are going to do your project...? Oh, are you calling to ask for my help!? I will go find our homemade scented glue-sticks! ... Oh? You have a partner this time? Kris!! That's wonderful!! I hope they like butterscotch-scented gluesticks! (Click...)
Subsequent times
(Ring, ring...) Kris, do not worry about going to the library. If Berdly harasses you about our debt... I will come and throw a smoke bomb so you can escape. ... maybe not. Hee hee hee. Try your best, honey. (Click...)

After returning from Cyber World, before going home

First time
(Ring, ring...) Hi honey! How was your group project? I can give you a ride home in a minute, I am just... ... oh? No need? You are still spending time with a friend...? Oh dear! Two days in a row, Kris! Is that a record? Have fun, honey! Be back before sunset! (Click...)
Subsequent times
(Ring, ring...) Calling me again? What must your friend think? Have fun with your friend, honey! (Click...)

After going home

Overrides the standard Dreemurr residence call dialogue.

First time
If you're in the kitchen / living room
(Ring, ring...) Kris, the phone is going. Would you mind getting it for me while I... ... Kris? Why are you calling our house?
If you're in the bathroom
(Ring, ring...) Hello, Dreemurr residence. What? You would like to talk to "Susie"? ... Of course, here you are. KRIS FINISH WASHING YOUR DAMN HANDS!!!
Subsequent times
(Ring, ring...) (Everybody seems too busy to pick up the phone...)

(Not) Sans's Number

Available only in Chapter 1, after asking Sans about friends and suggesting that he befriend you. Initially appears as "Sans's Number", and gets renamed to "Not Sans's Number" after the first time it's called.

First time

(Ring, ring...) Operator Hello. Operator You have reached the Hotline for Idiot Babies. Operator Please press 1 for Idiots, 2 for Babies, 3 for Idiot Babies, and 4 for None. Operator Ah, you're an Idiot? Operator ... Operator To tell you the truth, I'm one too. Operator Let's try our best, OK? Operator (Click...)

This number gets relabelled "Not Sans's Number".

 Operator Ah, you're an Baby? Operator ... Operator To tell you the truth, I'm one too. Operator Let's try our best, OK? Operator (Click...)

This number gets relabelled "Not Sans's Number".

 Operator Ah, you're an Idiot Baby? Operator ... Operator To tell you the truth, I'm one too. Operator Let's try our best, OK? Operator (Click...)

This number gets relabelled "Not Sans's Number".

 Operator Why'd you call the Hotline for Idiot Babies if you aren't an Idiot Baby? Operator (Click...)

This number gets relabelled "Not Sans's Number".

Unused fallback

For if you somehow responded with something other than 1, 2, 3, or 4.

Operator Huh!?

Subsequent times

(Ring, ring...) (Ring, ring...) (Ring, ring...) (The phone kept ringing infinitely.)
