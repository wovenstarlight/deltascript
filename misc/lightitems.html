Shared text

Text that's identical for all items, unless specified otherwise in their sections.

Dropping an item

Normal

The [ITEM] was thrown away.

Rare messages

There is a 4 in 30 chance that the normal drop text will be overridden by one of the following messages:

You bid a quiet farewell to the [ITEM]. You put the [ITEM] on the ground and gave it a little pat. You threw the [ITEM] on the ground like the piece of trash it is. You abandoned the [ITEM].

Attempting to drop a weapon

During Chapter 4.

(Recently, seems like weapons can't be thrown away so easily.)

Bandage

Info

"Bandage" - Heals 10 HP It has cartoon characters on it.

Use

The Bandage is removed from the inventory and replaced by the previously equipped armor.

If Kris's HP was 18 or less

You re-applied the bandage. You recovered 1 HP!

Kris's HP goes up by one.

If Kris's HP was at 19 or higher

You re-applied the bandage. Your HP was maxed out.
