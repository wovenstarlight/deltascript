Pacify

Available to Ralsei from the start of the game.

Description

Overworld

SPARE a tired enemy by putting them to sleep.

In battle

Spare TIRED foe 16% TP

Usage

If the targeted enemy is Tired

Ralsei cast PACIFY!

The enemy is spared.

If the targeted enemy is not Tired and has less than 100% Mercy

Ralsei cast PACIFY! But the enemy wasn't TIRED...

Nothing happens.

If the targeted enemy is not Tired and has 100% Mercy

Ralsei cast PACIFY! But the foe wasn't TIRED... try SPARING!

Nothing happens.
