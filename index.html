Site tips

Check the bottom right menu for extra features:

  • Compact mode: Just the dialogue, no sprites, so you can focus on what's being said.
  • Dim colors: Use slightly darker colors across the site, if the stark white-on-black strains your eyes.
  • Animations: Toggles animated images (replaced with static PNGs) and any text effects such as shaking text (replaced with italicized or otherwise statically-formatted text).
  • Dyslexia-friendly mode: Uses the OpenDyslexic font for easier reading.
  • Show all choices: Expands the results of all choices for easier reading.
