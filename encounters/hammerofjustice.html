Progressing the fight

Various actions can impress the Hammer of Justice, indicated by Mercy stars appearing over his head (see the Deltarune Wiki for a list of effective actions). The fight progresses as more stars are acquired.

Using Rude Buster

See the Spells page for more information on this spell, including description and cost.

Usage

Susie used RUDE BUSTER!

Susie lets loose a standard Rude Buster attack, sending an arrow of magic at Gerson. However, when it reaches him, he strikes it with his hammer and sends it back at Susie, with a warning message reading "PRESS <CONFIRM>". The first time this happens, the deflected attack slows down to give enough time to read this warning.

If [CONFIRM] is pressed in time, Susie counter-deflects the attack back at Gerson. This back-and-forth repeats once more for each time Rude Buster has been successfully counter-deflected.

Successfully counter-deflecting

If Rude Buster is successfully deflected enough times, Gerson spins from the force of the attack, but takes no damage. 8 Mercy stars are obtained. The fight proceeds to the appropriate dialogue for that turn.

Failing to counter-deflect, first time

If Rude Buster is not counter-deflected the first time it's used, it strikes Susie, making her flinch back as she takes damage.

Susie Wh... what kind of cheap trick was that!? Gerson Sorry, thought you'd hit it back! Geh heh heh! Susie Oh yeah!? I'll show you!! Kris, let's use it again!!

6 Mercy stars are obtained. The fight proceeds to the appropriate dialogue for that turn.

Attempting to use items

Susie begins using the item, pulling a shining something from her pocket and tossing it up in front of herself. But Gerson disappears and reappears in front of her, plucking the shine out of the air before it can take effect.

Susie used the <ITEM>... but it was stolen!

The item has no effect and is not removed from the inventory. Gerson reappears in his usual spot, laughing.

Gerson Woah there! I ain't usin' items, so fair's fair, ain't it!?

The ITEM button becomes grayed out and unselectable for the rest of the encounter. The fight proceeds to the appropriate dialogue for that turn.

End of fight

The battle box for turn 21 disappears and the music fades out.

Susie ... Alright, I figured it out.

Susie crouches slightly, hands cupped around the air.

Susie You wanna see what I got, then...

An orb of green magic appears between her palms, thrumming as it's charged up.

Susie Take THIS!!!

Susie lets the healing blast loose — but at a slight angle, so that it passes over Gerson's head. He pauses as it does so, looking up at it.

Gerson Ha, ya missed.

But as he's distracted speaking, Susie hefts her axe and slices clean through his beard hairs, shocking Gerson. As the slash echoes for another few seconds, the screen fades to white and the encounter ends.
