Description

Light 'em up

Flavor text

Kris, Susie, and Ralsei all wear gangster outfits and point a finger gun at the enemies.

Hat variant

Always used during the encounters alongside Tenna, Shuttah, or 2 other Shadowguys.

Everyone gets ready to knock the enemies' hats off!

Socks variant

Everyone gets ready to knock the enemies' socks off!

The shooting sequence begins, accompanied by the following instructions:

Prior to v0.0.094
Aim with [Left][Right] and [Up][Down]! Fire with [MENU]!
v0.0.094 onwards
Press directions to aim and [MENU] to shoot!

A red crosshair appears on the screen, controlled by directions; pressing [MENU] has all party members shoot red heart-shaped bullets that pass through the crosshair. Shadowguys' hats or socks and other enemies' body parts appear brighter and can be hit by these bullets. At most 30 bullets can be shot (10 from each party member), and there is a 6-second time limit starting from when the first bullets are fired.

Each bullet that hits gives 10% Mercy to Shadowguys and 5% Mercy to other enemy types. If any enemy has 100% Mercy prior to this ACT's usage, they are automatically spared after being hit by enough bullets.
