Unleash

Description

Reveal weakness 60% TP

Flavor text

Kris's SOUL emitted a brilliant light!

The SOUL appears where Kris stands and glows a brilliant white, the light briefly flooding the screen. Instead of the next scripted attack, a blank 3Ã—3 "maze" is presented, where the SOUL can approach the jack-o'-lantern directly.

In the first use of this ACT, the jack-o'-lantern approaches the SOUL laughing, but its smile turns into a look of dismay upon grazing the SOUL, and it declares "YOUR TOO BRIGHT".

The jack-o'-lantern flees from the SOUL, looking frightened and exclaiming garbled variations of "MAMMA MIA" upon being grazed. Each time the jack-o'-lantern is grazed, Jackenstein gains 1% Mercy, up to a maximum of 35% Mercy for the first two uses and 30% Mercy for the third use.

On the third use, once 30% Mercy is gained for a total of 100% across all uses, the jack-o'-lantern freezes in the middle of the "maze". After a pause, it says "MAMMA MIA" extremely slowly, growing in size with the last word as the screen floods with white light, and the encounter ends.
