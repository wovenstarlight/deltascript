End of fight

Occurs when the Dance, Dance, and Dance ACTs are used in a single turn on each of the three members of Sweet Cap'n Cakes.

The following flavor text is an example; the exact labels differ depending on which ACT was used on whom.

You danced with Sweet! Susie danced with K_K! Ralsei danced with Cap'n!

The music stops.

Everyone is dancing!

The screen cuts to black. When I Get Happy I Dance Like This plays as everyone is pictured dancing together—Kris with Sweet, Susie with K_K, Ralsei with Cap'n, followed by all of them dancing together, first in a circle and then in a line, with scribbly cuts of Sweet Cap'n Cakes in between. Everyone strikes poses as the music ends, with text overhead reading "BATTLE WON!", several music notes falling from it like confetti.

The screen fades to black; then, with the encounter ended, it fades back in on everyone standing in Cyber Field.
