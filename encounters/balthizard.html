Before the fight, when escorting Gerson

Plays only while escorting Gerson to his study, during the dual-Balthizard encounter in the many-windowed hall.

The encounter begins with the party on the left, two Balthizards on the right, and the old man Gerson standing at the bottom-center of the space between them, facing the party.

Gerson Hm, what's all this now? Susie H-hey!! We're fighting!! Get out of here!! Gerson I see, I see. Well, you kids have fun!

The music pauses as Gerson walks offscreen to the right, the Balthizards watching him go. After he leaves, the music resumes and the encounter continues.

Flavor text

Press Left and Right rapidly to shake!

A 6-second timer appears. Pressing left/right causes Balthizard's head to shake from side to side, gaining up to 60% Mercy in one use. If the Mercy gathered while using this ACT adds up to a total of 100%, the ACT ends early. If Balthizard was already at 100% Mercy, it's automatically spared upon using this ACT.
