Select the right spelling!

A set of three attempts at spelling the same word appear; only one is correct. The cursor automatically cycles through which option it's selecting; [CONFIRM] must be pressed when the correctly-spelled word is selected, before the timer (around 3 seconds) runs out.

Word sets
  • GALLERY (with incorrect alternatives GALLURY and GALLORY)
  • ROOTS (with incorrect alternatives REETS and RAATS)
  • FESTIVAL (with incorrect alternatives FESTIAVL and FESTAVIL)
  • FLASHBACK (with incorrect alternatives FLESHBECK and FLOSHBOCK)
  • SWORD (with incorrect alternatives SORWD and SOWRD)
  • GUARDIAN (with incorrect alternatives GAURDIAN and GAURDAIN)
  • ATRIUM (with incorrect alternatives ATRUIM and ARTIUM)
  • SYNTHESIS (with incorrect alternatives SYNTEHSIS and SINTHESIS)
  • AMPHITHEATER (with incorrect alternatives AMPITHEATER and AMPITHEATRE)
