In other words, if all Swatchlings present in the encounter aren't the same color once all ACTs have been executed. If all Swatchlings ARE the same color, see the below subsection.

After coordinating colors

In other words, if all Swatchlings present in the encounter are the same color once all ACTs have been executed.

In the below dialogue, the speech bubbles appear for the Swatchlings in enemy slot 1, 2, and 3, respectively. If there is no enemy in that slot, or the enemy is not a Swatchling, the corresponding dialogue does not play.

Swatchling We feel so coordinated. Swatchling Thank you, young masters. Swatchling Thank you.

After being left alone

When all other enemies in the encounter are spared or defeated, whether or not they were Swatchlings.

Swatchling My outfit is coordinated now. With myself.

Swatchling becomes Tired.
