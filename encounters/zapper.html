Flavor text

First time

You turned up the volume! ...

A meter labelled VOLUME appears just above the party's battle menus, showing the volume being increased. The battle music gets louder.

The bullets increased in volume, too...! (But, they'll give more TP!)

Zapper's bullets are larger during the next turn. After the enemy's turn ends, the volume meter reappears briefly to show the volume lowering back to its original value, with the battle music quieting simultaneously.

Subsequent times

The bullets increased in volume! Try getting close to gather TP!

A meter labelled VOLUME appears just above the party's battle menus, showing the volume being increased. The battle music gets louder.

Zapper's bullets are larger during the next turn. After the enemy's turn ends, the volume meter reappears briefly to show the volume lowering back to its original value, with the battle music quieting simultaneously.

Dialogue

In the below dialogue, the speech bubbles appear for the Zappers in enemy slot 1, 2, and 3, respectively. If there is no enemy in that slot, or the enemy is not a Zapper, the corresponding dialogue does not play.

(As only a maximum of 2 Zappers ever appears in a single encounter, the third dialogue goes unused.)

During the Bonus Round fight with Tenna

After a pause, the screen fades back in to show the encounter, but with the Zapper gone and the party members missing from inside their gachapon capsules. After a pause, Tenna grins painfully wide.

Tenna H-huh!?

Clutching his head and twisting in distress, Tenna approaches the capsules.

Tenna Susie...? Kris...? Hey...? HEY!!!

Increasingly frantic, Tenna moves faster.

Tenna DON'T TELL ME YOU DIDN'T LIKE YOUR CUSTOM MADE TOY BALLS!? Tenna Hey, Mike?

As Tenna addresses Mike, he slows down, then goes entirely still with his next line.

Tenna Could you, uh, cancel my 4am shock therapy session?

He leans forward, whispering conspiratorially.

Tenna Cause it looks like it's happening right now.

As an alarm plays, red light flashing slowly from the top of the screen, Tenna clutches his head in a panic.

Tenna ALL HANDS ON DECK!!! EVERYONE FIND THE LIGHTNERS!!!

Tenna leaves offscreen to the right, and the encounter ends.
