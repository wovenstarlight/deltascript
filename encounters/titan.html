Flavor text and dialogue for the battle against the Titan.

This encounter has 8 phases that are referenced throughout this page. The phases begin when their corresponding condition is achieved.

  • Phase 1: initial
  • Phase 2: shield destroyed, first time
  • Phase 3: shield regenerated, first time
  • Phase 4: shield destroyed, second time
  • Phase 5: shield regenerated, second time
  • Phase 6: shield destroyed, third time
  • Phase 7: Titan at 50% HP or less
  • Phase 8: Gerson joins the party

If the Titan is not sufficiently damaged during the first 6 phases, Phase 6 will continue until Phase 7's condition is met. If the Titan is sufficiently damaged during any of the first 6 phases, the fight automatically skips ahead to Phase 7.

General dialogue

Phase 7

Dialogue for Phase 7 is entirely scripted; the Titan does not attack, only using its turn to heal while the party talks.

First turn

Immediately upon reaching 50% HP, the battle music fades. Instead of the Titan attacking, the screen flashes white, the Titan tinted slightly red. The screen flashes again; this time, a rumbling starts as the Titan's form distorts and shudders, its body and wings now steadily pulsing red. (Any party members downed are revived at a sixth of their maximum HP.) After a moment, Susie draws back slightly, startled.

{%- comment %} see oldman's candy above re: why it's a sixth of max HP {% endcomment %} Susie Did... Did we do it...?

The rumbling stops and the Titan grows still, the red pulsing ending. Scattering feathers, the Titan's many wings fold in around its body. Susie looks frightened.

Susie H-hey, wait! What is it... doing...?

The Titan absorbs the many Spawn that appear around it and heals for 15% of its maximum HP. Ralsei lowers his head.

Ralsei It's... regenerating.

Second turn

Susie looks frightened.

Susie Hey, Ralsei, how do we stop it!?

Ralsei bows his head.

Ralsei I... Ralsei I don't know...

The Titan absorbs Spawn to heal for another 15% HP.

Third turn

Ralsei bows his head.

Ralsei It seems... it seems we were only... Ralsei ... damaging its outer shell. Ralsei As long as the inside is intact... Ralsei This battle... will just keep... Susie ...

The Titan absorbs Spawn to heal for 50% HP, and its wings unfurl to reveal its body, shielded once more.

Ralsei I suppose... Ralsei We just have to... keep on fighting.

A long pause. And then a hammer flies in from offscreen left, slamming into the Titan's shield with a flash of light and leaving it deeply cracked as tiny shards go flying. All party members jump when it hits, with Susie and Ralsei looking shocked. A voice speaks from offscreen left.

Gerson What're y'all givin' up for?

The camera swings over to the left to reveal the Hammer of Justice standing with a hand on his hip and his hammer braced against the ground, with his theme fading in right on cue. The party members turn to face him and Susie and Ralsei look stunned.

Gerson You're not out of ideas yet, are ya? Susie Old... old man! I thought you were... Gerson I was lookin' for a change a' scenery... Gerson But this is a bit ridiculous.

Gerson lifts up his hammer.

Gerson C'mon, let's get this fellow!

Susie and Ralsei straighten up, once again ready for battle.

Susie ... yeah!
