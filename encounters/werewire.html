Missing when FIGHTing

To be specific, this plays only when:

  • all party members attempting to FIGHT in this turn have missed their attacks, and
  • when all enemies in the encounter are currently Tired, and
  • when all enemies in the encounter are at maximum HP, i.e. none of the enemies present have taken damage so far.
Try timing your attacks by pressing [CONFIRM]!

Dialogue

Werewire's dialogue is exclusively various three-letter combinations of the letters "B", "Z", "T", and "!", set on an angled speech bubble in spiky letters.

{% include acticon chara="Susie" -%} ThrowWire

Description

Toss Kris to free wire

Flavor text

Press [MENU] to throw, aim for the weakpoint!

All Werewires in the encounter come forward and weak points labelled with X marks oscillate up and down their wires. As Susie hefts Kris up with one hand, an arrow appears in front of her and oscillates up and down. From it extends a curved, dotted line showing Kris's projected flight path.

After pressing [MENU], Kris is thrown; if they graze the weakpoints, a slashing animation plays over the weakpoint. Otherwise, it is counted as a miss. In either case, they fly offscreen to the right before reentering from the left, retaking their place in the lineup.

Hitting the weakpoint(s)

The wire greatly loosened!

Any Werewires whose weakpoints were hit gain 50% Mercy.

Unused variant

Likely a leftover from the K. Round encounters's Throw ACT, which made a distinction between precise and imprecise hits.
