Before the fight

Kris, Susie, and Ralsei enter Jevil's room.

Jevil UEE HEE! VISITORS, VISITORS! NOW WE CAN PLAY, PLAY! Jevil THEN, AFTER YOU, I CAN PLAY WITH EVERYONE ELSE, TOO! So what are we playing, exactly...? Jevil OH, IT'S JUST A SIMPLE NUMBERS GAME.

Jevil laughs as a scythe sweeps past the party members, who narrowly dodge, Susie and Ralsei looking shocked.

Jevil WHEN YOUR HP DROPS TO 0, YOU LOSE! So that's the kinda game you wanna play, huh...? Then, I gotta warn you...

Kris, Susie, and Ralsei draw their weapons.

Jevil UEE HEE HEE! SHARK-TO-SHARK! I WOULDN'T HAVE IT ANY OTHER WAY! Jevil NOW, NOW!! LET THE GAMES BEGIN!!

The fight begins.

Pirouette

Description

Random Chaos 20% TP

Usage

Increases Jevil's Tiredness by 0.5.

Kris spun around! JEVIL got slightly more TIRED, and...!

Pirouette's effects rotate through the following depending on the turn. Using Pirouette on turn 10 has the same effect as on turn 1, turn 11 has the same effect as on turn 2, and so on.

Turn 1
What! It was just foley!

Accompanied by an explosion, car honk, or toilet flush SFX.

Turn 2
JEVIL felt at ease! JEVIL's defense dropped!

Jevil's defense lowers by 4, up to a maximum reduction of 16.

Turn 3
Awkward! Upcoming attack will hurt rapidly!

Invincibility frames are reduced by 60% for this turn.

Turn 4
Tranquil! The dance defended the party!

Jevil's attack is reduced by 30% for this turn.

Turn 5
What! It's nothing but a useless bird!

A bird flies across the screen.

Turn 6
It felt comforting!

Heals a random party member for 25–55 HP.

Turn 7
Everyone's HP got jumbled up!

The party members' HP bars are shuffled, so that each party member takes on the current/maximum HP of another party member. Negative HP is split among all party members and anyone who is downed is revived with 1 HP. (Party members get their original HP bars back at the end of this encounter.)

Turn 8
JEVIL's upcoming move got powered up!

Jevil's attack is boosted by 25% for this turn.

Turn 9
A perfect 10!

Heals all party members for 36–50 HP.

Unused
Something happened!
