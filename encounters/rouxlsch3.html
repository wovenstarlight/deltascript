I must defeast thou, so TV Guy recognizes my Glorye! But feare not. The four of us needst not Attacke. For what is a Showe... If thou dost not ACT?

Rouxls holds out the Rules Card, which gleams. The party's battle menus have all their buttons replaced by the ACT button.

The party members, Rouxls, and the Shadowguys line up at the center of the encounter; they dance for a few moments.

(Lancer used the LANCER Card! Now you can... LANCER?)

Phase 1 begins. From the next turn onwards, the option to LANCER is also available in each party member's battle menu, cycling through the row of ACT options.

The party members, Rouxls, and the Shadowguys return to their original positions.

Dialogue

Rouxls Lancer, don'st bother Lesser Daddy when he's harming your friends.

LANCERing(?)

Available from Phase 1 onwards.

Flavor text

Lancer enters the encounter from the left, taking a spot ahead of the party. Rouxls's allies similarly move forward towards the center of the encounter.

(Lancer recruits the enemies to your side!)

Depending on how many times you selected LANCER instead of ACT in the party's battle menus:

No LANCERs

A splat SFX plays as Lancer falls to his hands and knees. Rouxls's allies gain 20% Mercy each.

(But it wasn't too effective...)

One or two LANCERs

One or two spades (depending on the number of LANCERs) are drawn in towards Lancer, who flashes white and begins spinning, drawing back towards the party. Rouxls's allies are lured after him slightly and gain 35% Mercy (one LANCER) / 45% Mercy (two LANCERs) each.

(It's working!)

Three LANCERs

Three spades are drawn in towards Lancer, who flashes white and begins spinning, drawing back towards the party. Rouxls's allies are lured after him slightly and gain 60% Mercy each.

(It's highly effective!)

Lancer leaves the encounter to the left, and depending on how much Mercy Rouxls's allies now have:

If Rouxls's allies have less than 100% Mercy

The enemies retake their place in the lineup.

If Rouxls's allies have 100% Mercy

(The enemies joined you!)

The enemies move to the left side of the encounter, joining the party, and turn to face Rouxls.
