Usage

The Photo ACTs randomly choose from a pool of photo challenges upon each use. See the Deltarune Wiki for specifics. The challenges have been given their own sections on this page for easier readability.

Using a Photo ACT gives instructions regarding the desired photo; see the "Instructions" heading in the challenge-specific sections below.

After instructions are given, a Shuttah-themed camera frame appears in the center of the encounter and a timer counts down. This timer lasts 5 seconds for ToughPhoto, and 10 seconds for {% include acticon chara="Ralsei" -%} EasyPhoto / {% include acticon chara="Susie" -%} PowerPhoto; for the first use overall of a Photo ACT, however, the timer lasts 18 seconds regardless of which ACT was used.

While the timer is counting down, the following explanation is given regarding the camera controls:

Press directions to aim! Press [CONFIRM] to take the photo!

Taking a photo

Upon pressing [CONFIRM], the screen flashes white and the captured photo is briefly presented at the center of the screen. (If sufficiently centered on the target, small yellow text reading "Great shot!" will appear towards the bottom right.)

Mercy gained depends on whether the photo was successfully taken, and whether {% include acticon chara="Susie" -%} PowerPhoto has been used at least once in the encounter so far:

  • Successful photos: 80% Mercy normally; 100% with {% include acticon chara="Susie" -%} PowerPhoto
  • Failed photos: 25% Mercy normally; 50% with {% include acticon chara="Susie" -%} PowerPhoto

When a photo is taken, Shuttah reacts; see the "Reactions" heading in the challenge-specific sections below.

Running out of time

If no photo is taken within the time limit, no Mercy is gained, and Shuttah reacts as follows. ("[Z]" in the below message is typically replaced by its platform-specific "[CONFIRM]" equivalent; the default PC value has been used here to preserve the in-game spacing.)

UUMM!! Maybe actually press [CONFIRM] to take a photo next time?

Shuttah challenge

Instructions

Take a photo of Shuttah's Smile!

The party members and Shuttah disappear. 4 Shuttahs appear in a circle, only one of which has its real mouth. The circle rotates, and the individual Shuttahs flip around periodically. Only a photo taken of the real-mouthed Shuttah while it's facing forward counts as a success.

Reactions

Success

At random:

Shuttah Good, good, EYE approve!
Shuttah Confidence, baby, CONFIDENCE!

Failure

Shuttah That's okay, baby! Give it another SSSHOT!
