Start of first encounter

The encounter begins with the party on the left, as is typical, but they are encased in gacha capsules and all look serious. At the top of the screen is a counter starting at "00", styled to resemble digital clock numbers. Tenna enters the encounter from the bottom of the screen, pointing upwards.

Tenna We've got a very special battle for you today, folks! Tenna Very SPECIAL indeed, hahaha. Tenna All you have to do to win. Tenna Is say "I LOVE TV."

As Tenna speaks, the counter at the top changes to read "01" with a chime.

Tenna And, hahaha, don't think you'll be able to try anything funny. Tenna Because I've... got THIS.

Tenna holds out a piece of paper, still wearing his fixed grin.

Tenna Ever read this CONTRACT? Hmm, says you guys are my ACTORs... Tenna And that means... You've gotta ACT!

The paper flashes as all the buttons in Kris's battle menu change to ACT buttons. With a jump, Tenna takes his place on the right side of the encounter, once again pointing into the air.

General dialogue

Neutral

Unlike other enemies, whose neutral dialogue is replaced by any ACT-specific dialogue, Tenna's neutral dialogue is also appended to his ACT-specific dialogue. Therefore, this line always plays no matter what before the enemies attack.

Tenna IT'S! TV! TIME!

If a party member is downed

Tenna looks ferocious.

Tenna HEY! Fainting isn't part of the SCRIPT! Tenna Eat some TV Dinner and GET UP!!!

All party members are healed for 100 HP.

After being hit by {% include acticon chara="Susie" -%} {% include acticon chara="Ralsei" -%} Sharpshoot

Tenna hunches over, clutching his nose as if in pain; Shadowguy turns to look at him, smile faded.

Tenna H-Hey!!!

Tenna looks furious.

Tenna How could you let them use that act!?

Tenna points upwards, lifting his mic to his mouth and grinning again.

Tenna You're!!! Fired!!!

Shadowguy looks downcast and leaves the encounter.

Tenna Alright, no more of THAT guy!

The screen cuts to black with a radial wipe as the encounter ends (battle #2, battle #3).

After using {% include acticon chara="Susie" -%} {% include acticon chara="Ralsei" -%} OffButton

You hit the OFF button!

The screen turns off, music stopping. After a pause, the screen fades back in to show the encounter, but with the Zapper gone and the party members missing from inside their gachapon capsules. After a pause, Tenna grins painfully wide.

Tenna H-huh!?

Clutching his head and twisting in distress, Tenna approaches the capsules.

Tenna Susie...? Kris...? Hey...? HEY!!!

Increasingly frantic, Tenna moves faster.

Tenna DON'T TELL ME YOU DIDN'T LIKE YOUR CUSTOM MADE TOY BALLS!? Tenna Hey, Mike?

As Tenna addresses Mike, he slows down, then goes entirely still with his next line.

Tenna Could you, uh, cancel my 4am shock therapy session?

He leans forward, whispering conspiratorially.

Tenna Cause it looks like it's happening right now.

As an alarm plays, red light flashing slowly from the top of the screen, Tenna clutches his head in a panic.

Tenna ALL HANDS ON DECK!!! EVERYONE FIND THE LIGHTNERS!!!

Tenna leaves offscreen to the right, and the encounter (and the Bonus Round as a whole) ends.
