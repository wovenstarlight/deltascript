Before the fight

Kris, Susie, and Ralsei stop in the hallway leading from Floor 5F to the throne room.

Rouxls Enough, Worms...!

Rouxls Kaard teleports in front of them. His theme begins playing.

Thou may have CHEATETHED through mine PUZZLES, but... In COMBAT, thou shalt be scattered about! Come, knaves...! Prepareth for battle with...!

Rouxls moves aside and teleports in K. Round.

Whatever this is!!! Huh...!? It's the enemy from earlier...! But its CROWN looks different! ... it looks the same. Yes! With my Control Crowne... I mayst control ANY disc-shaped Lifeforme!

K. Round takes an aggressive stance.

Oh no!!! It looks like it's in pain!! IT LOOKS THE SAME!!!! Yes... The control crown is painful... But, I'm certain this Creature will feeleth relief... When it sees how much MORE pain THOU'LL be in!!!

The fight begins.

General flavor text

Initial encounter

Here it comes. Again.

Neutral

K.Round shuffles furiously.

Tired

K.Round looks weak.

Low HP

K.Round's shuffle becomes lethargic.

After {% include acticon chara="Susie" -%} {% include acticon chara="Ralsei" -%} Susie's Idea / {% include acticon chara="Susie" -%} {% include acticon chara="Ralsei" -%} Throw

The crown is <NUMBER>-percent loose!

Healing itself

Triggers when K. Round falls to or below 600 HP. Replaces K. Round's attack for that turn.

K. ROUND practiced self-care!

A carton of milk appears, moving towards K. Round and healing it for 700 HP, as well as increasing its attack by 0.5 up to a maximum total attack of 10.

General dialogue

No dialogue; K. Round does not talk.

Check

Available if K. Round was not Checked in its first encounter. Otherwise, is replaced by the Checkers ACT.

K.ROUND - AT 9 DF 3 It's being controlled into attacking...! (Though, wouldn't it just attack anyway...?)

Checkers

Available if K. Round was Checked in its first encounter. Otherwise, is replaced by the Check ACT.

K.ROUND - AT 9 DF 3 Watch out for its Flying King attack! (Also, you need to get the CROWN off of its head.)

Bow

Standard

Kris bows.

You bowed to K. ROUND.

K. Round repeatedly bows back. Its crown loosens by 18%.

It bowed back. Its crown loosened a little.

On the first three uses, proceeds to one of the below sections.

First time

Follows the standard flavor text.

Huh!? That hardly did anything! How can we push off that CROWN...? ... Hmm.

Second time

Follows the standard flavor text.

It's still hardly working! Whatever can we do, Kris...? ... Hey.

Third time

Follows the standard flavor text.

Sometimes persistence is key, Kris!! It'll be hard, but we can do it!! HEY YOU GUYS!!!

{% include acticon chara="Susie" -%} {% include acticon chara="Ralsei" -%} Susie's Idea / {% include acticon chara="Susie" -%} {% include acticon chara="Ralsei" -%} Throw

Initially appears as "Susie's Idea", and changes to "Throw" after the first use.

First time only

Susie? You want to ACT? Aww, what's your idea? Umm... well... how do I say this. I kind of... Need you for this one. That's fine, Susie! I'll help! You want to apologize to it for earlier, right? Nah, I just need you to stay still.

Susie grabs Ralsei in one hand and hefts him.

Kris! We gotta get that CROWN off its head! Help me throw Ralsei at it!

Continues to the usage instructions.

Instructions

An arrow appears in front of Susie, oscillating up and down.

Press [MENU] to determine the ANGLE!

After selecting the angle, the arrow turns red and a curved, dotted line stretches forward from it, oscillating back and forth.

Press [MENU] to determine the POWER!

After selecting the power, Ralsei is thrown, either hitting the crown/missing and flying offscreen to the right, or hitting K. Round and bouncing off, falling offscreen. He reenters from the left, retaking his place in the lineup.

Hitting the crown

The crown greatly loosened!

K. Round's crown loosens by 35%.

Hitting K. Round

The crown loosened a little!

K. Round's crown loosens by 20%.

Missing

Missed!

Nothing happens.

After the fight

Once the crown is 100% loose, it slides off K. Round and falls to the ground, sliding offscreen. K. Round transforms back into C. Round and rolls offscreen, ending the encounter. Rouxls slides back into view from the right.

... ...

Rouxls's theme begins playing again.

Why, brilliant! Just BRILLIANT! I knew you couldst do it! Thou great heroes, thou! Thou see-eth, that was merely... A friendly TEST of thine abilities! But thou hast WON! And now, NOTHING blocks thine path! I, personally, could NOT be less of an obstruction!

The music cuts off. A pause.

But you - Luck be with you. Heroes!

Rouxls cuts off Ralsei; as he speaks, he exits rapidly towards the right. The party is able to proceed to the throne room, from where they can access the roof.
