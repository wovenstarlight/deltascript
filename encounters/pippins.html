{% include acticon chara="Susie" -%} Cheat

Description

TIRE Enemies, but...

Flavor text

First time

Susie drew 4 dots on all the dice! The enemies got TIRED!

All enemies become Tired. Any Pippinses present look dismayed.

Susie Who's the highroller now, idiot!?

Subsequent times

On the second use of this ACT, either the Kris or Ralsei variant is randomly chosen to play. On the third use, if the Kris variant was previously chosen, then the Ralsei variant plays; otherwise, and on subsequent uses, either variant is randomly chosen.

Ralsei variant
Susie got bored of cheating and drew a mustache on Ralsei!

A dark gray mustache appears over Ralsei's sprite and remains there until the encounter ends.

Kris variant
Susie got bored of cheating and drew dead eyes on Kris!

Red crosses appear over Kris's eyes and remain there until the encounter ends.

Dialogue - First time only

In the below dialogue, the speech bubbles appear for the Pippinses in enemy slot 1, 2, and 3, respectively. If there is no enemy in that slot, or the enemy is not a Pippins, the corresponding dialogue does not play.

Pippins (You do know that makes my attack stronger...) Pippins (This girl... she boggles the mind.) Pippins (What have you done, you yatzy!?)
