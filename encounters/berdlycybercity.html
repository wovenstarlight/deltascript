General flavor text

Initial encounter

Berdly blocks the way!

Neutral

Berdly thanks Noelle profusely.
Berdly tries to entice Noelle to his side by gyrating his hips!
Berdly preens condescendingly.
Berdly laughs and goes to his mind palace.
Berdly crushes his Smart Scouter out of frustration.
Smells like frozen chicken.

General dialogue

With specific turns

Turn 1

Berdly Kris! I get it! Your head's gotten big from solving that puzzle by DUMB LUCK!

Turn 2

Berdly So now you think it's your chance to usurp me at my weakest... Berdly By taking my most precious thing... Noelle!

Turn 3

Berdly But guess what, Kris! I have one or more things you will NEVER have! Berdly Behold!!! Comrades!!!

2 Werewires join the encounter.

Turn 4

Berdly That's right, Kris! Unlike YOU, I have a LOVING TEAM behind me! Berdly And you'll never beat US fighting alone!
If both Werewires have been defeated

By using Noelle's SleepMist.

A pause.

Berdly ... h-hey, wait, where'd the other guys go!?

Turn 5

If one or both Werewires remain
Berdly Kris... you're outmatched! My team is rich in natural resources! Berdly Intelligence, yes. Smarts, yes. Nipples, future YES.
If both Werewires have been defeated
Berdly My... my allies are gone? Ha! They're probably just... Helping Queen search for me! Berdly Sh... shut up, Kris! Shut up! I don't care if you're not saying anything!

Turn 6

If one or both Werewires remain
Berdly Noelle, this might hurt, but it's to save you!
If Noelle has not been downed
Noelle (Help.)
If both Werewires have been defeated
Berdly S-summon more guys!? I... I don't need to do that! Berdly Just WAIT! The original ones are gonna come back any minute now!

Turn 7 or later, if Noelle has taken damage

Noelle's dialogue plays only if she's not downed.

Noelle WHY ARE YOU HITTING ME!? Berdly Don't worry, it's part of my calculations!

With specific attacks

These dialogues play from turn 7 onwards. See the Deltarune Wiki for descriptions of each attack.

Berdly Partake, Kris! The Zephyr of Punishment!

Spear Blast

Berdly Holy Halbird! Grant me strength!

Chirashi

Berdly See if you can dodge this, Kris!

Check

BERDLY - He usually only gets this mad when you play games together.

Play Dumb

First time

You acted like you were an idiot next to Berdly's genius! Ahh, Kris... A shame I must defeat someone so lowly...

Berdly gains 8% Mercy.

Subsequent times

At random:

You did several math problems incorrectly!
You pretended to knit a home-made dunce cap!
You grunted like a primitive animal!

The following flavor text plays only once and does not repeat.

You pretended to spill your IQ points on the floor! You got Noelle to pretend to pick them up! (Why me!?)
Berdly liked that!

Berdly gains 8% Mercy.
