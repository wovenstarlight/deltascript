Challenge info

Listed as "Jigsaw Joe", with the reward being "Joe's Life Savings".

Learn how to spare like a champ.

Before the fight

Jigsaw Joe Boss! Jigsaw Joe You can ACT and SPARE on the same turn! Jigsaw Joe If you know Kris's ACT will give an enemy 100% mercy... Jigsaw Joe SPARE them on the same turn with Susie or Ralsei!

The encounter begins in phase 1.

General flavor text

Initial encounter

Jigsaw Joe jigs in!

Phase 1

Jigsaw Joe looks vulnerable to ACTING and SPARING on the same turn.

Phase 2

Jigsaw Joe looks like he could use a bedtime story.

General dialogue

Phase 1

Plays unless some ACT-specific dialogue overrides this.

Jigsaw Joe Boss! You gotta ACT and SPARE on the same turn!

Phase 2

Plays unless some ACT-specific dialogue overrides this.

At 16% TP or higher

Jigsaw Joe Boss! You're putting me to SLEEP! I mean, you AREN'T! Make me TIRED and PACIFY me, boss!

At less than 16% TP

Jigsaw Joe Boss! If you don't have the TP to use PACIFY, DEFENDing will give you TP!

Fallback

Unused.

Jigsaw Joe Boss!!

Check

Flavor text

JIGSAW JOE - Beat him up to earn his life savings!

Dialogue

Jigsaw Joe looks frantic.

Jigsaw Joe Boss! Don't listen to that! That's the wrong ACT!! The wrong ACT!!

Shave

Available during phase 1.

Flavor text

Kris lifts their sword.

(You shaved Jigsaw Joe's stubble!)

They swing, and Jigsaw Joe's stubble is shaved clean off; he gains 100% Mercy.

If neither Susie nor Ralsei spared Jigsaw Joe on the same turn

Jigsaw Joe's stubble grows back in and he loses 100% Mercy.

Jigsaw Joe's stubble regrew!

If either Susie or Ralsei spared Jigsaw Joe on the same turn

<Character> spared Jigsaw Joe!

Dialogue

If neither Susie nor Ralsei spared Jigsaw Joe on the same turn

Jigsaw Joe looks frantic. <#> is replaced by the number of times Shave has been used without a simultaneous spare, from 1 up to a maximum of 99.

Jigsaw Joe It's my <#> o'clock shadow!

If either Susie or Ralsei spared Jigsaw Joe on the same turn

Jigsaw Joe Ohhhh, boss, you're sparing me!?

Jigsaw Joe is spared. A pause. Then he reenters the encounter with the spare animation reversed and looks frantic.

Jigsaw Joe WAIT A SECOND!!! I got something else to remind you of!

Jigsaw Joe looks calmer.

Jigsaw Joe Boss! Jigsaw Joe It works the same with making an enemy TIRED! Jigsaw Joe If an ACT will make an enemy TIRED... Jigsaw Joe Use Ralsei's PACIFY right away!
At less than 16% TP only
Jigsaw Joe Of course, PACIFY is MAGIC, so you'll need TP. Jigsaw Joe Spend a turn DEFENDING to gain TP if you have to, boss!

This ACT is replaced by {% include acticon chara="Susie" -%} SleepyStory as phase 2 begins.

{% include acticon chara="Susie" -%} SleepyStory

Available during phase 2.

Flavor text

Susie told the enemy a bedtime story about beating them up! * The enemy became TIRED!

Jigsaw Joe becomes Tired and moves at half speed.

If Ralsei didn't pacify Jigsaw Joe on the same turn

Jigsaw Joe stops being Tired and moves at normal speed.

Jigsaw Joe drank coffee! Jigsaw Joe stopped being TIRED!

If Ralsei pacified Jigsaw Joe on the same turn

Ralsei cast PACIFY!

Dialogue, if Jigsaw Joe was pacified on the same turn

(If not pacified on the same turn, standard phase 2 dialogue is used.)

Jigsaw Joe You did it, Boss! If you can manage to SPARE enemies right away... Jigsaw Joe Battles will go a lot faster! That's all!

Jigsaw Joe is spared and the encounter ends.

S-Action

Unavailable even after returning from Cyber World, though fallback flavor text is present in the code.

Susie acted!

Nothing happens.

R-Action

Unavailable even after returning from Cyber World, though fallback flavor text is present in the code.

Ralsei acted!

Nothing happens.

After the challenge ends

If you won

First time

If you won by sparing/pacifying
Jigsaw Joe An amazing battle! Jigsaw Joe As promised, here is your reward!
If you won by fighting
Jigsaw Joe Hey! That's not how you SPARE someone! Jigsaw Joe ... but I guess a win is a win.
You received Joe's Life Savings ($1)!

$1 is added to your total money.

Subsequent times

Plays regardless of whether you spared or fought.

Jigsaw Joe An amazing battle! Jigsaw Joe ...I don't have any more to give. You've already taken everything I have.

If you lost

Unused, as Jigsaw Joe never attacks you, so there's no way to lose this challenge.

Jigsaw Joe You beefed it! Better luck next time, boss!
