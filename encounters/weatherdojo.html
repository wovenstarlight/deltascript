Flavor text

First time

You looked at the sky and took... A telescope!

Lanino and Elnina each gain 8% Mercy. During the following attack phase, the SOUL has a small telescope above it; if bullets collide with this, Lanino and Elnina gain additional Mercy, the rate of which is higher if the forecasted attack for this turn was moons. (Any additionally gained Mercy will be reset if the SOUL is hit by a red flame.)

The Mercy gained from this ACT can result in a total of at most 90% Mercy for each partner.
