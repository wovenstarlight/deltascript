General flavor text

With specific attacks

The attack rotation and therefore the accompanying flavor text is largely scripted. Unless overridden by flavor text for downed party members, the below lines play out sequentially. (See the Deltarune Wiki for descriptions of each attack.)

Initial encounter (Phase 1, Star Storm)

The Roaring Knight appeared.
Unused attack-specific variant

Hint towards the type of attack, similar to other turns. Gets overridden by the initial encounter text.

You felt lightheaded. You saw silver stars...

Phase 1, Tracking Swords

You felt something hovering close behind your head...

Phase 1, Box Splitter

Suddenly, the north wind blew fiercely.

Phase 1, Sword Tunnel

Your vision narrows.

Phase 1, Rotating Slash

Your chest feels tight.

Phase 2, Star Storm

You felt lightheaded. You saw golden stars...

Phase 2, Box Splitter

Suddenly, the north and east winds blew fiercely.

Phase 2, Sword Tunnel

Your vision narrows. ... Your head is spinning.

Phase 2, Tracking Swords

You feel surrounded.

Phase 2, Rotating Slash

You felt your chest twisting.

Phase 3, Star Storm

You felt lightheaded. You felt a migraine coming on...

Phase 3, Box Splitter

Suddenly, a tempest.

Phase 3, Tracking Swords

Your vision narrows. ... The world revolves around you.

Phase 3, Sword Tunnel

You feel cornered.

Phase 3/4, Rotating Slash

This attack occurs on turn 15 as a part of Phase 3. If Phase 4 is entered prior to turn 15, then this attack occurs as the first turn of Phase 4.

Your heartbeat becomes twisted.

Phase 4, Charge-up

The Knight does not attack on its turn, instead charging up.

If Susie is not down
Susie grew pale.
If Susie is down
Susie struggled to give some kind of warning.

Phase 4, Roaring

The Knight's hands glow a strange color...

Phase 4, after Roaring

If any hits were taken during the entire fight
The enemy suddenly let down its guard!
If no hits were taken during the entire fight
Kris coughed. The enemy slowly tilted its head...

Party member downed

The below messages override the attack-specific flavor text above. If more than one party member is downed/swooned, all applicable flavor text will be presented as a single flavor text box in the usual order of Kris → Susie → Ralsei.

Kris downed

Kris kneeled in silence.

Susie swooned

Susie was hurt and beaten.

Ralsei swooned

Ralsei became a pile of fluff.

Dialogue

From turn 6, Susie begins speaking before the Knight attacks; dialogue progresses with every additional turn where Susie is not downed. Her lines are listed here under the first turn number they can be played during.

Turn 6

Susie Heheh... Susie Didn't... think we'd still be standing, did you?

Turn 7

Susie Thing is, you actually... Susie You actually messed up, picking a fight with US!

Turn 8

Susie You? You're all damn alone... Susie Me? I got... Kris and Ralsei behind me.

Turn 9

If either Kris or Ralsei is not downed

Susie Even... even if you knock me down... Susie As long as Kris, Ralsei, are here...

If Kris and Ralsei are both downed

Susie Even... even if you knock them down... Susie As long as I'm here...

Turn 10

If either Kris or Ralsei is not downed

Susie As long as Kris has got a hand to lift me up with...

If Kris and Ralsei are both downed

Susie As long as I'm here to lift them back up...
Susie Heh... you're never gonna win, you hear me?!

Turn 11

Susie So... give up.

Turn 12

Susie You know you can't win... so... give up!

Turn 13

Susie ... You won't even... Susie ... say a thing, huh...

Turn 14

Susie ... heh... heheheh... Susie Man, I'm done talking. Susie ... people like you... just piss me off.
