Queen Finally... a worthy opponent...

Hints

For when you haven't completed certain actions during the previous turn. Overrides all above flavor text. If you didn't do multiple things last turn (e.g. you didn't dodge OR punch), then the first hint in this subsection takes precedence (e.g. the dodging hint takes priority over the punching hint).

Dodging

(Dodge Queen's attacks with [Left], [Right], and [Down]!)

Punching

(Punch during Queen's turn with [CONFIRM] or [CANCEL] after she attacks!)

Counter-punching

(Press [CONFIRM] or [CANCEL] to hit Queen after she attacks!)

Losing the fight

The battle music halts. GIGA Queen repeatedly ducks at the center of the screen with a squeaking SFX, then laughs. Black slides in from both sides like closing doors, coming together with a thud. On the now-black screen, the image of the Thrash Machine sitting defeatedly on a bench appears, with text above reading "Don't give up...!". Options below read "FIGHT AGAIN" and "RETIRE", an arrow indicating the currently-selected option.

Choosing "FIGHT AGAIN" results in the screen fading to white; as the battle music starts up again, the screen fades back in on the start of the encounter.

Choosing "RETIRE" results in the screen fading to white, then fading back in on the party standing at the start of the walkway immediately before encountering Queen and Noelle.

End of fight

When the Thrash Machine punches at the giant baseball, its fist lands with a squeak. The Machine shakes once, then twice, and the spinning baseball slows to a halt. Then it's launched back up, flying over the head of a shocked GIGA Queen, and as it falls towards her, she hoots in surprise and the screen fades to white.

There's an explosion unseen. A rattling begins from malfunctioning machinery, revealed to be GIGA Queen's red-tinted form as the screen fades back into the encounter. She still has her hands on her cheeks, looking horrified.

Queen Battle Power... Dropping... Queen Overheat... Activated Queen Listen Well Queen You May Have Defeated Me Queen But I Still Possess One Thing Queen You Three Will Never Have

GIGA Queen's tremors, the rattling, the smoke, all comes to an abrupt halt. She jumps up, grinning viciously, one fist held out in front of her. As she speaks, puffs of smoke are emitted from behind her outstretched hand's glove hem.

Queen Detachable Hands

Her entire fist detaches from her arm and launches downwards at the Thrash Machine, the top half of which is blown clear off its legs and sent flying with the force of GIGA Queen's rocket-powered fist. The screen fades to white as the encounter ends.
