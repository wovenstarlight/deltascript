General flavor text

Initial encounter

Mauswheel spins into you!

Neutral

Mauswheel is rolling around with an annoying wheel sound.
Mauswheel might make a good wreath, as a prank.
It's a cacophony of clicks.
Smells like a three-cheese pizza.

Tired

Unused; Mauswheel does not get Tired at low HP, and no ACTs make it Tired.

Mauswheel's DPI is lowering.

Spareable

Mauswheel is just a spareable husk now.

General dialogue

Neutral

Mauswheel (I'm no cheater! It's just cheese...)
Mauswheel (We are grippable. We are many.)
Mauswheel (Rolling around at the speed of mouse...)
Mauswheel (Wired is FASTER!)

Spareable

Mauswheel (Well, everyone's captured.) (What does that make me?)

Check

MAUSWHEEL - Two mice are better than one! ... Three, maybe not. The cursor will follow you everywhere. Keep moving!

Catch / {% include acticon chara="Susie" -%} {% include acticon chara="Ralsei" -%} CatchX

Instructions

Press [MENU] To Trap!!!

50 tiny Maice move back and forth at the center of the screen. A cage (sized larger when using {% include acticon chara="Susie" -%} {% include acticon chara="Ralsei" -%} CatchX) at the top of the screen also goes back and forth but at a different speed. [MENU] must be pressed to drop the cage at the right time, catching the little Maice.

If you caught any mini-Maice

Mauswheel gains 2% Mercy for each caught mini-Maus.

If Mauswheel does not reach 100% Mercy from this ACT usage

You caught them!

If Mauswheel reaches 100% Mercy from this ACT usage

The encounter ends automatically.

If you caught no mini-Maice

You missed!

Nothing happens.

N-Action

Noelle screamed quietly!

End of fight

Automatically plays when Mauswheel reaches 100% Mercy through a usage of Catch or {% include acticon chara="Susie" -%} {% include acticon chara="Ralsei" -%} CatchX.

If Tasque Manager was recruited

Mauswheel begins spinning faster and faster.

Mauswheel reached 100% mercy! But, it's still rolling out of control... Suddenly...!

Tasque Manager and an accompanying Tasque appear in front of the party, facing Mauswheel.

Tasque Manager We'll take it from here!

Mauswheel is recruited and disappears. Tasque Manager and the Tasque exit off to the right of the screen, and the encounter ends.

If Tasque Manager was not recruited

Mauswheel ran out of maice!

Mauswheel is recruited and disappears. The encounter ends.
