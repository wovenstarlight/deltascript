Available after using BegForMercy at least once across all Moonwarmer encounters. Unchanged from the Watercooler's version of this ACT, despite the now-inapplicable flavor text.

Flavor text

The party, wearing their gangster outfits and holding cups of water, stand around the Moonwarmer.

Everyone chatted around the watercooler. The watercooler felt included!

The Moonwarmer gains 25% Mercy.

Flirt

Available after using BegForMercy at least once across all Moonwarmer encounters.

Flavor text

At random:

You told the moonwarmer it should actually be called a moonwarmest...?
You took a slow sip of warms and asked if it ever mooned...?
You asked the moonwarmer,"hotter"they doing later...?

The Moonwarmer gains 10% Mercy if either Susie or Ralsei is up, and 14% Mercy if both Susie and Ralsei are downed.

The water turns pink for a second.

The Moonwarmer's water(?) briefly flashes pink; if it reaches 100% Mercy from this use of Flirt, the water(?) remains pink.
