Usage

The Photo ACTs cycle through several photo challenges in a fixed pattern. See the Deltarune Wiki for specifics. The challenges have been given their own sections on this page for easier readability.

Using a Photo ACT gives instructions regarding the desired photo; see the "Instructions" heading in the challenge-specific sections below.

After instructions are given, a Shuttah-themed camera frame appears in the center of the encounter and a timer counts down. This timer lasts 5 seconds for ToughPhoto, and 10 seconds for {% include acticon chara="Ralsei" -%} EasyPhoto / {% include acticon chara="Susie" -%} PowerPhoto; for the first use overall of a Photo ACT, however, the timer lasts 18 seconds regardless of which ACT was used.

While the timer is counting down, the following explanation is given regarding the camera controls:

Press directions to aim! Press [CONFIRM] to take the photo!

Taking a photo

Upon pressing [CONFIRM], the screen flashes white and the captured photo is briefly presented at the center of the screen. (If sufficiently centered on the target, small yellow text reading "Great shot!" will appear towards the bottom right.)

Mercy gained depends on whether the photo was successfully taken, and whether {% include acticon chara="Susie" -%} PowerPhoto has been used at least once in the encounter so far:

  • Successful photos: 10% Mercy normally; 20% Mercy with {% include acticon chara="Susie" -%} PowerPhoto
  • Failed photos: 6% Mercy normally; 12% Mercy with {% include acticon chara="Susie" -%} PowerPhoto
  • The group photo is an exception to the above, granting Mercy depending on how many party members are in the frame (with a generous margin):
    • 3 of 3 heroes in frame: 20% Mercy
    • 2 of 3 heroes in frame: 12% Mercy normally; 24% Mercy with {% include acticon chara="Susie" -%} PowerPhoto
    • 1 of 3 heroes in frame: 8% Mercy normally; 16% Mercy with {% include acticon chara="Susie" -%} PowerPhoto

When a photo is taken, Tenna reacts; see the "Reactions" heading in the challenge-specific sections below.

Shuttah then delivers dialogue depending on whether the photo was successful or not; see the "Dialogue" heading in the challenge-specific sections below.

Running out of time

If no photo is taken within the time limit, no Mercy is gained, and Tenna reacts as follows. ("[Z]" in the below message is typically replaced by its platform-specific "[CONFIRM]" equivalent; the default PC value has been used here to preserve the in-game spacing.)

UUMM!! Maybe actually press [Z] to take a photo next time?

Shuttah then delivers generic dialogue before its next attack.

Shuttah challenge

Appears twice, in easy and hard variants. The easy variant has 3 false Shuttahs and rotates slower, while the hard variant has 5 false Shuttahs and rotates faster.

Instructions

Take a photo of Shuttah's Smile!

The party members and Shuttah disappear. Various Shuttahs appear in a circle, only one of which has its real mouth. The circle rotates, and the individual Shuttahs flip around periodically. Only a photo taken of the real-mouthed Shuttah while it's facing forward counts as a success.

Reactions

Success

A PERFECT SHOT!! DON'T KNOW WHO WANTS IT, BUT STILL!

Failure

Wrong! No! I need a picture of Shutta's SMILE!!

Dialogue

Success - Easy variant

Shuttah Ooo la la. Was my smile always so beautiful? Shuttah The camera added 10 pounds of pure happiness to my teeth...

Success - Hard variant

Shuttah Ohhh, so beautiful! I save the picture, I save the picture. Shuttah It's my new background, it's my new foreground.

Failure

Shuttah Oh, a miserable picture. This is a mistake. Shuttah Let us agree to delete this picture. Forever.
