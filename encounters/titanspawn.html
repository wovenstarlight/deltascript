{% include acticon chara="Susie" -%} {% include acticon chara="Ralsei" -%} DualHeal

Description

Heal party 16% TP

Flavor text

Your SOUL shined its power on RALSEI and SUSIE!

The SOUL appears and the party members glow white for a brief moment, powering up; Susie and Ralsei both prepare their healing magic.

First four times

Ralsei and Susie cast DUAL HEAL! Effectiveness of DUAL HEAL has lowered!

Fifth time onwards

Ralsei and Susie cast DUAL HEAL!

All party members are healed for a varying amount of HP. The amount healed is the combination of Susie and Ralsei's magic stats, with a multiplier that depends on the number of times this ACT has been used:

  • First use: 9×
  • Second use: 6×
  • Third use: 4.8×
  • Fourth use: 1.8×
  • Fifth use onwards: 1.2×

Banish

Description

Defeat enemy 64% TP

Flavor text

Kris's SOUL emitted a brilliant light!

The SOUL appears where Kris stands and glows a brilliant white, the light briefly flooding the screen. When the light clears, any Titan Spawn present are gone, having been purified.

R-Action: ReviveKris

Description

Revive Kris 16% TP

Flavor text

Ralsei used Reviver!

A spotlight shines over Kris, and a small winged Ralsei flutters down to pat them rapidly on the head. If they were downed, Kris is revived with 30% HP (rounded up). If they were not downed, Kris is healed for 50% of their maximum HP (rounded up).
