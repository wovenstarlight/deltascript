General flavor text

Initial encounter

Berdly rides in!

Standard

Berdly calls Queen for help! But Queen is pretending to be on the phone! Berdly asks Queen what snacks they have at home! Queen nods! She has earbuds in! Berdly gives the secret sign! But Queen is busy with a coloring book! Berdly summons Queen! Queen calls in sick! Berdly shows Queen his card collection! Queen escaped successfully. Smells like fried chicken.

Low HP

Berdly asks Queen for healing! She says "You're Doing Great Honey"

Above 75% Mercy

Berdly's coaster car thing looks sick.

General dialogue

With specific turns

Turn 1

Berdly Kris, I get why you're getting in my way! You're jealous, aren't you!?

Turn 2

Berdly Kris, you're tired of being the class No.3, gazing distantly at Noelle and I's fruited figures...

Turn 3

Berdly So rather than ever STUDYING, you'd enlist SUSIE to take us down!

Turn 4

Berdly Kris... you are truly depraved. Playing in the mud with the class hellion.

Turn 5

Berdly But Kris, I am a kind and brave hero! If you come to our side... Berdly You may be a slightly less distant No. 3! Haha... slightly.

With specific attacks

These dialogues play only the first time each attack is used from turn 6 onwards. See the Deltarune Wiki for descriptions of each attack.

Tornado

Berdly Partake, Kris! The zephyr of punishment!

Spear Blast

Berdly Holy Halbird! Grant me strength!

Chirashi

Berdly Look, Susie! The closest you'll ever get to an "A"!

Low HP or above 50% Mercy, turn 6 or later

Berdly What? My car is breaking!? Berdly Fools! This is Smart Smoke! It means my car is getting stronger!

May continue into the dialogue below, if one of the Bump ACTs was previously used.

If previously bumped, turn 6 or later

May follow the dialogue above, if conditions for both are met.

Berdly Ha! Go ahead, "bump" me, you bumpbarians! Berdly I'll show you "bumper cars" is a game of INTELLECT, not skill!

Spareable

Berdly's coaster car jolts and Berdly looks stunned.

Berdly W-what...? My coaster, it's...!

The encounter automatically ends.
