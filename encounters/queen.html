{% include acticon chara="Susie" -%} Throw

Available only when Queen's status is "Defenseless" i.e. she has no acid shield.

Flavor text

Instructions

Press [MENU] to throw, aim for the weakpoint!

Berdly comes forward and weak points labelled with X marks oscillate up and down his wires. As Susie hefts Kris up with one hand, an arrow appears in front of her and oscillates up and down. From it extends a curved, dotted line showing Kris's projected flight path.

After pressing [MENU], Kris is thrown; if they graze the weakpoints, a slashing animation plays over the weakpoint. Otherwise, it is counted as a miss. In either case, they fly offscreen to the right before reentering from the left, retaking their place in the lineup.

Hitting the weakpoint(s)

The wire was cut!

Berdly gains a variable amount of Mercy depending on the fight phase:

  • Phase 1: 12.5% Mercy per target hit
  • Phase 2: Around 8.34% Mercy per target hit
  • Phase 3: 6.25% Mercy per target hit
  • Phase 4: 5% Mercy per target hit

Missed!

Nothing happens.

Subsequent times, shortened version

Overrides the standard flavor text when used in the same turn as other shortened ACTs. Displayed in a single dialogue box with the other ACTs' flavor text, in the order Kris → Susie → Ralsei.

You toasted Queen!

Queen's first acid shield (raised at the start of phase 2) loses 16.67% HP if either Susie or Ralsei are up, or 41.67% HP if both Susie and Ralsei are downed. The second and third shields (raised at the start of phase 3 and 4 respectively) lose 12.5% HP if either Susie or Ralsei are up, or 31.25% HP if both Susie and Ralsei are downed.

Queen sips from her acid!

Queen drinks from her glass of acid, with bars on the shield going dark to correspond with the HP loss. If all bars are gone (the shield is at/below 0% HP), then it disappears into a low-resolution explosion effect, sending Queen's chair briefly spinning away. If on the normal route, Queen lets out a hoot of laughter with her cheeks flushed as her chair spins.
