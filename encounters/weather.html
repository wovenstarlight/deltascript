--- title: Lanino and Elnina encounter description: >

Flavor text and dialogue for the Legend of Tenna - Desert Board battle against Lanino and Elnina.

This fight is largely scripted. For simplicity, this page uses "Turn 3" as the beginning of the primary confrontation, but said turn actually triggers under two conditions:

  • Turn 3 at the earliest, after I Love TV has been said at least once to either partner
  • Turn 7, if I Love TV has never been said to either partner

Headings on this page are named assuming "Turn 3" actually occurs on the true third turn, with subsequent turns numbered accordingly.

Before the fight

First attempt

The encounter begins with the party on the left and Lanino and Elnina on the right, as is typical. At the top of the screen is a counter starting at "00", styled to resemble digital clock numbers. Tenna enters the encounter from the bottom of the screen.

Tenna This battle has a SPECIAL win condition for our contestants!

Prior to v0.0.094

Tenna All you have to do say "I love to watch TV" 99 times and you will win!

v0.0.094 onwards

Tenna All you have to do is say "I love to watch TV" 99 times and you will win!

As Tenna mentions the required count, Ralsei reels with shock. Tenna heads offscreen towards the bottom.

Ralsei Ninety... Ni-

The encounter begins.
