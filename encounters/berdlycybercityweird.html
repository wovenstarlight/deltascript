Fifth time

Noelle cast SNOWGRAVE!

As with her other spells, Noelle lifts her hands up, sending up a burst of magic, but then she goes still. The music fades out.

Noelle's body briefly flashes white and her eyes open, now solid white. Across from her, Berdly stops moving as well, watching her with wide eyes. Noelle floats to the center of the screen as magical sparks continuously leave her hands and rise up above with the eerie chiming of bells.

The screen is overlaid with blue as snow rages past, with a twisting column of ice and snow flooding over Berdly. When the column of snow passes, it leaves behind a solid block of ice encasing Berdly and dealing immense damage.

The snowstorm fades and Noelle drops to the ground, falling to her knees. Her hands are together - perhaps clasped, perhaps touching the Thorn Ring. For a long moment, there is silence.

Noelle ...

Still kneeling, Noelle turns towards Kris. Berdly's ice crystal dims slightly.

Noelle What... Noelle What happened? Noelle There was so much snow, I couldn't see anything... Noelle I...

Noelle stands, facing away from the viewer. Another moment of silence.

Noelle I don't feel so good. Noelle I think Noelle I'm going to go home.

Noelle walks away unsteadily, disappearing off the top of the screen. The encounter ends with no victory message appearing and no D$/EXP earned.
