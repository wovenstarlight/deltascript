Before the fight

Kris, Ralsei, and Susie proceed over the Great Board until they come upon Lancer, facing away.

Hohoho... Well STEP on my BOOTS.

If you named your party "The $!$? Squad"

If it isn't the (name I'm not allowed to say).

If you named your party "The Lancer Fan Club"

If it isn't the Lancer Fan Club.

If you named your party "The Fun Gang"

If it isn't the Friendly Fun Gang.

Error

Displays if your team name choice wasn't stored properly.

If it isn't the Guys
You boys or girls had better turn back while you can. Lancer!!! What is it this time!? Hohoho... I'm simply warning you...!

Lancer turns around and steps forward towards the party, accompanied by a scare chord.

Something EXTREMELY dangerous is lying ahead!

Lancer steps forward again, accompanied by a higher-pitched scare chord.

Hohoho! It's actually really inconvenient!

Lancer steps forward again, accompanied by an even higher-pitched scare chord.

Ha! I can't go home at all because I'm so scared! Wh... What is it!?

Lancer moves out of the way for the party to proceed. The camera pans forward with them, revealing a C. Round standing on the second-to-last row of the Great Board.

Oh, hey, little guy... THAT??? THAT'S what you're afraid of??? W... Wow, purple girl! You aren't scared!? Heh, why would I be? What's it gonna do? Hmm, well, normally...

C. Round steps forward to the last row of the Great Board, which flashes under its feet. A crown falls from the sky, landing on top of it. C. Round grows in size into a K. Round, which then takes an aggressive stance, spooking Susie and Ralsei.

It crushes people to death, I think.

The fight begins.

After the fight

Once the crown is 100% loose, it slides off K. Round and falls to the ground, sliding offscreen. K. Round transforms back into C. Round and rolls offscreen, ending the encounter.

We... We did it!?

Lancer moves to stand in front of the party.

Wow!! You clowns really ARE heroes!! You saved the next twenty minutes of my life!! Yeah, yeah, I am pretty great, huh...?

Ralsei turns around to face Susie. Kris turns around as well.

Ummm, Susie...? Not to downplay your contributions, but... You... didn't actually help at all. You only made everything WORSE by attacking it. If you had just acted nice to it in the first place... We could have avoided that entire battle. Huh? Are you FOR REAL? That thing was BLOODTHIRSTY!!! The only thing keeping it at bay was MY AX!

Ralsei looks aside briefly before facing Susie again.

And, earlier, you terrorized those troops... Those guys were ENEMIES. They're FOR terrorizing. Yeah! She's right!! And before that, you ate an innocent person's cake...

Susie looks aside briefly before facing the others again.

Cakes... are also my enemy. ... Susie... Whether you like it or not... You're a hero. One with the power to bring peace to the future. Could you please start... acting like one?

Susie turns around, losing her smile and staying silent for a moment.

Yeah, when you lay it out like that... I've been a pretty bad hero, haven't I? ...

Susie walks to the side, still unsmiling. A brief pause.

Alright, Ralsei. You got it. I'll change my ways. From now on, I won't be such a rotten hero anymore.

Susie quickly makes her way forward to stand near Lancer and turns with a smile to face Kris and Ralsei, both looking at her.

I'll just be one of the BAD GUYS instead!

Ralsei reels in shock. Lancer's theme starts playing.

R... Really!? You're going to be on MY team? Yeah, sounds way easier, honestly. Susie, you can't just - Quiet, Toothpaste Boy! Susie is MY comrade now! Hahaha, yeah, Toothpaste Boy! We're going to have monogrammed track jackets! Yeah! And sleepovers! Where we tell each other secrets! Ummm, yeah? Anyway, uhh, see you guys never. Haha!! If you can even last that long!

Lancer and Susie laugh together as they leave the Great Board, heading ahead towards the Forest. The music stops. Ralsei walks ahead of Kris, looking after the other two. A brief pause.

Kris... Perhaps I shouldn't have been so hard on her. ... But, I just worry that if Susie is too eager to fight... Then... Well, let's just be kind to her, OK, Kris? I'm sure Susie'll come around soon!

Ralsei leads Kris forward; the scene fades to black, then fades back in to reveal the Forest.
