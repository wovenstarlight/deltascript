General flavor text

Initial encounter

Solo

Maus blocked the way!

With 1 or 2 other Maice

Maice blocked the way!

With Poppup

Poppup and Maus appeared.

With 2 Werewires

Unused.

Werewire and Maus swung down like stringed superheroes!

With 2 Swatchlings

Unused.

Swatchling and vermin appeared!

Standard

Maus is thinking about overly elaborate machines.
Maus is clicking and squeaking.
Maus keeps running between your feet.
Maus is keenly aware of the fear it invokes.

1% chance:

Maus would like to go to its favorite bistro in this marvelous city.

1% chance:

Smells like wood shavings.

Tired / Low HP

Maus's clicking sounds more like splat noises.

Spareable

Maus is squeaking cheerfully.

Press [MENU] To Trap!!!

The Maus moves back and forth at the center of the screen. A cage at the top of the screen also goes back and forth but at a different speed. [MENU] must be pressed to drop the cage at the right time, catching the Maus.

{% include acticon chara="Ralsei" -%} TrapAll / {% include acticon chara="Noelle" -%} TrapAll

Flavor text

Press [MENU] to trap all the enemies!

All Maice (and Tasques) move back and forth at the center of the screen. A cage at the top of the screen also goes back and forth but at a different speed. [MENU] must be pressed to drop the cage at the right time, catching the enemies.

Caught one enemy

Caught the enemy!

The caught enemy gains 100% Mercy.

Caught multiple enemies

The "#" is replaced by the number of captured enemies.

Caught # enemies!

The caught enemies gain 100% Mercy.

Missed

Didn't catch anything...

Nothing happens.
