Flavor text

First time across all Gueis in the encounter

The battle music pauses, and Gerson walks into the encounter from the left with thumping footsteps. He turns to the party and the music resumes.

Gerson How's it goin'? Susie Get out of here!! These guys are tough!! Gerson Alright, alright...

The music pauses as Gerson begins leaving the encounter towards the left, but stops and turns towards the Gueis as his cane flies out of his hand, falling into the center of the encounter.

Gerson Whoops, dropped my cane. Gerson Ya mind getting that for me?

Gerson walks offscreen to the left. The music resumes and the encounter continues. In the following turn, the cane is present inside the bullet box and moves to indicate spots that the Gueis's bullets will not hit.

Second time, if you haven't attacked any Guei

The battle music pauses, and Gerson (lacking his cane) walks into the encounter from the left with thumping footsteps, this time along the top instead of along the bottom. Gerson turns to the party and the music resumes.

Gerson Hmm, you called it a battle, but I don't see much fightin'. Ralsei We don't hurt the enemies, we SPARE them! Gerson Spare, huh? Like this?

Gerson turns to the Gueis and regains his cane long enough to wave it at them. They are automatically spared, described as being "RECRUITED BY OLDMAN". Susie and Ralsei look stunned.

Ralsei Y... you recruited them!? Gerson Huh, whaddya know, it works! Gerson I'll let you kids finish your battle! Geh heh heh!

The music pauses as Gerson walks offscreen to the left. After he leaves, Susie and Ralsei look calmer.

(But, the battle was already over.) (The Old Man received 438 Dark Dollars.)

The encounter ends.

Second time onwards, if you attacked any Guei

Called the OldMan! Nothing happened!
