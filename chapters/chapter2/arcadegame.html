The dynamic background fades out to reveal the arcade machine, with Queen now standing in front of the rightmost controls. Susie and Kris jump onto the still-kneeling Ralsei's back, squishing him flat so he's sprawled facedown on the ground. But they can now reach the controls!

Commence Virtual Combat

As commanded, the game begins.

Playing the game

The screen of the arcade machine expands to fill the player's screen, with the party and Queen still visible at the bottom, and the low-poly game begins, with a voiceover accompanying the text "ROUND 1" followed by "BEGIN". The fight UI appears, with a countdown at the center labelled "TIMER". Blue and green bars on the left and right are labelled "HERO HP" and "QUEEN HP" respectively.

Instructions

As soon as the fight UI appears, Queen gives attacking instructions:

Queen Press [CONFIRM] Or [CANCEL] If You Dare Susie Shut up!!

Once Queen starts kicking, she gives dodging instructions:

Queen DODGE with [LEFT] or [RIGHT]! Susie I said shut up!!

Once the wheel attacks begin, she gives ducking instructions:

Queen Press [DOWN] to Duck!

Finally, a wheel falls from the sky, with an arrow indicating to "HIT IT". No subsequent instructions are given until the game ends.

Losing the game

If HERO HP depletes to 0, the party's avatar disappears in an explosion as the fight UI disappears. Queen's avatar's appears and grows to a large size, laughing and jittering, after which the text "YOU LOSE!" appears. Then a menu appears reading "GIVE UP?" with the options "FIGHT AGAIN" and "RETIRE" underneath, an arrow indicating the currently-selected option.

Choosing "FIGHT AGAIN" instantly restarts the game. Choosing "RETIRE" causes Susie to force the selection cursor over to "FIGHT AGAIN", with the following dialogue:

First loss

Susie Shut up! That was just practice!

Subsequent losses

Susie Shut up, that was just a warm up!

Reaching a draw

If the timer runs out, the text "DRAW" blinks on the screen, and Queen's avatar no longer attacks. Attacking with [CONFIRM] or [CANCEL] causes the game to restart.

Winning the game

With the last hit from the party's avatar, the fight UI disappears and the avatars are frozen for a moment. The music cuts out as Queen's avatar's head explodes. The explosion then overtakes the whole screen, which fades back to the Cyber Field platform. The party and Queen hurriedly back away as the entire arcade machine explodes to pieces with the same low-resoluton VFX.

The party turns to Queen.
