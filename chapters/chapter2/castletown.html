Transforming Castle Town

Seeing the transformation

Kris reenters the Dark World with the same shimmering transformation as they fall. This time, however, the trash orb still on their head transforms into dozens of sparkles that float away. Kris lands in the town square, where Ralsei and Susie hurry up to them.

Hey, Kris, so where is every...

The party turns towards the castle as the sparks of light fall over their heads and the screen fades to white.

... one...?

The screen fades back in to reveal the transformed Castle Town, the party now standing in a line at its center. Lancer enters from the north and comes up to them, doing a spin. His theme starts playing.

Guess who's back, Clowns!? Lancer!!!

Susie runs up to him and jumps to give him a high-five; he falls flat on his back when he lands. They both laugh together. Susie turns to Kris.

Hey, Kris, dunno what you did, but hell yeah!

She turns to the side.

Everyone's here!

If any Card Kingdom Darkners were defeated violently

A pause. Susie turns away from everyone.

Uh, hey. Wait. I think we beat up some of these guys. Are they not gonna like... gang up on us now?

Lancer stands back up and Susie looks at him.

Don't worry, Susie!

He spins around.

Pop-pop got trapped in a hamster cage, making me Prince King. And with my new dad-like powers, I declared you forgiven!

Susie turns to Kris and Ralsei.

Cool, well, guess I won't beat anyone up here, either.

Susie looks to Lancer as he speaks.

Yep! Now all that's left is for everyone to enjoy the new home!

A pause. Susie turns towards the castle.

Wait, new home? Where's your... OLD home?

Lancer gets to his feet (if he hadn't already) and spins around. Susie looks at him.

Alllll gone! Gone?

Ralsei walks up beside Lancer; he and Susie look at Ralsei.

Right... When Kris sealed the fountain, That "Dark World" disappeared... ... And turned back into a normal classroom. ... So where's Lancer gonna live now? Don't worry, Susie!! We'll conquer Ralsei's castle as our own!!

Susie and Lancer turn to each other and laugh together. While they do, Ralsei walks back over to Kris.

(Kris, as you bring Dark World Denizens back here...) (The power of our Fountain...) (Will transform this town more and more.) (From now on, the enemies we SPARE...) (Will be RECRUITED to our town.) (So let's keep SPARING enemies, okay?)

Susie and Lancer turn to Ralsei and Kris, who look back at them.

Anyhow, why don't we all have a look around? We can head NORTH towards the CASTLE. I have a special surprise to show you there!

They all look at Lancer when he speaks.

Ahaha!! Not if I surprise myself first!! Last one there is a fresh and fragrant egg!!

Lancer speeds off to the north, his theme music fading. Susie comes to stand beside Kris, facing them, and Ralsei looks at them as well.

C'mon, Kris! You gonna let him beat us!?

Some of the visible Darkners move off to the sides as control returns to the player and Castle Town's updated theme begins playing. They can speak to the other residents or head north to see the surprise waiting in Ralsei's castle.
