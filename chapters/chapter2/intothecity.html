Falling into the Trash Zone

As the screen fades to black, a loud thud is heard as the party land after their fall. The quiet sounds of the Trash Zone fade in, and shortly after, the screen lightens to show the party members buried head-first in a pile of trash.

Susie's feet lower out of sight, and a moment later, her head pops up over the pile of trash, unsmiling.

L... Looks like this trash saved us.

She turns to the others; Kris and then Ralsei similarly right themselves.

C'mon...

Kris jumps down to the street, followed by Ralsei. Susie also jumps, but rather than going down to the street, she lands on a large red prohibition sign perched near the top of the pile. She poses, grinning, as the others turn to face her.

Haha, who's king of the trash pile now!?

Susie laughs, but under her feet, the prohibition sign shifts and slides out from under her. Susie falls down to the street, landing in a kneel. An alarmed Ralsei and Kris have turned to watch, and once she lands, they dart towards her. A pause.

O... Ow...! Susie, are you alright?

Susie stands up a little slower than normal, smiling, but her eyes are shadowed. A pause.

... Heh, it's nothing. I just...

Susie drops back into a kneel.

O... ow.

Ralsei walks up right beside her. A slight pause.

... sure you don't want me to look? L-look at what? I'm fine!

A pause. Then, slowly, Ralsei shifts even closer and puts his arms around her. A pause. Susie bolts to her feet, looking stunned.

WOAH HEY WHAT THE HECK ARE YOU--

She then flashes green, surrounded by sparkles, as Ralsei heals her. After a pause, he quickly pulls back, smiling at her.

Feel better?

Susie looks extremely agitated.

FEEL BETTER!? AFTER YOU JUST CAME UP AND--

She turns around. A long pause. Then she turns to the side away from the player.

Umm... doesn't feel WORSE, I guess. ...

A slight pause. Susie begins scratching her head.

How do you do that, anyway? That... healing... thingy. Oh, healing magic? It's simple, Susie. Anyone can do it. ... I could even teach you, if you're interested.

Still turned away, Susie lowers her hand.

Heh, ask me again when it's, like, blood explosion magic.

She turns her head slightly towards Ralsei, though her face still can't be seen.

...

She turns away and scratches her head again.

But, um, if you WANTED, I guess I could learn it. Then you wouldn't, y'know, have to annoy me with it anymore. Sure, Susie. Next time we have a break!

Susie turns, unsmiling, and hurries towards Kris, Ralsei backing out of her way.

(Let's just go already, Kris...)

Ralsei retakes his place in line, and control is returned to the player, who can proceed through the Trash Zone.

Splitting up

After exiting the Trash Zone, the party enters the start of an alleyway branching into two paths. Kris stops near the end of the path they came down, while Susie and Ralsei move to stand in front of the eastern and northern paths respectively. The party members all turn away from the player to look along the path Ralsei stands in front of for a long moment. Then Ralsei turns around to face Kris and Susie.

There's a fork in the path... Kris, it seems like... It might be advantageous if we split up. Who do you want to go with?

Dialogue continues.

Dialogue continues.

Susie charges forward to stand in front of Ralsei, making him take a step back.

Hey, why does Kris get to choose all the time!?

Susie grabs a startled Ralsei's hand and turns to face down the northern path, grinning widely.

Ralsei, you're going with me! H-huh!? K-Kris, I...!!

Susie drags Ralsei away down the northern alleyway. As they exit offscreen, traffic cones with dog-like faces appear to block the northern path. Interacting with these cones results in a high-pitched bark, but no other reaction.

Entering Cyber City

Kris must proceed down the eastern path alone, entering Cyber City proper. They walk past a sign reading "WELCOME TO CYBER CITY"; visible behind the sign is another path on which Susie and Ralsei can be seen walking.

Just past the welcome sign is a SAVE point:

You have neither fluffy boys nor mean girls with you now. Reluctantly, you are filled with the power from the cat sign.

Kris can walk further into Cyber City, coming upon roads filled with cars.

Reading the INFO sign
