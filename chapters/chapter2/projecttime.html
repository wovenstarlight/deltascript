Leaving school

Upon arriving back in the Light World, Kris and an unsmiling Susie stand outside the supply closet doors.

Well, damn. GUESS we gotta do our project. (Sigh...)

Susie looks at Kris, smiling.

Least we got Lancer, right!?

A pause. Susie looks around.

... Lancer? Where are you?

Susie loses her smile.

Damn, guess he skipped out after all, huh.

Susie walks away from the supply closet, Kris a step behind.

Hmm... so, where should we do this?

She looks at Kris. A slight pause.

... what? The Library?

Susie turns to the side.

Ugh... Well, alright.

She walks off towards the entrance. Control returns to the player.

Inside the library

Kris enters the library. Susie stands some distance ahead and turns to face them when she speaks.

Alright. We're here.

She walks into the center of the library.

School. Goddamn. Project.

She walks to the bookshelf on the left, closer to the counter. Kris walks further into the library, turning to look at her.

Books. God. Damn. Crazy about them.

Susie turns to Kris.

...

Susie turns away from the shelf, no longer smiling.

Hey, Kris, where the hell IS everybody?

She walks over to Kris.

Something seems kinda... off.

A long pause. Then Susie runs over to the computer lab doors.

Screw it let's just go play Space Pinball in the computer lab

She throws open the doors, but beyond them lies pitch darkness that casts the library into eerie shadow. Susie backs away slowly, and vaguely ominous music plays.

H... huh...? It's like...

Susie backs away further and Kris steps up beside her. They look at each other.

... the inside of the closet?

They look back to the doors.

Kris, you don't think this could be...

They approach the doors, then turn to each other.

... another Dark World?

They both look back at the doors. A slight pause.

... Well. Guess this means we can't start our project. ... unless. You know. Kris. If there's a Dark Fountain in there. You can seal it... And it'll turn back into the computer lab, right? ... So what's it gonna be, Kris? Yeah, let's just drop the act! Our last adventure was great, right?! I couldn't stop thinking about having another! I don't know what's in there, but... We can't live if we don't find out, right!? ... Kris, you're right. Like, you said a correct fact. But you made a mistake. The mistake of knowing me. 'Cause when I see a big pit, All I wanna do is jump in. And as long as you're with me... I'm dragging you in too!

Kris and Susie look at each other.

C'mon, Kris!!

They turn to the door, and with a quick run-up, leap into the Dark World.
