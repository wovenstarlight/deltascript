Annoying Mouse Room

Discovering the mice

In the first room to the south is a puzzle area with two doors in the north wall labelled "out" and "in" as well as an "INFO" screen between them, and a button on the way to the other side. When Kris steps on this button, the "out" door thuds open, drawing Noelle's attention. A mouse appears in the doorway with a squeak. Noelle spins around, hands flying up to her face in a panic.

A m-m-m-m-m... Mouse!?

Three mice rush out of the hole, turning when they hit any green rotator blocks. Noelle jumps out of their way with a shriek when they come close, landing near the right side of the room. Dialogue then proceeds depending on whether you already solved the puzzle or not.

If you already moved the rotator block to the correct position

The mice bounce off the blocks and enter the "in" hole. Once they're gone, Noelle walks over to Kris.

W-well, hopefully that won't happen again!

The puzzle is solved, and pressing the button no longer does anything. The party can proceed onwards.

If you didn't move the rotator block to the correct position

The mice bounce off the green rotator blocks and wall, exiting the room to the north. Noelle still looks frightened.

S... sorry, Kris, could you... d-deal with them somehow? Maybe if you can get them in the other mouse hole?

A sign appears near the bottom proclaiming this to be the "annoying mouse room", and Noelle looks calmer. Control is returned to the player, who must work on the puzzle. Alternatively, they can also pressure Noelle into skipping the puzzle.

Failing to solve the puzzle

Mice rush out and bounce off the walls and rotator blocks, passing Noelle or Kris on the northern/southern paths respectively as they leave the room. Noelle shrieks and jumps in fright when they appear.

First time

K-Kris! If the mice hit a wall, they'll come over here... (Try using those blocks to get them into the hole?)

Second time

K-Kris!! Gosh, you did that on purpose, didn't you!? (It's like when they swapped my eggnog with mayo...)

Subsequent times

...

Leaving to the north

As in, attempting to backtrack to the SAVE point.

Noelle turns to face Kris.

First time

Oh, merry Krismaaaas--! That's where we came from!

Subsequent times

Kris, are you afraid of mice, too? Fahahaha!

Leaving to the south

As in, attempting to leave down the bottom path without solving the puzzle.

First time

H-hey, Kris? You can't leave yet, y'know! You need to deal with the mice, first. You can do it, Kris! I believe in you!

Second time

Kris, you, um... aren't gonna leave me here, are you? Th-think about all the p-pencils I gave you! The... the peppermint cookies I shared with you!

Third time

... I'll do your homework for you.

Subsequent times

Kris? Are... you really going to leave me...? Th-thanks, Kris... ... h-huh? What did you say? B-but, th... the mice... Th-thanks, Kris... ... I... I... ...

Noelle shields her head with her arms and rushes past the puzzle, stepping on the button and sending mice running out behind her. She stops in front of Kris.

Ph... phew... (We'd better not have to do something like that again...)

She steps towards Kris.

(Seriously, when did Kris get so bossy?)

The puzzle is considered solved. The party can proceed onwards.

Solving the puzzle

That is, solving it by stepping on the button for the second time or later.

The mice enter the "in" hole. Noelle walks over to join Kris.

L-looks like that took care of it...

The puzzle is solved, and the party can proceed onwards.

Tutorial fight with Virovirokun

The Virovirokun switches locations to ensure it's the first encounter the player faces with Noelle in the party, depending on where the player guides the party:

  • If you leave the "trucies" alleyway towards the left, it appears in the previous room immediately in front of the two trashcans.
  • If you go through the Annoying Mouse Room towards the shopping area, it appears in the path to the shopping area.

Immediately upon entering the encounter with Virovirokun, Noelle is panicked:

H-huh!? What's going on!? What are we doing!?

Using N-Action has no effect initially, as Noelle does not understand the process of fighting:

Noelle was clueless!

The player can proceed the fight either peacefully or violently; the latter is required to start the Weird Route.

Ending the fight peacefully

The player must use {% include acticon chara="Noelle" -%} TakeCareX. The first use of this ACT gives the following dialogue:

H... huh? What are you telling me to do? ACT...? Can someone please explain what's going on?! Virovirokun Oh boy, is this your first time in a battle? Uh... well... um... yes. Virovirokun Oh boy...

The music cuts out and the screen fades to black.

You and Virovirokun explained how battles work to Noelle.

The encounter fades back in.

I get it! It's kind of like Dragon Blazers! And if we're nice to you, we can win through mercy? Virovirokun Yeah And the way I have to do that is... Dressing up in a weird costume. Virovirokun Yeah ...

The music resumes and the standard flavor text plays out. The Virovirokun gains 100% Mercy and may be spared. N-Action also becomes effective.

At this point, despite its full Mercy gauge, the Virovirokun may still be defeated violently to embark upon the Weird Route.

Ending the fight violently

The fight can be ended by attacking the Virovirokun until it is LOST, either via normal attacks using the FIGHT option or via Noelle's combat spell Ice Shock. For this fight only, Ice Shock is guaranteed to defeat the Virovirokun in a single hit. (One can also use normal attacks to whittle down the Virovirokun's health and then use Ice Shock as the finisher, though that isn't necessary.)

As long as the last hit dealt to the Virovirokun is from Ice Shock, the Weird Route is enabled.

Once the Virovirokun is defeated, Kris and Noelle may proceed further into Cyber City on the normal route, or backtrack to complete the next requirements for the Weird Route.
