Running into the others

As Kris approaches Noelle, standing in the center of an alleyway, the music fades. Noelle turns around, noticing Kris.

K-Kris!! What are you---

Queen speaks from somewhere offscreen:

Oh Noelle Sweetie

Noelle turns to look further down the alleyway, alerted. Then she runs into a niche in the wall, crouching down in the shadows and putting her hands over her mouth.

(Don't let her find me...)

A slight pause. Queen enters from the right side of the alleyway and pauses at the bend. Her theme begins playing as she speaks.

Noelle Sweetie Darling Honey Where Are You

She turns around, laughing.

We're A Team Now Remember You Still Have To Be My Willing Minion

She walks forward, stopping when she notices Kris.

... Kris It Appears Our Comrades Have Pressed The Escape Key On Us .... So It Has Come To This... In Order To Find Our Teams ... We Must Form An Uneasy Truce...

Queen turns to the side, looking shocked, as Berdly speaks from offscreen:

Queen!! My splendid Queen!! Where are you!! Oh No Don't Let Him Find Me

Queen hurries over to the same niche where Noelle is hiding; she jumps up and into the niche, bracing a foot and a hand against either side of the niche and keeping herself in the air. Noelle looks up at her, her eyes huge and round.

Meanwhile, Berdly enters from the right side of the alleyway and stops when he notices Kris.

Ah, Kris... NOT the lifeform I was looking for. ... Hmm? Where's the rest of the C+ Squad? Don't tell me you got abandoned!? HA!! HAHA!! That's what you get for trusting Susie!! HA!!

Berdly suddenly looks shocked.

H... Huh? Noelle? Queen?

Berdly turns away from Kris, raising his clenched fists and looking aggrieved.

They're just... busy retro gaming somewhere!!

He turns back to Kris, now looking smug.

I bet they're waiting for me on the player select screen!! Listening to strangely groovy music!! HAHA! HA!! HA!! ...

Berdly turns to the side away from the player.

...Sigh.

He turns to Kris.

So it's come to this. In order to find our respective teams... It APPEARS... We must form an uneasy truce...

Hidden in her niche, Queen looks shocked and angry.

What The Who The Beep Said You Could Double Trucies

A pause. Berdly turns to the side away from the player and facing the niche.

Huh? Did you hear something?

A pause. Queen smiles again.

Honk Honk No It's Just The Sounds Of The City Oh okay.

Berdly turns back to Kris.

Hmph. Well I'll be ahead. Don't be LATE!!!

Berdly exits the alleyway to the right, from where he came. Once he's gone, Queen jumps down from the niche, Noelle growing somewhat calmer once she's gone. Now standing normally in the center of the alleyway, Queen faces Kris.

Kris As Part Of Our Truce (The Better One) Keep Distracting Berdly And Find Noelle For You See...

Queen turns away from Kris and takes a few steps away. A pause.

Berdly... He's...

A pause. Queen turns to face Kris again.

OK There's Nothing Wrong With Him He's Just Annoying

Queen begins walking towards the right side of the alleyway, speaking as she goes; her theme fades into silence as she leaves.

Toodles

Once Queen is gone, Noelle emerges from the niche and approaches Kris, the camera panning to focus on them both.

Kris, I... um... if it's okay... ... G-got any room for another truce? Thanks, Kris... Har har, very funny! Well, I'd rather take my chances with you than her!

A gentle piano fanfare plays.

(Noelle joined the party!)

Noelle gets into line behind Kris. Control is returned to the player, who can either continue out of the alleyway or make a stop at the newly-spawned SAVE point.

SAVE point

Appears in the niche where Noelle and Queen were hiding, once the above cutscene ends. (This narration plays only when Noelle is in the party; see the SAVE points page for the alternate narration when she's not present.)

Noelle has joined your party. You are filled with the power of not knowing what a sugarplum is.

Leaving

The below sets of dialogue are available only once each. Also, they only play immediately after the "triple trucies" conversation ends; if you reenter this area or reload this SAVE file, these dialogues will not play.

To the left

That is, backtracking towards the Trash Zone and the Virovirokun encounter along the way.

K... Kris...? Isn't that the wrong way...? (Somehow I'm already regretting this...)

To the right

That is, heading further into Cyber City, towards the Annoying Mouse Room.

So, um, any idea where Susie is? What!? WHAT!? I'm just asking!! It's a normal question!!
