Before the fight

As Kris walks out of the long room with rollercoaster tracks, a shadowed figure hanging from green wires abruptly drops down in their way, throwing them back and leaving them kneeling. The shadow's head jerks unnaturally a few times, and the screen fades to black.

Eyes flash in the dark, pink and yellow.

Spamton NEO HOLY [[Cungadero]] DO I FEEL GOOD ...

The screen brightens to reveal Spamton NEO. Deal Gone Wrong plays as Spamton NEO's head jerks here and there, limbs swinging.

Spamton NEO HERE I AM!! KRIS!!

Spamton NEO's head grows larger.

Spamton NEO BIG

And larger...

Spamton NEO BIG,

And larger.

Spamton NEO [[BIGGER AND BETTER THAN EVER]]

His head returns to its normal size. Kris gets to their feet.

Spamton NEO HA HA HA ... THIS POWER IS

He looks down at Kris.

Spamton NEO FREEDOM.

His head starts jerking around again.

Spamton NEO I WON'T HAVE TO BE JUST A PUPPET ANY MORE!!!! Spamton NEO ...

Spamton NEO turns away from Kris.

Spamton NEO OR... so... I... thought. Spamton NEO WHAT ARE THESE STRINGS!? WHY AM I NOT [BIG] ENOUGH!? It's still DARK... SO DARK!

Spamton NEO KRIS.

Again, Spamton NEO approaches and Kris backs up.

Spamton NEO KRIS. KRIS. KRIS. Spamton NEO THAT'S RIGHT. YOU. I NEED YOU.

Spamton NEO H E A V E N

Spamton NEO draws back slightly and his head returns to its normal size. He lifts an arm with a cannon at its end, leveling it at Kris.

Spamton NEO I JUST NEED THAT LITTLE,

Spamton NEO [[SOUL]] Y O U HAVE

Spamton NEO approaches Kris, laughing, and has almost reached them when a Rude Buster flies in from screen left and sends him flying offscreen. Susie and Ralsei run over, pausing when they see Kris.

Kris!

They run over to Kris.

The hell was that weirdo!? Kris, we were worried about you, so...

Spamton NEO [Attention Customers! Clean up on Aisle 3!] Spamton NEO SOMEONE LEFT [There] SOULS, [Lyeing Around......]

Blue Spamton head-shaped bullets come flying at the party, but before they can land, the background cuts to black and the SOUL appears. It turns sideways, becoming yellow, and can fire shots at the heads to destroy them, ending with a charged-up big shot to destroy the largest bullet.

The background cuts back in, with Spamton NEO now dangling over the tracks in front of the alarmed party.
