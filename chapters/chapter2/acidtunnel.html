Beginning the journey

The corridor Kris and Ralsei are walking through abruptly gives way to a lake of acid. Upon approaching the lake, Ralsei is startled.

A... A giant lake of acid!?

Ralsei relaxes slightly as the screen on the wall turns on to show Queen.

Oh You Mean The Free Pool You Will Learn To Love It Like Family Why do you have this!? It Is My Mansion's Energy Source And Fun To Drink Bye Ha Ha Ha

Queen's monitor turns off.

Kris can walk over to the lever at the side and activate it, causing a swan-shaped boat to float up from the bottom of the screen.

I suppose... this... works. Haha.

Acid Tunnel of Love begins playing. Interacting with the lever again gives the following:

(The lever is stuck.)

Interacting with the boat has Kris and Ralsei board it; they start floating along the acid lake, occasionally having to move out of the way of small islands with tiny model foliage, mountains, and houses. (Floating the boat close to the model houses turns their roofs from blue to red with a ping.)

Now, we have to think of some way to distract Queen...

The closest monitor turns on, showing Queen.

The monitor turns off. The boat travels some distance in silence.

What are these... Little houses?

The closest monitor turns on, showing Queen.

The monitor turns off. The boat travels some distance in silence. A banana comes into view, rotating slowly above the acid ahead of the boat.

Kris Get The Banana Kr... Kris isn't going to do what you say!

If you collect the banana

The monitor turns off.

Th-that's okay, Kris. It's good for you.

If you don't collect the banana

After a moment, the closest monitor turns on to show Queen.

Fools I Wanted You To Avoid It Now You Will Become Potassium Deficient

The monitor turns off.

The boat travels further in silence. Islands on both sides bracket the boat, directing it towards a tunnel.

... looks like we're coming up on a tunnel!

In the tunnel, a giant hand statue sticks out of the acid, the opening to a corridor beside it. The boat runs into it, front squishing against the hand with a squeak. After a pause, Ralsei speaks, Kris turning to face him.

H-huh? There's something blocking the way...

Kris gets off the boat into the corridor to its left, Ralsei following them and turning to them.

Maybe we can find some way to get rid of this...?

The boat cannot be boarded at this point; interacting with it gives the following message:

(Looks like a certain bird is having a ride on the acid river.)

Interacting with the hand gives the following:

A giant hand... Sure would be nice to be pet by something like that!

Removing the obstacle

The corridor leads into an area filled with house puzzles. Solving the puzzles leads to a path overlooking the acid lake, where another hand statue stands in the acid. A lever lies at the end of the path; interacting with it causes the first hand to rise out of the acid in front of this second hand; the two of them slap together. A shortcut leading back to the puzzle area entrance opens up.

Interacting with the lever again gives the following:

(Pulling the lever back would undo the high-five.) (You can't undo a high-five.)

Kris and Ralsei can head back to the boat, now floating unobstructed on the acid lake. Interacting with it causes Kris and Ralsei to board the boat again.

Let's go, Kris!

The boat floats on.

Ralsei's talk

The boat enters a dim tunnel illuminated from below by the glowing acid. After a brief moment, Ralsei speaks up.

Kris... hey, Kris. ... is it s-strange to say... It's nice spending time alone with you like this...? ...

Ralsei turns to the side, facing the player.

I'm happy hearing you say that, Kris...

Ralsei turns to the side, facing away from the player.

Err, then, I, err -

A fractional pause; Ralsei turns away from Kris.

Ah, s-sarcastic! I was just being sarcastic! Haha, sorry about that!

Ralsei turns back towards Kris.

It's so... you-like!! ... ... I guess I like you-like things... haha. ... I just wonder what... being "Ralsei-like" even is...? ... Sorry! Haha, I got off-track. Let's just enjoy the ride, Kris. Haha.

Encountering Rouxls Kaard

After Ralsei finishes talking, there's a long pause. The tunnel opens up into a wider room, with an island at its center that blocks the swan boat's passage; as the boat comes close, the camera pans over to show the whole island. The island is covered in model houses, the top left one with a red roof, the bottom right one with a blue roof, and all others colorless.

W... wow, Kris! Look at all those houses! I wonder what'll happen if we change their color!

The screen darkens slightly as someone speaks.

The screen brightens as Rouxls Kaard teleports in on the other side of the island, wearing a pirate hat and standing atop a gray ship. His theme starts playing.
