Before the fight

As Kris, Susie, and Ralsei proceed through Cyber Field, Sweet hovers above them on a rocket-powered turntable and presents them with another musical challenge played over a moving section that connects two separate areas of the Field.

Upon reaching the other side of the gap, the turntable hits a wall, and Sweet is knocked off it onto the ground in front of the party. After a moment, Sweet stands up, and Cool Beat starts playing.

Sweet D-dead end...!

The party fans out to face Sweet, who takes a step back.

Alright, what's the deal, twerp! Sweet Who's a twerp!? The name's Sweet! Oh, like "suite?" Oh, like... sweets?

Sweet wags a finger.

Sweet Don't tell me you haven't heard of the Sweet Cap'n Cakes?!? Um, doesn't ring a bell...

Sweet reels in shock.

Sweet G-Guess our act's still a bit underground...

Sweet straightens back up, smiling and waving a finger again while speaking.

Sweet Sweet Cap'n Cakes! Not only are we Cyber Field's BEST musical trio... Sweet We're also the last resistance against the big bad Queen and all her crew! Sweet And that starts with resisting YOU!

Susie looks exasperated.

What!? We're fighting Queen, too! Sweet Nuh-uh-uh! Cap'n saw you help her kidnap some girl! Sweet Hey! Hey, Cap!

The party steps back as Cap'n drops down beside Sweet.

Sweet Cap'n Cakes We won't let anyone lay a finger on madamoizels...

Sweet flops face-down in exasperation while Cap'n speaks.

Sweet Cap'n Cakes Except us! That's not the point, Cap'n!!

Cap'n turns away.

Sweet Cap'n Cakes It's like half the point. 60%? 55... We're rebels, not players!

Cap'n turns back towards the party.

Sweet Cap'n Cakes We're rebels that play. We don't play!! We don't play!! Sweet Cap'n Cakes That's it!! K_K!!! Get over here!!

Sweet and Cap'n make space for K_K to drop down beside them.

Sweet Cap'n Cakes K_K, are we players!? ... Sweet Cap'n Cakes I'm a CD player.

Sweet, Cap'n, and K_K all dance as K_K plays simple, cheery music. Susie and Ralsei consider the situation; then, after a moment, they and Kris turn around and begin quietly walking away. Before they can go too far, though, Sweet falls over in alarm.

Sweet Cap'n Cakes Wait! They're getting away!

Sweet and Cap'n face the party, who all turn around. K_K continues playing music and dancing.

Sweet Cap'n Cakes Sweet, chill. There'll be other bad guys! No, this is our chance! Sweet Cap'n Cakes Are you ready!? Let's fight!!

A long pause as K_K continues dancing and playing music. Sweet and Cap'n turn to K_K.

Sweet Cap'n Cakes ... K_K. Yeah? Sweet Cap'n Cakes Play some battle music! OK!

Sweet Cap'n Cakes draw the party into an encounter.

After the fight

Sweet Cap'n Cakes are all dancing along to the music K_K is playing.

Sweet Cap'n Cakes Feel the beat! Watch my moves! Dance, dance!!

Susie points at them.

Hey, losers!! Wrap it up!! Battle's over!!

Sweet Cap'n Cakes stop dancing; Sweet and Cap'n are startled. Sweet falls over face-down.

Sweet Cap'n Cakes Over? In other-type words... We lost!? Sweet Cap'n Cakes ... ... ...

Sweet stands back up, facing K_K.

Sweet Cap'n Cakes Does that mean we can be friends? K_K!! No!!

Cap'n turns away.

Sweet Cap'n Cakes Fighting's not really my style anyways... Yeah! Because you're a coward! Sweet Cap'n Cakes ... I mean, it was fun dancing, but... Sweet. Don't worry. Sweet Cap'n Cakes I think our dancing beat them. Beat them...? Yeah, all mercy-like. Sweet Cap'n Cakes See, when we danced with them, it convinced them we're cool. Now they're on our side? Sweet Cap'n Cakes Yes.

Sweet and Cap'n turn around to face the party again, Sweet smiling and waving a finger.

Sweet Cap'n Cakes Hey!! You three!! Welcome to the good side!! WE ARE the good side!!! Just go with it, Susie... Sweet Cap'n Cakes Let's ronday-voo at our Junk Shop... Sweet Cap'n Cakes We jammed the door shut, but we'll kick it open! Sweet Cap'n Cakes Alright everyone, let's go!

The rocket-powered turntable returns from screen right. Sweet and Cap'n jump atop it, and K_K jumps on top of their heads. The trio fly off to the top of the screen while playing the same cheery music as before that fades into the distance.

The camera returns its focus to the party. They can now return to the Cyber Field crossroads, where the northern path is now unblocked.
