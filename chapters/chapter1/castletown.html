The screen returns to Kris, Susie, and Ralsei in front of the castle.

Kris, Susie... Thank you for listening to my long tale. I deeply believe you two are the HEROES of the LEGEND. That despite whatever enemies you may face... ... You two have the COURAGE to SAVE THE WORLD. DELTA WARRIORS! Please, won't you accept your destiny...?

Susie looks at Kris.

I'M going to find a way out of here. Susie... Wait!
