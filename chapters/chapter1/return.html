In the unused classroom

The screen is black.

Susie Wh... what happened!? I can't see anything!!

Various SFXs play - a crash, a car-like vroom, a slam, a squeak, and a gasp from Susie.

Susie Hey, WATCH IT!

More noises - a crunch, a bark-like squeak, and another crash. Finally, the lights turn on to reveal Susie and Kris standing by the light switch in the unused classroom at school. Cards, a checkerboard, chess pieces and more are strewn across the room. (If no Chapter 1 completion file exists, the window title reads "The Return".)

H... Huh...? The unused classroom...? How'd we get here...?

Susie walks slowly into the center of the room, and after a moment, turns to Kris.

... Hey, Kris... What... What just happened...? Like, that wasn't a dream or anything, was it...?

A pause. Susie turns back to the door.

... Anyway, as I was SAYING, Let's just tell Alphys we couldn't find any chalk.

Susie leaves the classroom. Kris is free to follow after her.

Leaving the classroom

When Kris leaves the classroom, Susie is standing just outside the door. The hallways are dim, with late afternoon light shining through the windows.

Man, the sun's already setting... Everyone else must have gone home by now.

Susie turns around to face Kris.

Guess Alphys'll know better than to ask US next time, huh?

They both walk down the hallway and stop in front of the supply closet, turning to face its doors.

...

Susie and Kris turn to each other.

Well, guess we should go. ... See ya later, I guess.

Kris watches as Susie walks off towards the entrance. But she stops a few steps in. A pause.

... ... ... Kris?

She turns towards Kris, hesitates for a second, then walks back over to them and puts a hand on their shoulder.

Let's go back there tomorrow, alright?

She pulls away. A pause. Susie leaves, and Kris watches her go. Once she's out of sight, they're free to follow.

If you haven't called Toriel by the time Kris reaches the school entrance, Kris will receive a call from Toriel asking where they've been.

After this, Kris can leave the school and explore Hometown before returning home for the night.
