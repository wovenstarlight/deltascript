Vessel creation

A red SOUL appears at the center of the screen.

Voice EXCELLENT. Voice TRULY  EXCELLENT. Voice NOW. Voice WE MAY BEGIN.

The SOUL disappears. A rippling purple background appears, similar to the Dark Fountains' texture.

Voice FIRST. Voice YOU MUST CREATE A VESSEL. Voice SELECT THE HEAD THAT YOU PREFER.

A selection of heads appears, the SOUL hovering over the currently-selected one.

Voice SELECT THE TORSO THAT YOU PREFER.

Voice SELECT THE LEGS THAT YOU PREFER.

Waking up

Kris's room displays, with Kris in bed and Toriel standing at the center. (If no Chapter 1 completion file exists, the window title reads "The Beginning".)

KRIS! Kris, if you do not wake up, we will be late for school!

Toriel opens the curtains.

I will wait outside for you, alright?

Toriel leaves the room. After a moment, Kris gets out of bed.

Skipping ahead

Available only if a Chapter 1 completion file exists, i.e. if you've played Chapter 1 to completion and viewed the credits previously.

Interacting with the bed gives the following dialogue:

(It's your bed.) (If you go back to sleep, you may miss a lot of important things.) (Will you go to bed?) (For some reason, you decided to go back to sleep...)

The screen fades to black.

(But, when you opened your eyes...)

It cuts to Kris waking up in the Dark World. (If no Chapter 1 completion file exists, the window title reads "THE DARK".)

 (You can sleep in class.)

Leaving the house

Kris leaves the house to find the car in the driveway and Toriel standing next to the driver's side door. Toriel turns towards them.

Kris! There you are! We might still be able to make it!

As the following dialogue plays, Kris and Toriel get into the car, which then drives off.

Toriel Kris... It is a beautiful day today, is it not? Toriel I hope it stays this way when ASRIEL visits next week. Toriel But I wonder... After the excitement of university... Toriel Will he still enjoy coming home to this little town...?

The screen flashes white, then fades to reveal Hometown's streets. The car drives past several Hometown residents, some of whom wave as it goes past.

At the school, Toriel parks the car and gets out along with Kris. Toriel takes Kris's hand (Kris ducks their head) and walks them into the school. At the branching corridors, she hugs them. Kris walks left toward their classroom; after a moment, Toriel walks offscreen to the right towards hers.

At the end of the corridor, Kris enters their classroom.
