First meeting

Upon taking the elevator to "??????" and descending the stairs, the party comes upon a large door with no handle that can be interacted with.

First time

Jevil BOO HOO, BOO HOO, UEE HEE HEE. Jevil SO LONELY, LONELY, I BE... Jevil BUT LO, THREE VISITORS STANDING INSIDE? Jevil WHO ARE YOU FEW? Nice to meet you! I'm Ralsei, this is Kris, and... So, what the hell are you doing in there? Jevil I AM INNOCENT, INNOCENT. I JUST WANTED TO PLAY A GAME, GAME. Jevil BUT THE BORING KINGS FOUND SUCH FUN TO BE A TROUBLE. Jevil AS PUNISHMENT, THEY CRAVED TO IMPRISON MY BODY. Jevil BUT I'M FAST, FAST, CLEVER, CLEVER. Jevil THEY LOST THE CHASE, AND LOCKED UP THEIR ENTIRE RACE, Jevil BUILDING A PRISON AROUND THE WHOLE WORLD. NOW I'M THE ONLY FREE ONE. Huh? Free? But you're clearly the one behind bars... Jevil THINGS DON'T SEEM SUCH FROM HERE, LIGHTNERS! Well, sounds like you don't need any help, then. Jevil THAT IS UN-SO. I GROW LONELY IN MY LITTLE FREEDOM. Jevil SO, SHALL YOU PLAY A GAME WITH ME, ME...? Jevil I'LL SHOW YOU WHAT IT MEANS TO BE FREE!

Dialogue skips to Jevil's instructions, then ends.

 Jevil BOO HOO, BOO HOO, UEE HEE HEE!! Jevil HOW CAN YOU REFUSE, WHEN YOU ARE ALREADY PLAYING...?

Dialogue ends here.

You can now ask Seam about Jevil and the key.

Subsequent times before obtaining the Door Key

Jevil FIND THE KEY, AND I'LL LET YOU BE FREE, FREE. Jevil THE KEY IS HIDDEN FROM THE EYES. THE OLD SHOPKEEPER KNOWS A SECRET.

Opening the door

Plays when you first return to the door with the repaired Door Key.

Jevil UEE HEE HEE, THE KEY, THE KEY. Jevil A MARVELLOUS FUN IS ABOUT TO BREAK FREE. Jevil WON'T YOU LET YOURSELF OUTSIDE? (You put the Door Key in front of the Door.) Jevil YOU CAME TOO FAR! YOUR CHOICE HAS RUN OUT!

The Door Key is removed from the inventory. A door forms in the prison gate.

(The Door Key created a Door.)

The door opens outward and can be entered.

Before the fight

Kris, Susie, and Ralsei enter Jevil's room.

Jevil UEE HEE! VISITORS, VISITORS! NOW WE CAN PLAY, PLAY! Jevil THEN, AFTER YOU, I CAN PLAY WITH EVERYONE ELSE, TOO! So what are we playing, exactly...? Jevil OH, IT'S JUST A SIMPLE NUMBERS GAME.

Jevil laughs as a scythe sweeps past the party members, who narrowly dodge, Susie and Ralsei looking shocked.

Jevil WHEN YOUR HP DROPS TO 0, YOU LOSE! So that's the kinda game you wanna play, huh...? Then, I gotta warn you...

Kris, Susie, and Ralsei draw their weapons.

You're dealing with a couple of sharks.

Jevil laughs.

Jevil UEE HEE HEE! SHARK-TO-SHARK! I WOULDN'T HAVE IT ANY OTHER WAY! Jevil NOW, NOW!! LET THE GAMES BEGIN!!

The fight begins.

After the fight

The fight ends automatically when Jevil is brought to 0 HP or made Tired and pacified.

If you defeated Jevil by fighting

Jevil HA HA HA. WHAT FUN!!! Jevil YOU'RE FAST, FAST, STRONG, STRONG. Jevil BUT THERE ARE YET FASTER, YET STRONGER. Jevil THE HAND OF THE KNIGHT IS DRIFTING FORWARD. Jevil SOON, THE "QUEEN" RETURNS, AND HELL'S ROAR BUBBLES FROM THE DEPTHS... Jevil LIGHTNERS, CAN YOU STOP IT? Jevil UEE HEE HEE! Jevil EITHER WAY, A MISCHIEF-MISCHIEF, A CHAOS-CHAOS...! Jevil LIGHTNERS! FROM INSIDE YOUR LITTLE CELL!! Jevil TAKE ME AND DO YOUR STRONGEST---!

If you defeated Jevil by pacifying

Jevil WHAT FUN!!! I'M EXHAUSTED!!! YOU KIDS TIRED ME UP!! Jevil NOW I WILL SLEEP FOR THE OTHER 100 YEARS. Jevil UNFORTUNATELY, YOU KIDS CANNOT HAVE SUCH A PLEASANT DREAM. Jevil FROM NOW, A NIGHTMARE WILL AWAKEN IN YOUR HEARTS. Jevil IN THE SHADOW OF THE KNIGHT'S HAND... Jevil LIGHTNERS, CAN YOU STOP IT? Jevil UEE HEE HEE! Jevil EITHER WAY, A MISCHIEF-MISCHIEF, A CHAOS-CHAOS...! Jevil LIGHTNERS! FROM INSIDE YOUR LITTLE CELL!! Jevil TAKE THIS AND DO YOUR STRONGEST---!

Jevil transforms into the Devilsknife, accompanied by the voice line "METAMORPHOSIS!", and disappears. The following line is accompanied by Jevil's laughter.

(You got ShadowCrystal.)

If defeated through fighting

(DEVILSKNIFE was added to your WEAPONs.)

Devilsknife is added to the inventory.

If defeated through pacifying

(JEVILSTAIL was added to your ARMORs.)

Jevilstail is added to the inventory.

In both cases, if you have no room in your weapon/armor inventory respectively

(A strange chest appeared outside the room...)

This chest will contain the Devilsknife/Jevilstail.

The screen fades to black, then fades back in with the party members standing on the prison floor. The prison gate is gone completely. The party can return to traversing the castle.
