"Coming" is repeated in the background at a higher pitch. "THE KING OF ONLY" is displayed onscreen and read aloud.

He's GROOVY and NEVER glooby! You can't get this from an EGG! The sensation of your screen The show that makes you scream Say it with him, folks!

Tenna appears at the top right of the screen, and sings along as the text "MR. (Ant) TENNA'S TV TIME!!!" appears onscreen. The text and background then fade away, leaving Tenna standing in front of the party.

Tenna introduces his show

Fading in from the previous scene, Tenna stands on the boardwalk in front of the party, pointing upwards. The howling of the wind is once again audible. Susie and Ralsei are now standing beside Kris, both violently shocked while Kris remains impassive.

Tenna What contestants we've got TODAY, folks---!!!

Ralsei faces Tenna, serious and unsmiling.

Con... Contestants?

An annoyed-looking Susie also turns to face Tenna as he points at them.

Tenna YES!!! You three are the of today's SHOW!!!

Tenna stands up straight, his screen going dark, and snaps his fingers. The party faces the background, Susie and Ralsei jumping in shock, as said background — the boardwalk, the hills in the back, dark sky and all — splits apart down the center and peel away to reveal a wooden stage, set against a star-patterned blue background. Cheery music begins playing.

Once the new stage is set, Susie and Ralsei stand more calmly, and Tenna spreads his hands and grins as the tagline for his show appears on the blue background:

Tenna Mr. Tenna's Marvelous Mystery Board...

Susie and Ralsei jump again as the TV Time logo thuds into place beneath the tagline. Tenna points at the viewer, mic held up to his grinning mouth.

Tenna !!!

An audience appears below the stage, cheering and throwing flowers to Tenna's feet as he bows repeatedly to them. The party has turned to look at the audience, Ralsei and Susie still stunned. Kris and Ralsei turn to Tenna after a moment, calmer, but Susie continues to stare at the audience in shock.

Wh... what!?

She turns to Tenna, now looking exasperated.

What the hell are you doing!?

Tenna points at Susie, who draws back in alarm.

Tenna What am I doing? You should be wondering what YOU'RE doing! Tenna You're about to play the greatest game of YOUR life!

Tenna straightens and points at the audience, accompanied by a splat SFX; Susie calms and resumes her position, looking unhappy.

Tenna Answer CRAZY QUESTIONS!! Pass ...

Tenna points upwards.

Tenna And you'll all win some TV-Tastic !!!

In a rush that proceeds without the player's input, he begins announcing prizes that appear on-screen as their names are called. Tenna appears at various points on the screen around said prizes, sometimes even appearing twice.

Tenna FLORAL COWBOY BATH CURTAINS!!! Tenna A BRAND NEW FAMILY CAR!!! Tenna BIG BRO'S TALKER-BACKER!! Tenna KITCHEN SINK FUR-GUARD!! Tenna CHOCOLATE CHEWY ROLL-UM'S!!! Tenna A GENUINE RALSEI PLUSH!! Hey I didn't agree to

Without the player's input, Tenna cuts Ralsei off, appearing in front of the plush and pointing at the viewer, accompanied by car honking.

Tenna AND THAT'S!

A squeak accompanies the next line. Tenna grows closer to the camera.

Tenna NOT!

The boom of an explosion accompanies the next line. Tenna grows closer to the camera.

Tenna ALL!

Tenna spins back from the camera and takes his position pointing upwards on the right side of the screen. The background of prizes is replaced by the star-patterned blue.

Tenna Hey Doctor Tennaville! What's today's prescription?

Tenna slides offscreen to the right, then reappears dancing from the top of the screen, dressed in a black suit with a cane and hat.

Tenna Why, it looks like a prescription for... ONE !?

A gachapon machine falls from the top of the screen and takes "Doctor Tennaville's" place. Tenna, back in his red and yellow outfit, reappears from the right, hands pressed happily together by his face.

Tenna That's right! Today's is this season's MOST popular item...

The ball machine shakes, then produces a red and white capsule that floats upwards as the screen turns white. As Tenna speaks, the ball becomes a black silhouette.

Tenna Encased in our patented MYSTERY BALL... Tenna This Dark World's ONE AND GREATEST TREASURE!

Tenna pops up in front of the silhouetted ball, pointing at the viewer.

Tenna Hey, bet you wanna know what's INSIDE, don't ya, folks!?

The audience laughs. A pause. Tenna leaps backwards, and the white background fades back into the stage, where Susie and Ralsei look shocked. Tenna points upwards and continues.

Tenna Well, great news, CADOOS and CADETTES! EVERYTHING can be YOURS!

Tenna folds over at the waist, head down.

Tenna All you have to do is...

He throws himself back upright, pointing upwards again.

Tenna

The audience throws flowers at his feet, oohing and aahing. Susie and Ralsei calm down and face Tenna like Kris has been doing, none of them smiling.

Tenna then points to the left, where a gauge labelled "FUN" appears, filled about 80% of the way.

Tenna WOW!!! I've never seen the like THIS, folks!!!

Tenna points at the audience.

Tenna Now don't touch that DIAL, because TONIGHT, WE are gonna have one HECK of a...

Everything stops — the music, the oscillating stars on the background, the waving audience — as Susie lowers her head, eyes cast in shadow, and speaks.

Who the hell is "WE"?

Tenna turns to her, straightening his tie, a little flustered.

Tenna Well, uh... this is a GAME SHOW, and you guys are the CONTESTANTs... right?

Susie's fist begins trembling.

Lemme be a little clearer...

She throws her hands up, exasperated, and glares at Tenna.

We aren't being on your STUPID SHOW!!

Tenna leans back in shock.

Tenna You... you aren't?

The blue background fades to black, the Fun-O-Meter drops to about 50% as the audience disappears, and Tenna himself slumps, head bowed.

Tenna Oh. Well, uh... that's just...

Tenna throws his hands up with a wobbly smile, but shrinks down to about half his size. On the left, the Fun-O-Meter drops to barely 25%, the red filling the gauge now becoming blue. The cheery music starts back up with an aborted, distorted blare, then continues as a very slow, low-pitched version of itself.

Tenna That's just hunky-doody, y'know! You do YOU! Meanwhile, I, uh...

Tenna turns away, shoulders slumped, looking a little more shrunken. The Fun-O-Meter dangles at an angle, like a frame with the nails on one side having come loose.

Tenna Guess it'll just be... me and the logo! Ha.

The party turns to look at the logo, which fades to gray after a moment, red text appearing over it reading "KILLED" with an ominous, thunder-like rumble. A long pause. Then Ralsei and Kris turn to Susie.

Umm, Susie...? ... what?

Susie turns to Ralsei as he walks up to her.

...

Ralsei smiles.

Susie, I...

His smile drops as he ducks his head, looking sad.

I know... I can't go to the festival with you... but...

Head still down, expression still sad, he smiles again.

If I can, I still... want to have fun with you and Kris.

Susie ducks her head as well.

... Ralsei...

Ralsei lifts his head, smile fading.

Maybe...

He turns to look at the TV Time logo in the back.

Maybe we could just try playing this game? If we can't watch TV, maybe we can... be on it?

Susie turns to look at the logo as well.

...

Ralsei turns back to Susie, smiling once more. She scratches her head, still looking at the logo.

Alright, FINE. We'll give it a shot.

Tenna turns around, his head still hanging, and a smiling Susie turns to look at him.

Tenna You... you'll give it a shot? My game?

Susie takes a few steps forward.

...I guess.

Tenna hunches over, shaking for a moment, then throws himself back upright with a grin, arms flung high in the air. The Fun-O-Meter swings back into place and fills up to maximum, shaking violently and flickering through colors. In the back, the star-patterned blue reappears. The cheery music and the cheering audience return as well.

Tenna Oh, you WILL!?!? Oh, you THRILL!?

Tenna points erratically upwards and to the sides, grinning all the while.

Tenna Mike!! The lights!! The sounds!! The whatzits!! The doo-zits!!

He points even faster.

Tenna Turn 'em all on!!

Tenna stops pointing and instead leans in conspiratorially to the audience, whispering with a hand by his mouth.

Tenna

Bowing repeatedly, Tenna moves offscreen to the right.

Tenna Right over here, ladies and germs!!

A red curtain falls over the screen, startling Susie and Ralsei. When it lifts, the stage backdrop, Fun-O-Meter, and audience are all gone. The curtain then drops back down to cover the back half of the stage, and control is returned to the player to move on and meet some of the other performers before the show starts.
