Waking up

Intro

The screen is dark. Someone is snoring. A circle of light fades in to reveal that the culprit is a snoozing Susie in her Dark World outfit. After a few moments, the snot bubble over her nose pops. A pause. She opens her eyes and blinks rapidly.

H... huh!?

The circle of light expands to show Susie lying on a large couch in a new Dark World. Kris is sitting slouched on the couch at her feet. Susie looks "down" at them.

Kris...

She sticks a leg out and prods their head with her foot. A pause. She prods them again.

Kris... Kris... Hey, wake up!!

She kicks them in the head again.

We're... in the Dark World!!

Kris falls over on the couch; they and Susie roll off it, dropping to a crouch on the boardwalk, then get to their feet. After a pause, they turn to each other, silent for a moment.

The hell did this happen...?

Susie faces forward again. After a moment, she poses with a fist up, grinning.

... well, who cares! This means another adventure, right?

Susie gets in line behind Kris.

Let's see what else is around.

Control is returned to the player, who can head ahead to meet up with Ralsei.

Meeting up with Ralsei

Some distance into the next room, Kris and Susie stop when Ralsei calls out from offscreen.

W-wait for me!

Kris and Susie turn around, Susie alerted. The camera pans over to reveal Ralsei walking up from behind them.

Ralsei!

Susie walks up to Ralsei. He turns to the side facing the player, adjusting his glasses.

I felt a dark presence and hurried here...

He lowers his hand and turns back to Susie.

If we keep going, the Dark Fountain should be...

Susie steps closer to Ralsei.

Hey, Ralsei!

Susie poses, grinning.

You're not gonna BELIEVE what I did after our adventure yesterday!

She turns back to Ralsei.

After we saved the WORLD, I went over Kris's...

She turns to Kris…

Almost baked an ENTIRE pie...

…then to the side, posing again.

And then marathoned movies ALL NIGHT!

Ralsei laughs.

Aww, Susie! It sounds like you had a wonderful time!

Susie turns away from Ralsei and puts her hands behind her head casually, grinning.

Yeah, it wasn't AWFUL, I guess... I want to hear all about it...

Ralsei turns briefly to the side.

Um, let's just get going, first!

As he turns back to Susie, she faces him as well, dropping her arms.

Oh, uh, yeah!

Susie and Ralsei line up behind Kris, the camera panning over to focus on them. All three spin and strike poses. Control is returned to the player, who can head into the next area.

Ralsei's explanation

As the party continues down the boardwalk, Kris and Susie slow to a halt as Ralsei walks past them.

I...

He turns to the side, smile gone. A long pause. Then he turns to Susie and Kris, head bowed, as Susie's eyes widen.

I... don't think I could.

A long pause. Susie turns to the side facing away from the player, scratching her head.

Heh, yeah, I mean, it was...

Susie turns to Ralsei and puts her hands behind her head casually, grinning.

The Festival's kinda stupid, y'know? I mean, Obviously.

She takes a few steps forward…

Just... uh...

…and then another few.

I mean, we don't even have to play any games.

Susie takes another step forward.

We could... just make a mess. Cause some trouble.

A long pause. She drops her arms and advances a step towards Ralsei.

Just...

Another step forward.

I dunno, could be... fun if you...

Susie's gaze drops.

... just went, you know?

A pause. Ralsei turns away, head raised but still unsmiling.

No, Susie... I... I... want to, I just...

He turns to the side facing the player and the howling of the wind fades to silence. As he bows his head, looking upset, Susie's smile disappears.

I can't go.

A long pause.

... ... um, did I say something weird?

Ralsei turns to Susie, tipping his head up and smiling at her.

No! No, not at all, Susie!

He stops smiling.

I'm happy to be invited by you, Susie, I am... It's just...

A pause. Ralsei's head dips, as Susie watches him unhappily.

I'm a Darkner.

A pause. Ralsei briefly lifts his head, looking serious.

I can't go to the Light World.

He bows his head again.

Even if I... wanted to.

When Susie speaks, Ralsei lifts his head to look at her.

Uhh... why?

Ralsei bows his head sadly again.

Because I... Because we...

A long pause. Ralsei turns to the side facing the player, looking unhappy.

...

As Ralsei speaks, Susie's gaze drops.

Susie, it's... hard for me to explain this, but...

The screen fades slowly to black, then fades back in on a nondescript room, sparsely furnished with only a bed and a chair, and a window on the wall bracketed by a poster on one side and a shelf on the other. Slow, haunting music plays.

Ralsei You see, Susie. You and Kris live in the "Light World." Ralsei A place where everything is as you see.

With the sound of distorted laughter, the light in the room fades.

Ralsei That is... as long as there's light. Ralsei You see, when it gets dark...

The room grows darker still, and the poster and chair begin to distort, shapes seeming to bubble.

Ralsei Things become more indistinct. Ralsei A chair can look like a monster. Ralsei A poster can look like it's moving. Ralsei Your eyes can't see the truth anymore. Ralsei And when the light runs out...

Distorted laughter is heard again, and the room fades to complete black.

Ralsei You see nothing. Ralsei Of course, your mind can't make anything of nothing.

A long pause.

Ralsei But what if? Ralsei What if it became even darker?

A glowing Dark Fountain appears through the blackness, pulsing through various colors.

Ralsei Darker than dark. Ralsei What if we could take away the light that wasn't there Ralsei Until we reached another side? Ralsei If that were to happen...

The Fountain fades out of sight. Then the outline of a many-legged creature with pointed ears appears through the dark, where the chair in the room once was. As Ralsei speaks, accompanied by distorted laughter, the creature is joined by more visible things: the outline of the room behind itself; eyes with green sclera and black irises on the wall where the window was; an odd blinking slit where the shelf was; and a shifting amalgam of many hands where the poster was.

Ralsei You could start to see things again.

Below the blinking slit, where the bed formerly stood, an opening in the wall appears with a river flowing audibly through it.

Ralsei And hear them.

A face appears on the many-legged creature, made up of yellow and pink eyes and an unnervingly wide grin.

Ralsei And feel them.

The creature laughs and rapidly zooms towards the viewer, until the screen fades into the black of its body. Its laughter pitches lower and quieter, fading out. The screen then fades in on a section of the field in Card Kingdom, where two Rudinns are visible.

Ralsei That is the Dark World, Susie. Ralsei An alternate view of reality through the lens of shadow. Ralsei In other words...

From the center of the screen, a column of light blooms outwards until the entire screen is white, the same visual effect as when the SOUL seals a Dark Fountain. And sure enough, the white light fades to reveal the unused classroom in Hometown's school, with all the Light World equivalents of the Card Kingdom Darkners still scattered about.

Ralsei ... an illusion. Ralsei The Dark Fountains change everything into a fantasy.

Briefly fading to white, the scene transitions to the upstairs hallway of the Dreemurr residence. Lancer, Queen, Rudinn, and Hathy stand in it.

Lancer spins, then turns into the Ace of Spades card; the card falls to the hallway carpet.

Ralsei Lancer is just an average playing card.

Queen laughs, unheard; she too transforms into an open laptop and falls to the ground.

Ralsei Queen... is just a laptop.

Ralsei In other words... when the lights come back on... Ralsei This world is no longer "real". Ralsei The places you go, the people you meet... none of them are.

Ralsei Even I, the one telling you this... I too, am...

Susie's disagreement

The scene abruptly cuts back to the party standing in the newest Dark World. Susie has grabbed Ralsei's shoulder and is looking at him with concern, while Ralsei is wide-eyed and startled.

But Ralsei, how can you say you're not real!? I can see you... I can feel you... I can hear you... "Normal objects" don't have... feelings and stuff!

A long pause. Then Ralsei bows his head, wearing a sad smile.

That's right, Susie.

Ralsei pulls Susie's hand off his shoulder, then takes several steps back. Susie's gaze turns away, though she's still facing Ralsei.

They... shouldn't.

A pause.

That's why... I don't want you and Kris... ... to worry too much about us, OK?

Ralsei turns away from the others.

It's funny, I was... overjoyed to make friends.

He bows his head, looking downcast.

Then, selfishly, I... started feeling sad, too.

A sad smile appears on his face. Susie looks at him again, eyes slightly wide.

Sad I couldn't be your friend in real life, Susie. That I CAN'T do your homework. That I can't go with you. That I... can't be there to help you when you really need me.

Susie's gaze lowers, though she has a slight smile.

Ralsei... I'm sorry. That's why I... If, you know, me, Lancer... We can't do it all. So... if you ever feel like we're not enough...

A pause. Susie looks up with wide eyes again, no longer smiling, when Ralsei turns slowly to face her, his head ducked and smile gone. Achingly slow, he lifts his head and puts on a smile.

Just forget about us and make some real friends, okay?

Susie ducks her head, eyes cast in shadow. Her teeth are bared in a grimace and her hand, clenched in a fist at her side, trembles.

... Ralsei... you...

Susie charges forward and grabs Ralsei by the front of his scarf, hauling him up on his toes as she snarls in his face. Her hands are still shaking.

You idiot! How can you SAY that?! You think I could just FORGET you!? Forget Lancer!? Forget everyone!?

She gives Ralsei a shake.

I don't care what you say you are... You're real to me, OK!? ...

Ralsei looks away unhappily, not meeting her eyes. Her snarl softens.

Susie, I...

He smiles, faintly visible over his scarf. Susie also looks away, no longer snarling.

I'm happy to hear that, but...

Susie shakes Ralsei again, yelling in his face. Ralsei looks startled.

SHUT UP and listen to me!

Ralsei looks unhappy again, not meeting Susie's eyes as she bares her teeth.

No matter what you say... No matter what you are... Me... you... Kris... We're a TEAM, alright? None of us are gonna forget each other!

Susie's hand, still fisted in Ralsei's scarf, goes still.

So please...

Her snarl fades into a serious expression.

Just... ... just...

A long pause. Suddenly, a spotlight comes on focused on Susie and Ralsei, with the rest of the area cast into darkness. Susie and Ralsei are wide-eyed with surprise. Confetti blasts out from either side of the screen as a mechanical voice talks:

Tenna Wow, what an
                 performance!!! Tenna The audience has been brought to , folks!

A pause. Then Susie and Ralsei yell, looking furious.

Who the HECK are you!? Tenna Haha, I was hoping you'd ask! Tenna MIKE, PLAY THE VHS!!!

The camera pans up, past the source of the spotlight and into darkness.
