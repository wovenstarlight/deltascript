Introduction to the Bonus Round

The "technical difficulties" screen cuts briefly to black, then cuts to the TV Time set, where the party stands behind their podiums and Tenna stands to the side. Susie and Ralsei look shocked, while Tenna is pointing upwards and smiling like usual. The TV Time theme begins playing.

Tenna WELCOME BACK to another ELECTRIFYING episode of TV TIME!

The party members all turn to Tenna; Ralsei is wide-eyed while Susie looks unhappy.

W-wait a second...

The screen in the back blips as it displays a TV-themed cityscape. Tenna presses his hands together by his face, cooing. As Tenna speaks, Ralsei loses his surprise and looks uneasy.

Tenna Today's board will take place in beautiful TV CITY...

Susie grits her teeth, head lowering and casting a shadow over her eyes, as Tenna continues talking conspiratorially to the audience.

Tenna

The audience laughs. Tenna points upwards with a too-wide, strained smile and continues talking as Susie's fists begin to tremble at her side.

Tenna Collect two Q's from each corner of the CITY, and...

Susie stomps and looks furious, cutting off both Tenna and the music. Tenna stops rocking from side to side as she talks.

ENOUGH ALREADY! The game's over. Give back Toriel and let us go home. Now.

A pause.

Tenna Game... over?

A pause. Susie and Ralsei both duck their heads.

Mr. Tenna, I'm not certain what your plan is, but...

Ralsei lifts his head. A pause.

We'd like to go seal the Dark Fountain now.

He ducks his head again.

I think... the show's gotten a little old.

A long pause. Susie and Ralsei lift their heads when Tenna speaks.

Tenna ... Old.

Ralsei ducks his head.

Umm, sorry, Tenna, I...

The theme music starts back up as Tenna spreads his hands, grinning and once again rocking on his feet. Ralsei lifts his head.

Tenna No, haha! Ralsei, RALSEI! Buddy-BOY! It's fine! I GET IT! Look, I get it!

Tenna presses his hands together by his face.

Tenna CLASSIC TV... sometimes just takes a little TIME to APPRECIATE!

He smiles and points upwards.

Tenna So how about this?

He spreads his hands.

Tenna We'll all do ONE MORE LITTLE GAME...

Tenna straightens up, resting a hand on his tie.

Tenna One last ENCORE! Tenna And this time, we'll play

The music cuts off and Susie and Ralsei look startled as Tenna snarls ferociously. His next line plays slower than usual.

Tenna Until you LIKE IT.

Gachapon capsules appear and close around each of the party members, shocking them, and the set is cast in red lighting. The TV-themed cityscape on the back screen is replaced by a cityscape on fire.

H-hey, what the heck are you...

The music resumes, pitched up and strained, as fire rises where the audience normally stands and the red lighting begins pulsing as Tenna dances.

Tenna Tenna THE 'S never !!! Tenna And if YOU don't wanna play anymore... Tenna It's time for ME to TAKE CONTROL!!! Tenna MIKE, crank it all up to MAX!!!

Tenna continues dancing as Susie and Ralsei begin struggling to get out of their capsules, Susie pounding against the plastic wall while Ralsei pushes ineffectually at it. After a moment's pause, Tenna points at the viewer.

Tenna

The Bonus Round starts

Tenna points upwards as the screen in the background cuts to black and his TV Time avatar appears on it.

Tenna Now it's my turn to play!!!

He throws his hands in the air. On the screen in the back, a burning landscape flickers into view around his avatar. As his avatar begins walking, the pounding of Susie's fists against her capsule goes silent, replaced by ominous music.

Tenna Hey, isn't this fun!?

Tenna, wearing a black and white suit and hat, dances with a cane.

Tenna I'm not broken! I don't have burn-in! I'm... fun!!!

Once again wearing his normal outfit, he throws his hands in the air.

Tenna C'mon, say it!!! I'm!!! Fun!!!

A long pause. Tenna fixes his tie with a tight, aggrieved smile.

Tenna ... Fine. You don't wanna say it?

A quiz comes into view on the screen in the back, walking away from Tenna's avatar.

Tenna Maybe a quiz will help you answer!!!

Tenna's avatar picks up speed, and the quiz similarly begins running away from him, but can't outspeed him. Tenna's avatar catches up and a "QUIZ!" begins.

Tenna OUT OF TIME!!! WRONG!!!

He points upwards with a painfully wide grin as all the party members are hit by lightning, taking damage, and drop down in pain.

The quiz ends, cutting with a radial wipe to black and then to the TV Time set.

Minigame #1: Cooking Show

The screen radial wipes to black, then to the Cooking Show set. Kris stands on the counter in their server outfit under dimmed lighting. Susie and Ralsei are at their stations in the back, both still in their capsules and original outfits; while Susie remains frozen and despondent, Ralsei is struggling again. After a moment, Tenna slides in from screen right next to Kris, leaning towards them with his hands pressed together by his face.

Tenna Watching cartoons. Playing games.

Tenna begins rocking back and forth, laughing and flapping a hand.

Tenna Smiling. Laughing. Crying.

He freezes mid-laugh. Then he begins trembling.

A slight pause. Tenna bolts upright and back, reeling in shock.

Tenna What!? He went to COLLEGE?

Tenna straightens his tie, looking aggrieved and trembling.

Tenna Well... why'd he have to go do THAT!?

He points upwards, rocking back and forth as usual; his next line is spoken haltingly.

Tenna You. Can learn. ANYTHING! On TV!

He rocks back and forth in wild laughter again.

Tenna Ahahahah!!!

Tenna bolts offscreen. A long pause. Then he returns, adjusting his tie.

Tenna Yes. The instructions. Right. The INSTRUCTIONS!!!!

Two pipes appear on either side of the screen in front of the counter, arrows indicating them. Tenna points slowly between the pipes.

Tenna They're coming out of the pipes. Tenna They're crawling out of the pipes.

Tenna freezes, hand still outstretched.

Tenna Where are they going...!?

He begins laughing wildly again.

Tenna Hahaha... I... don't... KNOW!!

Still laughing, Tenna bolts offscreen.

Tenna Ahahahahahaha!!!

The screen in the back counts down — 3, 2, 1 — with black text on a stark white background. When the countdown ends, the minigame begins, fire falling from the top of the screen that Kris must dodge. Black silhouettes pass in front of the counter, coming out of the right pipe and into the left. The screen in the back acts as a timer, the white being overtaken by black.

Once the timer runs out, the minigame ends and the scene returns to the TV Time set with the same radial wipe animation as before.

Healing spot

Appears when any party member is below 50% HP after a quiz or a battle, and occasionally alters subsequent dialogue.

Standard

First time

A smiling Tenna stands straight, resting a hand over his tie, as a small green spring appears in his avatar's path; his avatar approaches and pauses beside the spring.

Tenna Wow! A healing spot! That way, everyone can keep playing!

Tenna's avatar walks over the spring; all party members are healed to maximum HP.

Subsequent times

A smiling Tenna stands straight, resting a hand over his tie, as a small green spring appears in his avatar's path.

Tenna Here comes a healing spring!

After quiz #2 or battle #2

Tenna turns away from the party members and the player, head hanging.

Tenna Oh... a healing spot...

Ending the Bonus Round

By using Zapper's {% include acticon chara="Susie" -%} {% include acticon chara="Ralsei" -%} OffButton ACT during a battle.

You hit the OFF button!

The screen turns off, music stopping. After a pause, the screen fades back in to show the encounter, but with the Zapper gone and the party members missing from inside their gachapon capsules. After a pause, Tenna grins painfully wide.

Tenna H-huh!?

Clutching his head and twisting in distress, Tenna approaches the capsules.

Tenna Susie...? Kris...? Hey...? HEY!!!

Increasingly frantic, Tenna moves faster.

Tenna DON'T TELL ME YOU DIDN'T LIKE YOUR CUSTOM MADE TOY BALLS!? Tenna Hey, Mike?

As Tenna addresses Mike, he slows down, then goes entirely still with his next line.

Tenna Could you, uh, cancel my 4am shock therapy session?

He leans forward, whispering conspiratorially.

Tenna Cause it looks like it's happening right now.

As an alarm plays, red light flashing slowly from the top of the screen, Tenna clutches his head in a panic.

Tenna ALL HANDS ON DECK!!! EVERYONE FIND THE LIGHTNERS!!!

Tenna leaves offscreen to the right, and the encounter — and Bonus Round — ends.
