Talking to Ramb in the S-Rank changing room

First time

Ramb stands in front of a set of double doors.

Ramb Ah, Kris. Look at you, a dressing room big shot! Cheers, cheers.

Ramb shifts to the side, looking conspiratorial.

Ramb So how do you fancy the game, then? Right-o. It ain't much, innit, luv. Ramb Now, I KNEW you wouldn't like it, I KNEW. So listen up... Ramb Y'see, that game you been playin'? It's not, EXACTLY, the original. Ramb Ol' Tenna's done a little somethin' here, a little somethin' there... Ramb And made the whole adventure some big ol' blasted line from A to B. Ramb Primo. Boring. Stuff. Ramb But if I know Kris, and boy do I know Kris... that's not what YOU want, innit? Ramb Freedom. That's what YOU want, chum, freedom! Ramb Well, if I can go hush-hush for a minute here... Ramb Kris, luv. THAT'S what we got backstage. Ramb The ORIGINAL game. Where YOU decide what to do. Ramb All right, enough explaining, more gaming! Ramb I'll keep an eye out for Tenna, you go have some REAL fun, alright, luv?

Ramb moves out of the way of the doors.

Ramb There you go! And, I'm sure you know, but... secret to Tenna, right, luv?

Subsequent times

Ramb Ah, Kris. Stick around here long enough, and you'll learn... Ramb Freedom's just a thing for big shots.

Starting up the game

Interacting with the console without Odd Controller

(Looks like a game console.) (Play it?)

The console turns on. The intro sequence of the original game plays, showing a shadowed logo that appears to be the word "MANTLE", but the sequence is abruptly cut short. Instead, the screen shows an error message reading "NO CONTROLLER". After a moment and a burst of static, the console shuts off.

Nothing happens.

Obtaining the Odd Controller

When trying to leave the backstage area, an object blocks Kris's way.

(...? Seems there's a controller fallen on the ground.) (ODDCONTROLLER was added to your KEY ITEMS.)

The Odd Controller is added to the inventory.

 (You didn't pick up the strangely familiar controller.)

The Controller remains in Kris's way, making it impossible to leave.

Interacting with the console with the Odd Controller

(Looks like a game console.) (Play it?)

The console turns on. The intro sequence of the original game plays, showing a shadowed logo that appears to be the word "MANTLE", but the sequence is abruptly cut short. Instead, the screen shows an error message reading "NO CONTROLLER".

This time, Kris kneels and plugs the Odd Controller into the console. The game screen begins flashing until, with a high-pitched noise, the SOUL appears on screen. With a series of softening beeps, the Desert Board is overlaid on the screen in steps, with HERO_SWORD appearing over the SOUL, and gameplay begins.

Nothing happens.

Gameplay

Interface

At the top left of the screen is a bar labelled HP.

After obtaining the sword, a level marker and progress bar appear at the top center of the screen, advancing through the stages LV1 → LV2 → LV3 → MAX (i.e. LV4). A small sword icon is added on the top right every time HERO_SWORD's LV goes up.

Pyramid shop

The eastern door in the pyramid which led to Tenna's shop in Legend of Tenna now lies empty.

After obtaining the sword, at LV1 and LV2, it displays the following text:

BECOME STRONGER

At LV3, it displays the following text:

BECAME STRONGER

At MAX LV, it contains the following text in solid black that blends in with the background and can be revealed by having HERO_SWORD walk behind it:

Shadow Mantle holder Having fun?

Southwest room with staircases

Entering any of the staircases leads to a dark room. Walking into the center will cause the hidden Shadow Mantle holder to fly away. The center of the room contains solid black text that blends in with the background and can be revealed by having HERO_SWORD walk behind it:

Shadow Mantle holder See you soon.

Finding the Ice Key

HERO_SWORD opens the chest. The following narration plays soundlessly.

YOU GOT THE ICE KEY

The Ice Key icon appears in the top right and an unsettling, drawn-out tune plays like a reward SFX. The game shuts off, and Kris can now walk away.

Talking to Ramb after obtaining the Ice Key

First time

Ramb Kris! How was it? Lots 'o fun? Ramb Maybe had a little extra taste of that... you know? Ramb ... Ramb Well, regardless, Tenna almost came in. Gotta be extra careful next time.

Subsequent times

Ramb Come again next time, Kris, if you fancy. Ramb All I wanna do is make sure you can play the games you want, Kris.

Meeting back up with Susie and Ralsei

Only plays after successfully completing this stage of the original game.

Ralsei and Susie are standing by the S-rank room door, facing Kris.

Umm, welcome back, Kris!

Susie scratches her head.

What the hell took you so long?

Susie's hand stops moving.

Really? Hey, maybe we should play too, next time. Th-that's okay, I'm not... really good at games.

Susie's hand stops moving.

Uh, okay.

Susie lowers her hand.

Anyway, let's hurry up and get the hell out of here.

The party is free to move on.
