Back in the Green Room

The party finds themselves once more in the Green Room, Susie and Ralsei shocked by the transition. They calm after a moment, but Susie isn't smiling. Ralsei also stops smiling after a brief moment, bowing his head.

I suppose... we should get ready for the next round.

A pause. Ralsei lifts his head, smiling again.

... I'm fine as long as you want to keep playing, Susie!

Susie lowers her head. A slight pause.

...

Susie lifts her head, still unhappy. A slight pause.

I mean, the last game was wicked fun, but... Get the feeling this'll keep going, until it gets old.

She lowers her head again.

... feels bad, but...

She lifts her head to look at Kris.

Let's go tell him this time's gonna be the last, okay, Kris?

The camera pans to focus on Kris. As Susie regains her smile, control is returned to the player. You can explore for a while; notable interactions are listed below:

  • Green Room
    • talking to Ramb
    • talking to Rouxls and the weather duo
    • talking to Lancer
    • (see the Green Room page for other interactions)
  • Red carpet hall to the south
    • talking to Zapper
    • talking to Pippins
    • talking to Shuttah
    • (see the red carpet hall page for other interactions)
  • S-Rank changing room
    • playing the original Island Board backstage
    • talking to Ramb before and after completing the original Island Board
    • checking the vending machine
    • talking to Pippins
    • checking the right corkboard
    • talking to Rabbick
    • talking to someone behind the curtain

At any point, you can move on to the left, where you'll find the interstitial hall and Legend of Tenna set.

Talking to Tenna as a group

The dark screen fades to show the Legend of Tenna set, with Tenna standing to the left, one hand pressed to the side of his head as though on call.

The party enters from the right, led by Susie.

Hey, Tenna...

Waves periodically radiate off Tenna's antennae.

Tenna What do you MEAN!? Then MAKE. The AIRHOLES. BIGGER!

Tenna leans forward, gesturing in annoyance with his free hand.

Tenna What do you think I'M PAYING your ELECTRICITY BILL FOR!?

Tenna leans back and throws his hands in the air, frustrated.

Tenna And HOW are they not HERE YET!? The LIGHTNERS are gonna get BORED!

Susie's eyes widen in surprise and she stops smiling as Tenna continues speaking:

Tenna If that purple girl finds out about this, it's ALL OVER!

A pause. Susie speaks, and Tenna's antennae stop radiating waves as his call ends.

... what are you talking about?

Tenna jumps in shock, whirling around. He raises his hands, flustered.

Tenna S-Susie!? Kris!? Uh, Susie, I...

He hurries towards the party, pressing his hands together by his face and leaning forward, trembling violently.

Tenna Susie, sweetHEART! Hey, what's the trouble?

A pause. Susie looks down, smile still absent. Another pause. When she speaks, Tenna freezes.

... what was with that phone call?

Tenna stands straight, adjusting his tie with a flustered smile.

Tenna Oh, THAT! Oh, NO I can't believe you, uh, HEARD!

Tenna dramatically throws his hands in the air, bouncing slightly on his feet.

Tenna We were, uh, just planning a SPECIAL SURPRISE GAME for you...

He straightens up and presses his hands together in front of himself.

Tenna You, uh, didn't hear what it WAS, did you?

Susie raises her head, smiling again.

... nah. Uh...

Susie turns around and takes a step away; Tenna looks agitated.

... I gotta, go, actually.

Tenna's head hangs as Susie walks away, her gaze lowered and her smile gone; Kris and Ralsei back out of her way as she passes, then follow her out. The screen fades to black, then to the interstitial hall as the party walks through; Ralsei is no longer smiling. Susie leads the others to stop in front of the doors. A pause.

... ... man.

Susie turns to the side away from the player.

There's nothing that pisses me off more...

Susie turns to the left, gaze still lowered. As she speaks, Ralsei ducks his head into his scarf.

... than people who don't tell you the whole deal.

Ralsei lifts his head.

... You mean, Tenna...?

Susie lifts her head as well, looking serious.

Yeah. I dunno what, but... he's hiding something. ... Let's go take a look around, Kris.

Susie and Ralsei regain their smiles, and control is returned to the player, who can explore the Green Room some more, head back towards the left to talk to Tenna again, or go looking for what Tenna's hiding behind the Green Room's eastern doors.

Talking to Tenna as a group

The dark screen fades to show the Legend of Tenna set, with Tenna standing at the center absolutely still, facing the side away from the player, head hanging low and hands limp at his sides.

Kris enters from the right, but Tenna doesn't notice.

Tenna Keep it TOGETHER, Tenna! They... they're STILL loving it! They have to be!

Still turned away, Tenna flaps his hands exaggeratedly, throwing them in the air in dramatic upset.

Tenna CLASSIC TV NEVER GETS OLD!!! And Susie! Susie, she was so excited...

Tenna turns to the left, head and antennae still hanging low. He lets out a deep sigh. Then he turns to the right and is startled to see Kris, flailing and spinning for a moment before standing up straight and adjusting his tie with a wobbly smile. A silent pause.

Tenna K... Kris! Hey, Kris! Kris ol' buddy, ol' pal! How's it going!

Tenna presses his hands together in front of himself, trembling faintly.

Tenna It's uh, it's uh, it's goin' great, isn't it!? Everything's, just hunky doody!

Tenna takes a step back and shades the top of his face with one hand as his antennae stand up perfectly straight.

Tenna Haha... Uh, heheh... Where are your, uh, buddies? Your pals?

He takes another step back and lowers the hand to rest on his tie, other hand behind his back.

Tenna By yourself, huh? Don't you wanna, y'know, heheh, keep 'em company? Heheh...

He edges another step or two back, still trembling. Then he turns around sharply. The trembling stops as he presses a finger to the side of his head, holding up his other hand as well.

Tenna Oh, uh, hey, would you look at that! My, uh, my beeper's going!

Tenna spreads his hands with a wide grin.

Tenna You know, my silent beeper! Haha, it's a new one! I... I gotta be goin' now!

He turns to Kris and bows twice, then turns and starts heading to the left. A few steps away from the edge of the screen, he stops. A pause. Tenna lowers his head. A slight pause.

Tenna By the way... Seen your father recently? Your brother?

He lifts his head, mouth absent.

Tenna You know, we still got that cowboy program... The one your dad likes...

Tenna pirouettes back to center stage before pressing his hands together fondly by his face and leaning towards Kris.

Tenna He was, heh, bouncin' you on your knee as you watched it together...

Tenna freezes and looks alarmed for a moment. He turns away, putting his hands over his face.

Tenna Sorry, Kris! Sorry! I gotta...

Tenna turns to Kris while adjusting his tie, flustered.

Tenna I have to go!

Putting his hands over his face again, he rushes offscreen to the left. Control is returned to the player, who can explore the Green Room some more or go looking for what Tenna's hiding behind the Green Room's eastern doors.
