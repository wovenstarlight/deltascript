Talking to Ramb backstage

Dialogue for before as well as after the Sword Route ends. The doors to the backstage area are only open if the first two stages of the Sword Route were completed.

First time

A half-petrified Ramb stands to the side, blocking the way to the original game's console, awash in light coming from its room.

Ramb Kris... Kris, that's you, isn't it, luv? Ramb Good show, good show... Ramb Tenna's been up to this and that, that and this... All sorts of boring stuff. Ramb But Kris! Oh, Kris! You and I know... The REAL drama happens BACKSTAGE! Ramb That's why... I got it ready for you, y'know. Ramb One last game. Ramb And I'll keep watch. Ramb Well, just as long as... haha. Just as long as I can. Ramb Now, Kris, I hate to trouble you, I really do, but... Ramb Y'see, I can't really move myself now. Ramb If you could just shove me out of the way... Ramb Yes, that's right! Just like that!

Kris carefully shifts Ramb out of the way.

Ramb Kris! Ramb ... enjoy the games, eh, luv?

Kris can now go to the game console.

Ramb Heh heh heh... some REAL fun, just like the old days. Ramb ... Ramb That's been my purpose, y'know. Letting you play your games. Ramb But, heh, now that the games are almost over... Ramb Makes you think, was it really all such a good idea? Ramb Your mother. Your poor mother, what will happen if she wakes up...? Ramb ... ah, what am I doing? A power strip, giving YOU a lecture... Ramb ... just play your games, luv.

Subsequent times

Ramb Kris... if not for fun, why'd you do it? Ramb ... mum's the word, innit. Fair play, fair play.

Starting up the game

(Looks like a game console.) (Play it?)

The console turns on. The intro sequence of the original game plays, showing a shadowed logo that appears to be the word "MANTLE", but the sequence is abruptly cut short. Instead, the screen shows an error message reading NO CONTROLLER.

Kris kneels and plugs the Odd Controller into the console. The game screen begins flashing until, with a high-pitched noise, the SOUL appears on screen. With a series of softening beeps, the City Board is overlaid on the screen in steps, with HERO_SWORD (as well as HERO_AXE and HERO_SCARF hidden behind them) appearing over the SOUL, and gameplay begins.

Nothing happens.

Gameplay

Interface

At the top left of the screen is a bar labelled HP. The Shelter Key icon is present at the top center-right.

After obtaining the sword, a level marker and progress bar appear at the top center of the screen, advancing through the stages LV1 → LV2 → LV3 → MAX (i.e. LV4). A small sword icon is added on the top right every time HERO_SWORD's LV goes up.

There are no enemies available on the City Board. Unlike in other stages, HERO_SWORD must attack HERO_AXE and HERO_SCARF to reach LV3; a pale Kris flinches and looks away each time one of them is killed. After this, they can access the Manhole Dungeon, where they will be able to reach MAX LV.

Tenna blocks the path ahead, and must be killed to proceed.

Using the Shelter Key

Ahead, the scenery abruptly turns into that of the path to the Hometown shelter. As the player directs HERO_SWORD to approach it, Kris will independently override the controls multiple times to try and stop them from getting to the shelter doors, but their efforts are in vain.

Upon interacting with the shelter doors, the Shelter Key icon disappears from the top right and the following messages play:

USED THE SHELTER KEY.

The doors swing open slowly. Inside the shelter is a long hallway leading to the area where the Shadow Mantle holder is battled.

Obtaining the Shadow Mantle

Following the path opened after the Mantle holder's defeat takes HERO_SWORD to a corridor with a treasure chest sitting on a higher level. Interacting with it gives the following:

A TREASURE CHEST. IT'S TOO HIGH UP TO REACH.

HERO_SWORD cannot exit back into the previous room. The only way out is the path leading to the right, which lines up with a channel on the wall. Rather than being taken to another area within the game, HERO_SWORD exits the game screen and comes out into the room with Kris, who watches them exit the game. When they stand on the same floor as them, Kris drops their controller and backs away. They quickly back up further if HERO_SWORD approaches, and if HERO_SWORD attacks with their sword, Kris will collapse.

After a while, if the player has not already directed HERO_SWORD to exit the door to the right, they will walk down and out of sight.

Once HERO_SWORD is gone, Kris stands back up (if they collapsed) and the player regains control of them. Kris can now walk directly into the original game; they are tall enough to reach the treasure chest.

(ShadowMantle was added to your ARMORs.)

After Kris exits the game screen, they walk back over to the console to pick up the controller, but are interrupted from acting further by Susie's voice.

Hey Kris!

Susie walks into the room and comes to stand beside Kris, looking at the screen with them.

Standard conversation

At this point, the player regains control of HERO_SWORD and can make them reenter the room to Susie's right. At any point during the below conversation, they can approach Susie (which will make Kris watch them instead of Susie) and even attack her (which will trigger a cutscene that interrupts any ongoing dialogue).

What the hell are you doing? Being a tryhard all by yourself?

A pause. Kris and Susie look at each other.

You seriously enjoy this?

Kris's head hangs down. A pause. Susie grins.

... If you get all sweaty playing games alone... You're gonna end up like Berdly, right?

A pause.

If Chapter 2's Weird Route was completed

Susie stops smiling.

... Hey, uh, it's just a joke. Chill.

Otherwise

... Okay, enough bird noises. I get it.

From this point on, HERO_SWORD can no longer be made to interrupt the conversation by attacking Susie, and will leave offscreen to the right if they were made to reenter.

After a brief moment, Susie stops smiling.

... Okay, then here's an idea.

Susie walks towards Kris, who backs away. She stops in front of the console, kneels, and unplugs their controller. (If HERO_SWORD was made to reenter the room, they are destroyed when the controller is unplugged.) Susie stands back up, controller in hand, then demonstratively drops it on the ground.

Don't play it.

Kris turns back towards the screen, though they keep looking at Susie.

If HERO_SWORD is made to attack Susie

Kris, who was watching HERO_SWORD, reacts as soon as they draw their sword and grabs Susie by the collar, yanking her well out of range. They skid some distance with the force of the pull, and Kris is visibly breathing fast for a few seconds before they slowly calm down. Susie also gets over her surprise and stares in confusion at Kris. While they both calm down, control over HERO_SWORD is lost and they leave the room.

K-Kris...? What are you... ...

Susie straightens up slightly.

Alright, I... I get it. Well, if... if you really want to.

After a pause, Susie ducks her head and jams it against Kris's face so that they're lost in her hair.

Go ahead.

Susie looks away from Kris, eyes still wide.

I tasted your shampoo in your bathroom, here, you can taste mine.

A 2-second pause. Abruptly, Susie yanks her head back, blushing.

WHAT? HOW AM I THE WEIRD ONE HERE!?

Susie pulls away, unsmiling.

Jeez.

Susie quickly walks away, stopping after a few steps.

Anyway. Here. Stole you a drink. Now stop making us wait already.

Susie puts down a can of Flat Soda and leaves the room. Control is returned to Kris, who can follow her out.

Meeting back up with Susie and Ralsei

Only plays after successfully completing this stage of the original game.

Out in the Green Room, Susie is waiting by the S-Rank door, while Ralsei sits on the closest couch playing a game. As Kris comes outside, Susie turns to them.

Took you long enough.

A moment later, Ralsei speaks, drawing Susie's attention.

Susie! Susie, look! I made it to Level 2! Level 2, huh?

Susie turns to Kris. As she speaks, Ralsei's hands freeze on his controller.

Kris, you're the tryhard. Whaddya you think?

Ralsei looks stunned. A brief pause.

... K... Kris?

No longer smiling, Ralsei jumps off the couch and joins Kris and Susie in front of the door; they both look at him.

I'm sorry, Kris! I... I forgot all about you! I got so into playing games with Susie, I... Heh, you should have seen it, Kris!

Susie laughs as she speaks.

He was actually RAGING at the falling arrow part.

Ralsei turns around, head ducked as he adjusts his glasses.

I suppose I was, not very chuffed, by that dart.

He lowers his hand, blushing.

But I... I had fun.

A pause. Ralsei startles, turning back around.

... s-sorry! Did you want to play, Kris?

Susie glances towards the doors to the Cold Place.

Dude, we need to seal the fountain.

Ralsei turns away.

Oh! Right! I...

He hangs his head.

How did I... forget about that...?

Ralsei takes his place next to Susie, forming a line with Kris at the head. Susie turns to Kris.

Let's go.

The party is free to move on.
