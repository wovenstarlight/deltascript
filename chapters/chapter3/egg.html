Getting caught by the Zapper

To the far southeast of the Green Room, in the boardwalks area, is a Stealth Zone with a lone spotlight (present only if Zappers have not been lost). If the party is caught by this spotlight, a Zapper jumps up onto the boardwalk to their right, with the party turning to face it.

Zapper You's a cheater, ain't ya. Zapper Now, honesty, that'll take you somewhere. Zapper Where you headed? Zapper The entrance...? That can be arranged.

The screen turns off, then fades back in to show the party in the previous area, as if they just walked out of the Zapper's area.

Unused variant

Seems to have been intended to go with a puzzle blocking the way back to the entrance of this area.

Zapper The entrance...? Well, it's blocked by a puzzle. Solve it and head left. Zapper That can be arranged.

The screen turns off, then fades back in to show the party to the left of the spotlight, facing it as though about to walk past it.

Available only if the Trip Ticket has not already been obtained from the Bibliox. (Remains available if the TripTicket was obtained from under the Ball Machine.)

Zapper You's was already heading that way, weren't ya, Kris?

The screen turns off, then fades back in to "nowhere".

 Zapper Then we's got no business!

The Zapper jumps back down off the boardwalk and the spotlight disappears. If the area is left and reentered, it will reappear.

Going nowhere

In a backstage area, Zapper stands to the left while Kris, alone, stands to the right.

The first area to the east has a puzzle screen; normally, when solved, this opens the path to another area further east. If the Shadow Mantle has been obtained, however, all screens are disabled and this path is already opened.

In the far east area is another screen with a Bibliox in front of it.

Finding the ticket under the Ball Machine

If the Shadow Mantle has been obtained and the screens across TV World have turned off, Bibliox will direct you to check under the Ball Machine for the Trip Ticket.

After putting 1225 points into the Ball Machine and leaving the resulting room, the player is returned to the B-Rank rewards room. The room is now almost entirely empty, save for something sparkling on the floor that can be interacted with:

(You thought you saw something on the ground... but you must've already had it.)

The Trip Ticket is added to the inventory.

Red X

After obtaining the Trip Ticket, entering an encounter with Ribbick and opening the ACT menu shows that one of the ACTs is labelled "{% include acticon chara="Ralsei" -%} CroakOnX". Moving the selection cursor to this ACT and pressing [Left] will result in the SOUL cursor gaining legs and walking off to the left until it can no longer be seen.
