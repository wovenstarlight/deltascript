Talking to Ramb in the S-Rank changing room

If you completed the original game's Desert Board

First time

Ramb once again stands in front of the doors leading backstage.

Ramb I'm glad you're having REAL fun, Kris. It's the least I could do. Ramb Not often a Dark World pops up in your own house, you know? Ramb ... though, it could. If you ever wanted it to.

Ramb moves out of the way of the doors.

Subsequent times

Ramb Sorry, Kris. Don't know what I was saying there. Ramb Just go and enjoy the games, eh? Cheers.

If you didn't play the original game's Desert Board

Dialogue is identical to speaking to Ramb after Round 1.

Starting up the game

If the Odd Controller has not been obtained

Dialogue for trying to play the game in vain and obtaining the Odd Controller is identical to doing so after Round 1.

Interacting with the console with the Odd Controller

(Looks like a game console.) (Play it?)

The console turns on. The intro sequence of the original game plays, showing a shadowed logo that appears to be the word "MANTLE", but the sequence is abruptly cut short. Instead, the screen shows an error message reading "NO CONTROLLER".

Kris kneels and plugs the Odd Controller into the console. The game screen begins flashing until, with a high-pitched noise, the SOUL appears on screen. With a series of softening beeps, the Island Board is overlaid on the screen in steps, with HERO_SWORD appearing over the SOUL, and gameplay begins.

Nothing happens.

Gameplay

Interface

At the top left of the screen is a bar labelled HP. If the original game's Desert Board was completed, then the Ice Key icon is present at the top center-right.

After obtaining the sword, a level marker and progress bar appear at the top center of the screen, advancing through the stages LV1 → LV2 → LV3 → MAX (i.e. LV4). A small sword icon is added on the top right every time HERO_SWORD's LV goes up.

Overhearing Tenna

On the Tropic of Love, the tree that triggers the music has been replaced by stairs. Entering them leads to a dark room with another door at the top of the screen. Past this door, Tenna can be overseen on a path.

Tenna PHEW! FINALLY, SOME PEACE AND QUIET! Tenna ... I WONDER IF THEY EVEN LIKE THE GAME...? Tenna I'M TRYING MY BEST TO MAKE IT FUN, BUT... Tenna HERE AND THERE, STRANGE THINGS ARE SHOWING THROUGH... Tenna OH, WHY DID I BASE IT OFF THIS STUPID OLD THING!! Tenna ... KRIS, OH, KRIS IF YOU COULD JUST SMILE... LAUGH... Tenna TELL ME I'M DOING SOMETHING RIGHT... PLEASE...

Tenna falls silent. Moving HERO_SWORD at all prompts the following messages:

Tenna WHAT THE? I HEAR FOOTSTEPS! Tenna NO GOOD I NEED TO MAKE MY GETAWAY

Tenna flees.

Cave message

After reaching LV2 and riding the eastern raft up the waterfall, following any of the accessible staircases or doors leads to a room with a message, depending on whether the Ice Key was obtained.

If the Ice Key was obtained

White text on a deep blue background.

GO TO NORTHERN LIGHT

If the Ice Key was not obtained

Deep blue text on a sandy background.

THE KEY WAS FORGOTTEN

Reaching the Ice Palace

If the Ice Key was obtained

HERO_SWORD stands before the door. The following narration plays soundlessly.

UNLOCKED WITH THE ICE KEY

The door opens and HERO_SWORD can enter the palace.

If the Ice Key was not obtained

The screen turns a solid blue and displays the following:

AREN'T YOU FORGETTING SOMETHING IMPORTANT?

After a moment, the game turns off.

Northwestern room

A room to the northwest in the Ice Palace displays text when first entered. This text does not reappear on subsequent entries into the room.

FOLLOW THE WHITE CLOAK

Eastern room

A room to the east in the Ice Palace displays text when first entered. This text does not reappear on subsequent entries into the room.

THE FORBIDDEN PATH BEGAN WITH ICE MAGIC

Locked door

Upon reaching the door with the White Cloak, HERO_SWORD can interact with it.

THE DOOR IS LOCKED THE ICE KEY WAS USED UP

With an ominous sound, the Ice Key icon on the top right disappears.

SHE WAS USED UP

With an ominous sound, the White Cloak disappears. The door opens and HERO_SWORD can enter the dark area beyond it.

Dark area

Upon walking some distance into the dark area, the Shadow Mantle holder speaks.

Shadow Mantle holder Well done. Shadow Mantle holder You found my secret hiding place. Shadow Mantle holder But there's nothing here... Shadow Mantle holder ...is there?

Exploring further reveals a shining star similar to SAVE points. Interacting with it leads to the following narration, played soundlessly:

YOU GOT THE SHELTER KEY

The Shelter Key appears in the top right.

Shadow Mantle holder Oh, you found that, too! Shadow Mantle holder Perfect, just perfect... Shadow Mantle holder Thanks for all your hard work, Kris!

The music stops. The following narration plays soundlessly.

YOU WERE USED UP.

The screen cuts to static for a moment, then turns off. Kris looks to the right, towards the room where NPCs' controllers lie waiting, then kneels and unplugs the controller; they are now free to walk away.

Talking to Ramb after playing

If you obtained the Shelter Key

First time

Ramb Kris! How were the games? Just to your liking? Ramb ... What? ... someone backstage with you? Ramb No, Kris. I was back here on security the whole time. Ramb Tenna's really starting to stick that nose of his around, though. Ramb Sorry, but I think we're gonna have to make the next one the last one.

Subsequent times

Ramb Kris, you sure you're feeling... normal? Ramb ... Well, you are talking to a power strip. So maybe not. Haha!

If you weren't able to obtain the Shelter Key

Ramb How was the game, luv...? Ramb ... what? You couldn't finish it? Ramb Blast it. Maybe you missed something from the first stage...?

If you didn't get S-rank in Round 1

Ramb If only you'd gotten S-Rank earlier...

Meeting back up with Susie and Ralsei

Only plays after successfully completing this stage of the original game.

Susie is standing by the S-rank room door, facing Kris.

Hey, Kris, you're back!

Susie turns to the left side of the room.

... dude, Ralsei, Kris is back! H... huh? O-Oops...

Ralsei hurriedly walks over from the left side of the room.

Sorry, I got wrapped up in playing an RPG... Yeah, just walking around and not fighting anything.

As Susie speaks, Ralsei looks embarrassed. Once she's done talking, he walks closer to her and Kris; Susie and Ralsei both turn to face Kris.

S-sorry! We can get going now.

The party is free to move on.
