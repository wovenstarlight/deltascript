Before entering Ralsei's room

Kris, Susie, and Ralsei approach the tape blocking off the green door.

Hey, what's that room over there? Your room? Err... Sorry, it's still really dusty and dirty, s-so...!

Susie turns to Ralsei.

... So what?

Ralsei turns to Susie.

So you can't, er, go inside. Until I clean it.

A pause. Then Susie turns to the door.

C'mon, what are you hiding that's that embarassing!?

Susie bolts through the tape, ripping it apart, and into Ralsei's room while he reels in shock.

Last one there's a fresh egg!

Ralsei runs after Susie.

S-Susie, w... wait!

Kris is now free to move, but trying to leave 3F or enter one of the other rooms gives the following narration:

(Your friends are still in Ralsei's room...)

They can only follow the others into Ralsei's room.

Entering the room

Susie stands at the entrance, staring at the empty room. Ralsei and Kris walk up behind her.

It's...

Susie walks to the side, looking around.

It's... empty...? ...

Ralsei crosses the room to the window as Kris and Susie watch.

My own room? ... I... I don't need a room! ... ...at least... that's what I thought. So... So I never...

Kris and Susie approach Ralsei.

... You... you LIVE here. Why do WE have rooms and not you? Making you two happy... Is... my purpose. I... If I think about it... There's no reason... For me... to have... anything.

Susie steps up beside Ralsei.

That's the stupidest goddamn thing I ever heard! You see this stupid excuse for a room? I don't care what the reason is. Me and Kris are gonna fill it up. Wall to wall. Until you have the most badass room in the castle.

Ralsei turns towards Susie.

Susie...

Susie turns away and scratches her head.

Look. You can't be the only one with an empty room. It's...

Susie's hand goes still.

It's too sad, y'know?

Susie, Ralsei, and Kris face each other.

Wherever we go, we'll keep an eye out for furniture, Right, Kris? C'mon.

Susie leaves the room.

...

Ralsei walks up to Kris.

Kris, Susie is so nice... It... makes my heart hurt.

A pause. Ralsei turns away.

I don't really have any hobbies, or interests. Baking, sewing, singing... those are all just... Things I thought to do... for you two. ... But recently...

Ralsei slowly turns around, facing away from the player.

I'm starting to feel like... Like I'm developing my own opinions. My own likes. My own dislikes. My own desires. My own... fears.

A pause. Ralsei slowly turns to Kris.

... please... tell me... Should I... Should a... Darkner... be feeling like this?

Ralsei turns away, blushing.

... I...

He turns back to Kris.

I... K... Kris, hearing that from you, I might just... Umm, d-did you say "of course?" You covered up your mouth yawning, there... Ah, you're nodding! That means, um, yes, doesn't it? ...

Ralsei turns away from Kris.

I'm sorry, Kris, I... I was being...

Ralsei looks at the door, startled, as Susie comes in pushing either the ICE-E statue or a trashcan ahead of her.

Alright, furniture piece number one! Th... thanks, Susie! Let's go, Kris. (Can... you please take this back?)
