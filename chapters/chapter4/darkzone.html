Finding the notes to play

Available on either side of the Northwest Library. The order in which you get the halves doesn't matter; dialogue only depends on which half was discovered first. These cutscenes only play once upon the initial discovery of each half. The two prophecy panels containing the notes are accompanied by the following captions:

A CONUNDRUM'S FIRST FOUR TONES. A CONUNDRUM'S LAST FOUR TONES.

First set of notes to be discovered

Once the party gets far enough into the room, the camera follows them as they walk forward and fan out to view the floating prophecy panel. A pause.

Looks kinda like... notes? Kris, maybe you can play 'em on a piano somewhere? Good idea, Susie! But, doesn't the song look, um... ... a little unfinished? ... huh? Yeah, guess we gotta find the other half? It's probably close. Let's go.

After a slight pause, the camera pans back to focus on the party as they turn away from the prophecy to face each other, and control is returned to the player.

Second set of notes to be discovered

Once the party gets far enough into the room, the camera follows them as they walk forward and fan out to view the floating prophecy panel.

Oh, Kris! It's the second half of the melody! Now, you just need to play it... Yeah, unless there's, like, a third half. (Um... there isn't.)

After a slight pause, the camera pans back to focus on the party as they turn away from the prophecy to face each other, and control is returned to the player.

Playing the piano incorrectly

As in, playing any sequence of notes that's not the correct answer to this puzzle, then exiting the piano screen.

Before finding either half of the notes

First time

Hmm... I wonder if we need to play something specific? Perhaps there's a hint somewhere... ... wait, it's a puzzle? Thought Kris was just jamming.

Subsequent times

Dude, what if you just smashed all the keys? That'll probably work, right?

After finding one half of the notes, but not the other

(Which half you have is irrelevant.)

... Looks like the music is incomplete. Maybe we need another hint...? Or, maybe you just gotta play something cooler.

Playing the piano correctly

Once the correct notes are played, the barred gate to the right lifts up and disappears with a chime.

If you didn't find either half of the notes

Ralsei turns his head slightly to the right, likely looking at Kris.

Amazing, Kris! You already knew the answer...

He faces forward again.

... You must really... know a lot of things, don't you.

If you found one half of the notes, but not the other

Ralsei turns his head slightly to the right, likely looking at Kris.

Amazing, Kris! You solved it without the second hint...

He faces forward again.

... You must really... be an expert on the prophecy.

If you found both halves of the notes

Susie puts her hands on her hips.

Nice, Kris!

She gestures with a hand as Kris and Ralsei turn to her.

All we had to do was follow the prophecy thing.

A pause. Susie puts her hand back on her hip.

If it's that easy, maybe we should just do whatever it says?

Kris and Susie turn to Ralsei, who takes a step back, his smile dropping.

How about it, Ralsei. Wanna memorize it for us?

A slight pause. Ralsei steps forward, retaking his original position, and smiles again. Susie, however, stops smiling.

... umm, that's okay, Susie. I already know it.

Susie smiles again.

Oh, okay, sweet.

Into the darkness

The party walks along the tops of bookshelves; the area quickly grows dark enough that only their silhouettes can be seen. Some distance into the new area, the party comes to a halt, Susie turning around to face Ralsei.

... hey, Ralsei. If you know the whole prophecy already...

Kris turns around to face the other two.

... why didn't you just say how to do the piano?

Ralsei takes a step backwards.

H-huh...? Well, I just thought...

Ralsei turns to the side, facing away from the player.

I just thought, isn't it better if I don't... say anything?

A long pause.

...

Susie steps forward, coming up right in front of Ralsei as he turns to face her.

Why?

A long pause. Ralsei ducks his head.

Um...

A pause. Then Ralsei hurries past Susie and Kris; they turn to watch him as he passes. He stops a step ahead of Kris and turns away from the player, seeming to look at something on the wall behind them.

H-Hey, I think I found a light!

He takes a step forward and flips a switch. But when the scene is abruptly cast in light, the party is standing well past the end of the bookshelf path, hanging in open air. Susie and Ralsei jump in shock.

Wh-whoops!!

Ralsei, Susie, and Kris plummet into the darkness one by one, and the scene fades to black. There's a cry from Susie and a splat SFX.

After a long pause, the scene fades back in to reveal the party fallen onto another row of bookshelves; Susie is kneeling, Kris sitting hunched over, and Ralsei sprawled facedown on the "ground". The area is dark, with circles around each party member being the only illuminated spots. Ralsei twitches.

O... ow...

He twitches again, then gets to his feet, shortly followed by Susie; neither of them are smiling. They both turn to Kris, and Susie notices something. A pause. She approaches them and kneels, lifting a hand in preparation to cast on them.

Here, lemme...

Ralsei takes a step forward, then heals all three of them to maximum HP at once, regaining his smile. Susie, though, is wide-eyed and frozen. After a pause, she gets to her feet alongside Kris, still unsmiling. They both face Ralsei.

Everyone feeling better?

Susie's gaze lowers.

... uh, yeah. Thanks.

Ralsei gets in line behind Susie, and control is returned to the player, who must lead the party further through the darkness.

Entering the Dark Zone

As the party proceeds along the shelves, someone unseen speaks at various intervals.

Jackenstein ... LAIGHT... Jackenstein ... WHO DAIRE BRING LAIGHT HEAR...
Jackenstein ... GO... BACK... Jackenstein ... YOU SUDN'T COME HERE...
Jackenstein ... THIS ZOAN... IS FORBIDEN... Jackenstein ... CONTINUE AND YOU MAY FIND YOURSELF SPOOCKED IN THE DARK...

The circles of light around each party member gradually grow smaller as they proceed. Eventually, the party stops walking, and Susie and Ralsei's eyes widen as the background fades into complete blackness and the camera pans over to the right.

Jackenstein ... VERY WELL... IF YOU COME CLOASER TO THIS PLACE... Jackenstein ... THEN YOU MAY FIND... Jackenstein ... THE DARK...

Everything fades to black for a brief moment; then Susie and Ralsei's eyes become visible again in a cartoonish manner, and to the right, the glowing orange eyes of the hidden speaker become visible.

Jackenstein ... HAS COME TO YOU!!!

The hidden figure draws the party into an encounter.

After the fight

Jackenstein's accident

When the light from the last use of Unleash fades, the silhouetted form of the large, bulky enemy — Jackenstein — can be seen.

Jackenstein THE LAIGHT... NOT THE LAIGHT!!!!

Jackenstein's eyes begin flashing white.

Jackenstein NOOOOOOO!!!

His eyes radiate more intense light, flooding the screen with white, which slowly fades to reveal Jackenstein and the party standing amongst the bookshelves. Susie and Ralsei look alarmed, while Kris is impassive as always. To the right, Jackenstein is clutching his head, his head and hands shaking. After a moment, he puts his hands over his face, going still.

Jackenstein N... NO... DOAN'T LOOK... I'M... I'M...

He clutches his head and trembles again.

Jackenstein I'M TOO SCAIREY!!!

After a slight pause, Jackenstein turns around, hands covering his face again, and flees. The screen shakes as a loud crash sounds from offscreen.

Are you all right? The chandelier... It...

Kris hurries forward, a serious-looking Susie and Ralsei following, and they approach Jackenstein. Beside his head, a broken chandelier swings violently, and there is shattered glass at his feet.

Jackenstein OUCHI... OHHH, IT HOARTS... IT HOARTS... Jackenstein EVERYTHING... SO BRAIGHT... CULDN'T SEE... Jackenstein HEAD HIT SHANDALIERE... We're sorry, we were just looking for a way...

Jackenstein turns around, clutching his head and trembling again. Ralsei smiles when he speaks.

Jackenstein OH... MY HEAD... IT HOARTS... IT HOARTS INSIDE Um, don't worry! We know how to use healing, so...

Ralsei hurries up to Jackenstein and casts his healing spell. The orange face of his head briefly flashes white, surrounded by green sparkles. Jackenstein stops moving.

Jackenstein HMM...

A very long pause. Jackenstein suddenly throws his head back and wails harder than before, whole body shaking with the force of it; Ralsei and Susie look shocked.

Jackenstein OAAAHHHH IT STILL HOARTS!!!!!!!!!

Susie and Ralsei calm, though Ralsei's eyes are still wide.

H-huh? Sorry, I guess I'll, um, try it again...!

Susie's gaze drops.

Umm, maybe I could...

Ralsei turns to look at Susie, smiling.

Susie...?

Susie puts her hands behind her head, grinning faux-casually.

Umm, nothin'. Nevermind.

A slight pause. Susie turns and walks offscreen down the path; after a brief moment, Ralsei turns back to Jackenstein. The camera pans over to focus on Kris as Ralsei begins repeatedly attempting to heal Jackenstein. Control is returned to the player, who can now check in on Ralsei or Susie.

Talking to Ralsei and Jackenstein

Ralsei is repeatedly trying to heal Jackenstein; the outside of his head keeps flashing with magic.

How's this? Feel better? Jackenstein NO... STILL HOARTS...

Checking on Susie

Walking far enough to the left causes Kris to stop as the camera pans over to the left to show Susie standing with her head bowed, unsmiling.

... Gerson How's the adventure, hero?

At the words from offscreen, Susie lifts her head, startled. The camera pans to the side to reveal Gerson. Slow guitar begins playing as he takes a few steps forward.

... Old man?

She turns to the side away from the player.

Shouldn't you be busy... writing a letter or something?

She turns back to Gerson as he laughs, then jabs his cane at her, one hand on his beard.

Gerson ... Shouldn't you be helping that pumpkin fella?

He lowers his cane. Susie turns away, her gaze falling as she speaks.

Nah, Ralsei's got it covered. I... I quit.

She turns back to Gerson. He strokes his beard for a moment, then stops smiling.

Gerson Quit? Quit what?

Susie turns to the side and scratches her hair.

You know. Healing. That green sparkle thing.

Gerson stops stroking his beard and takes a few steps forward, smiling again and watching Susie. When he speaks, Susie turns to him with a matching smile.

Gerson Hmm. So you don't like doing it?

Susie turns to the side away from the player.

Well, uh... I dunno, actually.

She turns and takes a step towards Gerson.

At first, I was really... I was really excited I could do it.

Her gaze and smile drop.

But like... I sort of realized...

Susie raises her head, smiling, and Gerson stops watching her as intently.

I just suck at it. Heh.

Gerson strokes his beard for a moment. Then he looks at her steadily.

Gerson What if you practiced? Put a little shell into it.

Susie turns to the side away from the player.

I mean, I kind of... When people aren't looking...

She scratches her head.

I was kind of trying to practice a little, but...

She stops moving, hand still in her hair.

I sorta realized, y'know?

She turns back to Gerson with a smile.

I'll never be as good as Ralsei, so what's the point?

She ducks her head.

So everyone can see how... bad I am?

Susie turns to the side away from the player…

I should just stick to what I'm good for.

…then back to Gerson, smiling at him.

Y'know... uh... hurting people... magic.

Gerson's head dips, his eyes closing.

Gerson Hm. Is that so.

Head still bowed, he turns away. A pause. When he speaks, Susie's eyes widen.

Gerson Didn't take you for a purple bellied coward.

She takes several steps towards him, no longer smiling.

Coward...? Hey, what do you mean by that!?

Gerson stops smiling and shakes his head.

Gerson You're afraid of making some little green sparkles.

Susie charges up in front of Gerson and snarls in frustration; he lifts his head to look at her.

Look, just CASTING it isn't a big deal!

Gerson turns around, stroking his beard.

Gerson Alrighty then...

He takes a few steps away, then turns to face Susie again and lifts a hand in the air as he speaks.

Gerson Here.

Susie straightens up, unsmiling.

What? Gerson I pricked my finger with my pen earlier.

Susie's eyes widen when Gerson continues:

Gerson If yer not a coward, give me your best shot!

She looks exasperated.

Fine, if it gets you to shut up! Take this!!

The music fades out and Susie charges up a healing blast. Just as she's about to let loose, though, a paper falls from Gerson's jacket, and he stoops to fetch it:

Gerson Oop, my letter!

As a result, Susie's healing blast flies well over his head. The camera pans over to the right where Jackenstein is still in pain, Gerson's dialogue box disappearing almost immediately to make way for the scene.

Kris and Ralsei stand in front of Jackenstein, looking at him. Ralsei looks shocked as the healing blast approaches, flying into Jackenstein's gaping mouth. His eye sockets and the inside of his mouth flash green as healing sparkles fly, and Jackenstein stops shaking. A pause. Then he stops clutching his head and leans forward again, no longer pained.

Jackenstein OH!!!! THE OUCHI... THE HOART... IT'S GONE!!!

An unsmiling Susie walks over, stopping in front of Kris. Ralsei turns to look at her, surprised.

... Susie?

Ralsei jumps in place, head tilted up and eyes closed in a smile.

Susie, that was a great idea!

Susie mirrors Ralsei's gentler smile.

H-huh?

Ralsei turns to Jackenstein.

I was wondering why my healing wasn't working...

Ralsei dips his head in a sheepish smile.

I suppose... I was only healing the outside.

He turns around and takes a step towards Susie, whose smile fades.

But Susie, your healing blast seemed to hit the spot!

Susie looks stunned.

Huh?

She faces Ralsei, smile still gone.

Well, heh, I was uh, actually just trying to heal that old man...

A pause. Ralsei's smile fades as well.

... Old... man?

A slight pause. Kris and Susie turn around to look in the old man's direction.

Yeah, he's...

The camera pans all the way to the left to show a completely empty path. When it pans back, Susie's eyes are wide.

...

A long pause. Then Susie smiles.

Huh. Guess he left. Heh.

A pause. Kris and Susie turn back to Ralsei as he walks up to them with a smile.

Well, Mr. Jack is better, so let's be on our way!

The camera refocuses on the party, and as the music resumes, control is returned to the player.

Talking to Jackenstein

Jackenstein waits to the right.

First time

The party bunches up in front of Jackenstein.

Jackenstein YOU... GIRL... YOU HELPED ME... Hey, it was, uh...

Jackenstein spreads his arms wide, then catches up all the party members in a big hug, clutching them to his chest.

Jackenstein YOU THREE... MY FRAINDS!!!

He sets the party members back down.

Jackenstein ME NAME IS JACKENSTEIN.

Jackenstein clutches his head, trembling.

Jackenstein I WAS BULLYED, AND HAITED BY THE OTHERS, FROAM MY LARGE AND UGLY BODDY. Jackenstein I WAS BANNISHED TO THE DARK ZONE, ONLY TO APPEAR ON FESTIVAL NAIGHT...

He leans back, whole body shaking.

Jackenstein I WAIR THIS MASK, BEACAUSE MY TRUE FAICE, SCAIRES EVEN MYSELF.

A pause. Jackenstein leans forward again, no longer shaking.

Jackenstein DID YOU Jackenstein DID YOU WANNA SEE IT Jackenstein COVER YOUR EAYES...

Susie's eyes widen as Jackenstein reaches up and grips his "head", revealed to actually be a mask as he lifts it off. Underneath, a small candle-like white head is revealed. A long pause. Then Susie calms and Ralsei beams up at Jackenstein.

Umm, actually, it's kind of cu--

Susie and Ralsei jump in shock as Jackenstein jams his mask back on and cuts Ralsei off without player input:

Jackenstein ACTUALLY IT'S TOO SCAIRY

The party members all face Jackenstein again as he lowers his hands.

 Jackenstein YES. ME NEATHER. Jackenstein THE POINT IS... YOU THREE... ARE MORE DIFFERENT, THEN THE OTHERS. Jackenstein YOU ARE KAIND. MERSIFULL. AND, MOAST OF ALL... Jackenstein YOU DOAN'T KNOW HOW TO CLAIMB. Jackenstein TAKE THIS... MY FRAINDS... MY CLAIMBING SET. (You got the ClaimbClaws.)

The Claimb Claws are added to the inventory.

Jackenstein IT'S TOO SMALL FOR ME

The camera pans back over to the party members and control is returned to the player, who can now climb out of the Dark Zone.

Subsequent times

Jackenstein YOUNG WOMUN... THAINK YOU FOR THE WONDERFUL HEALING. Hey, it was nothing. Jackenstein LET ME KNOW IF I CAN EVER REPAY YOU... Jackenstein I LIVE IN A HOAL

Leaving the Dark Zone

Hint regarding charge jumps

When first climbing up and approaching a bell, if no charge jumps have already been made, the following message will appear:

Hold [CONFIRM] : Charge Jump

Treasure chest

A treasure chest towards the lower right contains $500 and uses generic narration.

Susie regaining OK Heal

On the way out of the Dark Zone, Susie stops the party.

(Hey, uh, Kris?) (... can't guarantee it will be any good, but, uh...) (Y'know, feel free to ask me to heal again. If you need it.)

Susie's unusable Heal spell reverts to the usable OK Heal. The party can now leave the Northwest Library and return to the study, where the Claimb Claws have rendered the East Belltower newly accessible.
