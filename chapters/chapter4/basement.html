Susie and Noelle, partially visible at the top of the screen, get off the sofa and head for the basement.

Basement

Susie and Noelle enter from the right side of the screen, carrying flashlights which they proceed to look around the area with. As they search, Susie and Noelle talk, pausing between exchanges.

The SOUL can shuffle towards Susie or Noelle; touching either one results in any ongoing conversation being cut off as the scene skips to the girls discovering the doll is moving.

SOUL is discovered

If the SOUL touched Noelle, she jumps in shock, knocking the doll backwards and causing her to briefly lose her grip on her flashlight; her scream alerts Susie, who jumps to her feet.

If the SOUL touched Susie, it startles her into kicking the doll towards Noelle, at which point Noelle gets shocked as previously described.

In both cases, Susie and Noelle point their flashlights at the angel doll-covered SOUL and talk as they watch it for signs of life, pausing between exchanges.

If at any point the SOUL moves, any ongoing conversation is cut off as the scene skips to the girls capturing the SOUL.

Oh my gosh, this just brushed against my leg... What the hell is that!? It's... it's an... angel doll... It... it was goddamn moving!
Dude, it, uh, totally moved though. Let's just... wait and see if it moves...
It's gonna move. Yeah...

Susie and Noelle remain silent after this.

SOUL gets captured

When the SOUL moves, Susie and Noelle both panic and drop their flashlights, running away to the other side of the basement. They come back slowly, Noelle carrying a flat pan and Susie carrying a large candy cane decoration.

Okay, ready to kill. I got a cane... You... ... You're gonna beat it to death with a cookie pan? N-no! No, I'm just going to...

Susie watches wide-eyed as Noelle approaches the angel doll in fits and starts, pauses to ready herself, then quickly scoops the angel doll onto the tray, keeping it pressed down with a hand to trap what's underneath. She turns to Susie, who backs up a few steps.

Huh? Dude, you got guts! There's probably just like... A mouse underneath it, right? It's... nothing to be afraid of.

Susie walks closer.

Damn...

The screen fades to black as Susie and Noelle leave the basement.
