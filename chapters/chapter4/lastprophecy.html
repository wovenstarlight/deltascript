After defeating the Titan

The screen, white from the blinding radiance of the SOUL, fades out into a round Sanctuary room somewhat similar to the one where the Knight created the Titan's Fountain. Kris and Susie are both crouching at its center.

W... We did it... ...

Kris stands, followed by Susie. A pause. Then they look at each other.

... hey Kris, what's the deal with that red heart thing...? Is that... your...

Susie lowers her head slightly. A longer pause. Then she looks startled.

W-wait a sec! The old man! C'mon!! We... we still gotta talk to him!

Susie runs off through the northern path, Kris watching her go. A pause. Then they turn towards the south upon hearing Ralsei's voice.

Ralsei Kris!

Ralsei comes into the room, walking up to Kris. He ducks his head, smiling.

Kris... Kris, you're alright...!

He lifts his head.

I can't believe... it actually worked!

Ralsei ducks his head again, briefly losing his smile.

We... actually defeated a Titan...! If we can do that, then...

Ralsei lifts his head as he speaks, but then looks surprised. He glances slightly to the right, towards the northern path.

Kris, w... where's Susie?

After a slight pause, Ralsei draws back, shocked. A longer pause. Then he turns to the north and sprints after Susie as he yells:

SUSIE, WAIT!!

Kris watches him go. After a pause, control is returned to the player to lead Kris north after their friends.

Discovering the Last Prophecy

In the next room, unlike before, the prophecies are pulsing red and a low, tense heartbeat is playing. Kris hurries down the path into the room, turning at the first bend where a prophecy panel floats:

THE LAST PROPHECY. THE STORY'S END.

Ralsei stands a few steps further down the path, staring at the prophecy panel.

No...

He sprints off to the right, yelling.

SUSIE, STOP!!!

Control is returned to the player, who can have Kris follow after Ralsei, walking past a few more prophecy panels:

AND THEN. WHEN ALL HOPE IS LOST FOR THE TALE THE FINAL TRAGEDY UNVEILS. TO SAVE THE WORLDS, THERE IS ONLY ONE WAY.

Ralsei is standing just a little past this last prophecy panel, looking further down the path, eyes wide. As Kris approaches him (control being taken from the player), the camera pans over to focus on him.

SUSIE, DON'T LOOK--

Ralsei flinches back as a prophecy panel up ahead, out of sight, is shattered, sending a ripple throughout the room that stops the pulsing red lighting and returns the prophecy panels to their usual blue. The heartbeat music is cut short.

Looking distressed, head bowed slightly, Ralsei rushes forward to where Susie stands amidst shards of glass, her back to the player and her right fist dripping blood. As Kris follows, stopping just beyond the glass shards, Ralsei hurries up to Susie and puts a hand on her shoulder, rubbing as though to comfort her. He speaks in a rush, dialogue advancing on its own without player input:

S-Susie, I'm sorry, I-- I, I'm sorry, I-- I'm sorry, I-- Shut up.

Ralsei flinches and freezes when Susie speaks, then takes a step back, returning his hands behind his back. A pause. Suddenly, Susie throws her head back and laughs, and Ralsei looks up, surprised, as soft music begins playing.

The hell are you apologizing for? You're worried about THAT!? Seriously!?

Susie's laughter slows to a stop. She lowers her head.

... this stupid prophecy?

She laughs again, then goes still.

Like something like that would happen.

A slight pause.

I wouldn't let it happen... Kris wouldn't let it happen... And obviously YOU wouldn't let it happen. So...

She lowers her head and turns around to face Ralsei, who lowers his head when she speaks, his face growing more downcast as she continues.

Why wouldn't you laugh? It's just stupid.

Susie lifts her right hand and cups Ralsei's cheek, lifting his head. He looks at her, startled.

So stop crying, 'k? Everything's gonna be all right.

A pause. Ralsei's face falls again as Susie pulls away and turns around, taking a few steps forward with her hands behind her head, wearing a grin.

Heh. Hey, Kris. Let's go seal the fountain, OK?

At Susie's last words, Ralsei's face falls further. As she walks off towards the fountain, Ralsei turns back to where the prophecy once hung in the air, his head still turned slightly towards her. Kris takes a few steps towards Ralsei, but says nothing, and control is returned to the player.

Talking to Ralsei

First time

Ralsei stands in front of the broken prophecy panel, head turned towards the path to leave. Kris stands at his side, facing him.

Ralsei Susie... Ralsei ... she's... Ralsei She's a really really nice person, Kris. Ralsei How can she be so kind...? Ralsei ... how?

A long pause.

Ralsei I'm sorry, Kris. Ralsei I'm sorry I didn't say the whole prophecy at first. Ralsei I just Ralsei I thought if I said something different Ralsei If we did something different Ralsei If we were just kind enough Ralsei Perhaps by the time we got here... it would change. Ralsei But... but no matter what we do...

A slight pause. Ralsei turns to Kris, his head bowed.

Our fate...

After a moment, Ralsei lifts his head, smiling, his cheek bloodied. Another slight pause.

... is already decided.

A pause. Ralsei falls to his knees, hands coming up over his eyes, shoulders rising and falling with unheard sobs.

Ralsei ... Ralsei S-sorry... don't look at me... I... Ralsei I don't mean to always...

Ralsei goes still.

Ralsei Make everything about me... Ralsei I'm sorry. I...

He wipes a sleeve over his face, then lowers his arms, smiling. The blood on his cheek is fainter now.

Everything's OK, Kris. Look, I... I'm smiling!

A pause.

Kris takes a step closer and kneels in front of Ralsei, putting their left arm around him.

K... Kris? Kris, you... Haha...

A pause. Ralsei's head lowers and he looks sad.

I... I'm scared... It's getting harder and harder To just stand there smiling all the time. To be the one Pretending I'm not afraid, too. But I Being with you two... I... The more I'm with you two, I realize... Friendship... isn't just... About being happy all the time. It's something far, far greater than I could've imagined... ...

A pause.

I... I want to. I want to believe again.

On the normal route

I want to believe... it can change! That there isn't just one ending! Susie's hope... Her naive hope... It's... infectious, isn't it, Kris? ... So, until we see fate with our own eyes... Let's believe, too.

On the Weird Route

I want to believe... it can change! That there isn't just one ending! But... What would that be...? If there was something else, what would it be...? And how do we know It wouldn't be something even worse? ... ... Kris... Nevermind. I'll see you tomorrow, okay?

A pause. Kris pulls away and gets to their feet, taking a step back. Ralsei also stands and gives Kris a smile.

... Kris, you can go to the fountain now. Susie's waiting.

Ralsei turns back to face to the spot where the prophecy panel was, and control is returned to the player.

 Of... of course! You can count on me! I'll... make sure we... ... have a wonderful adventure tomorrow! The next day, too... After all, that's what you want, isn't it? ...

Ralsei stands and turns back to face to the spot where the prophecy panel was, and control is returned to the player.

Umm... Kris? You were

You were cool when you played the piano earlier!

Leaving towards the Fountain

The player can take Kris further down the path, past the shattered prophecy and Ralsei. One last prophecy panel floats beside the path:

ONLY THEN, WILL THE WORLDS BE SAVED.

And after passing this, Kris can go through the door leading to the fountain.
