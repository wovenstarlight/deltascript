Entering the dark

Falling

Kris and Susie fall into the darkness, disappearing into the distance. After a moment, they hit the ground, unseen but heard. There is silence for a moment before the rushing of wind becomes audible for several seconds.

The wind abruptly goes silent as the darkness cuts to show Kris is sprawled on the ground in a dark, rocky area; Kris's color palette is altered and they're wearing different clothing. After a moment, they sit up, then struggle to their feet. Control is returned to the player. The area remains silent, Kris's echoing footsteps the only thing to be heard.

(If no Chapter 1 completion file exists, loading a SAVE file created in this area — the Dark World — or choosing to continue after dying will change the window title to read "THE DARK".)

Obtaining the Wrist Protector

Available only in the Chapter 1 Demo, if reaching the Dark World within 8 minutes, most easily accomplished by skipping the initial Light World scenes. Removed completely from the Chapter 1&2 Demo onwards.

Something glimmers in the darkness to the north of the platform where Kris wakes up; it can be interacted with.

First time

(You found something shining in the dark.) ("Even in a rush, you need to take care of yourself.") (You got the Wrist Protector.) (Hold [MENU] to skip text.)

The ability to skip dialogue using [MENU] is enabled. The glimmer disappears, though the spot can still be interacted with.

Subsequent times

(Nothing.)

SAVE point

At times, you see it flickering. The light only you can see. By second nature, you reach out, and...

If you've never made a SAVE in this slot before

The SAVE menu appears, displaying the name "Kris" at "LV 1", with the location as "---".

If you've erased the SAVE in this slot before

The SAVE menu appears, displaying the name "[EMPTY]" at "LV 0", with the location as "---".

Choosing to SAVE overwrites this with your true name (chosen during the vessel creation sequence) at "LV 1", with the location as "??????".

Heading through the cliffs

Past the SAVE point, eerie music begins playing. The player can lead Kris through a rocky area filled with wobbly creatures and piles of dust. The sides of the cliffs bordering the path occasionally have holes leaking a dark fluid; interacting with these gives the following:

(It's too dark to see anything.)

A shadowy figure can occasionally be spotted on the tops of these bordering cliffs, though they move offscreen every time Kris gets near.

Glowshard

Some distance along, there's a split in the path that leads to a side area with another one of the leaking holes in a cliff. This one, however, has something gleaming inside. Interacting with it gives the following:

(There's something glowing inside.) (Take it?) (You got the Glowshard.)

The Glowshard is added to the inventory.

 (It stayed.)

Interacting with this leaking hole after taking the Glowshard gives the following:

(It's dark inside.)

SAVE point

The player can continue on, passing more wobbly creatures that shoot bullets at Kris. There's another SAVE point in the room past this:

If Kris's HP is at max
You bathed your body in the light. A power shines within you, breaking through the darkness. Any pain you may have had melted away... (HP fully restored.)
If Kris's HP is below max
You bathed your body in the light. A power shines within you, breaking through the darkness. The pain you had melted away... (HP fully restored.)
If Kris's HP is below 30

The "spores" don't do enough damage to lower Kris's HP this much unless you reenter the room and get hit multiple times.

For some reason, you punished yourself with the spores. The light relieves you... (HP fully restored.)

Eye puzzle

Next to the SAVE point is a sign:

In this land^1, only eyes blinded by darkness can see the way...

The cliff beside the one holding the sign has a series of holes with something gleaming inside; each hole is labelled with a trio of dots, signalling which of the eye markings above it controls. The player must make all three eyes go dark, which creates a bridge leading onward with a thud.

Finding Susie

Eventually, Kris comes upon a series of dust piles that scatter as they're checked. A shadow darts from one to the next as Kris checks each one. The furthest one to the right scatters to reveal an agitated Susie.

H-hey! B... back off! Come any closer and I'll...!!

Susie straightens up as she looks at Kris.

... K... Kris!? Phew... ... Hey, don't scare me like that, dumbass! Unless you WANT to get clocked in the face, heh. ... anyway, enough screwing around. We gotta find a way outta here.

Susie looks around.

Ummm... Where is "here," anyway...?

Susie looks at Kris.

It doesn't matter! YOU got us into this mess, YOU get us out!

Susie gets behind Kris.

Lead the way, Kris!

Susie is now in the party and follows Kris as they walk.

Going left

So, back the way you came.

Oh my god. Nevermind. You have NO idea where you're going.

Going right

If you walked

Or ran less than a certain amount; you know the drill.

Oh my god. Nevermind. You walk WAY too slow.
If you ran
Huff... puff...

A stranger attacks

In the next room, Susie stands at the base of a cliff. She turns to Kris as they join her.

Hey, Kris. There's someone up there waving at us. Any idea what they want...?

Kris and Susie look up. The shadow up on the cliff raises a hand; spade-shaped bullets form and fly down towards Susie as the shadow brings its hand down. Susie dodges, panicked.

R-run, Kris!

Susie dashes off to the right; Kris follows, dodging spade-shaped bullets along the way. Susie waits for them at the top of a slide.

Kris, down here!

They both slide down, Susie outpacing Kris, and eventually hit the bottom.
