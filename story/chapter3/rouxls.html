Initial meeting

A short distance past the SAVE point, the party stops when a shadow falls over the room and the music fades out.

Rouxls Geh-ha-ha-ha...

The party takes a few steps forward. Susie, Ralsei, and the Pippinses/Shadowguys/occasional Zapper in the background (present only if at least one TV World Darkner has been recruited so far) look shocked as Rouxls Kaard teleports in, a wide-eyed Lanino and Elnina also tumbling out of his teleport beam and falling to the ground. Elnina and Lanino's expressions turn downcast. Rouxls's theme starts playing.

R-Rouxls Kaard!

Susie and Ralsei look calmer.

AND, don't forget my Loverse, Sun Guy and Moon Girl.

Ralsei turns to the side.

Um...

Ralsei faces forward again.

Kris, get ready for another fight! Ha! Quell your mouth, my dearest Bimbitoe!

Rouxls turns around and approaches a snack bowl on the counter.

We're just hereth for the Snackse. Goeth on. I hath no quarrel with thee.

Ralsei briefly looks mildly surprised.

Umm... you don't? But... if you capture us, won't you earn Tenna's admiration?

Rouxls turns back around.

Haha! Oh, please! I am Beyonde such shallow Desires... For you See, I hath already Gotten them! My two Paramours art already TV Guy's Second-In-Commande... And by the Transitive Propertie, that also maketh ME...? This world's Personal Duke of Puzzles?

A long pause. Rouxls takes a step forward, and Lanino and Elnina's eyes grow wide.

Right, Honey 1 and Honey 2? That's howeth this Workse?

A pause. Lanino and Elnina avoid eye contact.

Lanino and Elnina Umm...?

They look downcast again as Rouxls continues.

See? It's totally how it Workse. Now begone!

The music resumes, and the party is free to continue on past the weather duo and Rouxls. Behind the counter, any Darkners resume dancing.

Rouxls's revelation

A short distance past the throuple, the party stops when a shadow falls over the room and the music fades out.

Rouxls Guyse?

The party lines up as Rouxls Kaard teleports in, now alone. His theme starts playing.

So it actually Doesn't Worke. We had a talk, and it doesn't Worke. Even IF I dateth them... I'm NOT?? 2nd in Commande???

The music pauses for a long moment, then resumes.

So. Headsts together. What art we going to Do about That.

The music stops. Susie points threateningly at Rouxls.

If you fight us we'll kick your ass.

Susie lowers her hand as Rouxls continues.

Yes, haha. I Knowst. We shalth not fight.

The music resumes and Susie and Ralsei's eyes widen.

Unless we did... Nonviolentlye?

The music stops. A long pause. Susie and Ralsei stop smiling.

Please don't start a battle. Okaye,

A long pause. Susie and Ralsei's eyes widen and any TV World Darkners behind the counter jump in shock when Rouxls continues.

But what if... I Do

The encounter begins.

After the fight

As the encounter ends, the screen returns to the Cooking Challenge area. Behind the counter, any Darkners have resumed partying. In front of the party, Elnina and Lanino are standing facing each other, both blushing with lowered gazes.

Lanino Oh my sweet cloud... I... I... after you! Elnina No, my sunny moon! I... I... You go first!

A three-second pause. Then Elnina and Lanino begin crying.

Lanino I really didn't like that guy that much! Elnina He was hot but really weird! Lanino He moved the laser pointer too fast!

Elnina and Lanino stop crying with a sneeze, then straighten up, blushing with lowered gazes again.

Elnina and Lanino put their sunglasses back on. Both vanish with a sparkle as they are recruited.

The camera pans back over to the party. After a pause, Rouxls lifts his head from behind the counter, next to a bowl of snacks; the party looks over at him.

Looke. I saidst what I cameth for.

Rouxls snatches the bowl up and disappears behind the counter. The music resumes as control is returned to the player, who can proceed on to find Tenna.
