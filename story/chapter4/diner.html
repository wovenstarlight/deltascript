The hangout

While QC goes through the door behind the counter, presumably leading to the kitchen, Kris and Susie sit down in the corner booth. Susie yawns and folds her arms on the table, resting her head on them.

The diner, huh... Haven't eaten here since... Your mom took me. I remember plowing into my food with a runny nose. I probably became every janitor's enemy after that. ... But... it's funny. Even though I was only making a mess... Her face looked so... Happy. Just sitting there... smiling, watching me. ... Kind of like you're doing now. ... Jeez. Kris, anyone tell you your family is goddamn weird?

QC comes out carrying a tray. As she approaches their table, Susie sits up to make space, and QC puts down Kris's hot chocolate.

QC Here you go. QC And here's a little something extra for your friend.

She sets a sundae in front of a surprised Susie.

Huh!? QC If you need anything else, just holler.

QC returns to her spot behind the counter.

... Alright, Kris. Don't hold anything back.

The screen fades to black. The weapon-drawing SFX is followed by an explosion.

... phew. ... Want the last bite of my sundae?

The following options are presented for 1 second.

Jeez, if you're that energetic. The hell do you want me to have it so bad!?

If you didn't make a choice in time

Too slow! Hahaha!

Someone swallows. A pause.

So, guess we should get going, huh...? ...? What are you doing? Breathing on the window...?

Kris draws on the window with a squeak.

... Is that me? Heh. Not bad, y'know. ...

A pause.

Here, lemme draw something.

Susie draws on the window with a squeak.

It's you. Whaddya think? Heh, what can I say. I'm an artist. Even got your ear in there. One of them. Whatever. That's because I'm drawing from life, dumbass!

Susie draws on the window with a squeak.

Look, I made you talk. "Susie rulez"!

If you selected "Draw something" earlier

What are you-- I WOULDN'T SAY THAT!!! Here, I'll help.

Susie draws on the window with a squeak.

Now I'm saying "Shut up".

A pause.

And then the rain fell down and...

Susie wipes away their drawings with a drawn-out squeak.

Washed everything away. Heh.

Kris writes something on the window with a squeak.

... huh? ... are you writing something?

Kris writes something on the window with a squeak.

Heh, Kris... I can never tell when you're messing around.

The screen fades back in to reveal Kris and Susie's table ravaged. Susie yawns.

Well, let's get out of here.

Kris gets up, but before Susie can follow, QC looks over at them.

QC You mind cleaning your place up a bit first, hon? Huh? Ohh, uhhh, yeah, sure... heheh.

The screen briefly fades to black; they set their table to rights, and the screen fades back in to show Kris standing by the table while Susie is still seated. She gets up and joins them.

Alright, let's go.

Another day in hometown resumes playing. Kris and Susie are free to continue exploring Hometown, where they can spend time with Ralsei or go to Noelle's house for their "study session".
