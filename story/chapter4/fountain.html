Susie is standing in front of the Dark Fountain. Kris walks up behind her; she backs out of the way to give them space in front of the fountain. They look at her for a moment, then join her in looking at the fountain.

Susie ... Susie Hey, Kris... Susie ... Susie Can I tell you Susie ... a stupid dream I have?

Susie ducks her head for just a moment, then lifts it again.

Susie ... Susie No matter what Susie I... want to keep being friends with you and Ralsei.

A pause. She bows her head.

Susie When the sun comes up again... Susie I want tomorrow to be the same as yesterday. Susie And the next day Susie To be just the same as that.

A pause. She looks up, turning slightly towards Kris.

Susie That in the end Susie We can always go back to the way things were before.

A pause. Susie bows her head.

Susie ... Susie You don't have to say anything back, Kris. Susie I... Susie ... Susie I know... Susie You're thinking the same thing

She and Kris turn to face each other. A pause.

Susie ... aren't you, dumbass?

A pause. The following narration plays soundlessly.

(Susie's smile, which seemed to defeat everything) (Shined on in front of you, and for a moment) (It was as if all the darkness had been blown away.)

Kris walks forward to the Dark Fountain, Susie watching them go. The music fades out.

(As if in imitation, your SOUL began to glow...)

Kris's SOUL flashes into view. Your Power plays as it floats forward into the center of the Fountain, illuminating it from inside as the SOUL glows bright enough to turn the screen white. The screen then fades into black as Kris and Susie return to the Light World.
