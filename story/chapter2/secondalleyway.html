Initial encounter

Normal route

Kris leads Noelle into the alleyway; suddenly, they pause.

Uh-uh oh--

The camera pans over to reveal Berdly standing further along the alleyway. He turns towards them and is stunned; Noelle also looks alarmed. Berdly's theme begins playing.

Noelle!? What are you doing with Kris!? Um, I... um... I... I...

Noelle and Berdly look calmer as they face each other.

We... have a truce...?

Berdly reels in shock…

A... truce!? With... Kris?

…but then starts laughing.

Hahaha!! That's impossible!

He raises a smug finger.

That would be double trucies.

Noelle looks panicked.

Huh?

Berdly takes a step towards them.

Worry not, my dearest Noelle. I know what you really want to say. You.... you do?

Berdly turns to the side.

Going along with all this... You feel... forced, don't you? It's why you're not with Queen right now.

Noelle puts a hand to her mouth.

Berdly... you get it?

Berdly takes another step closer.

Yes!! I do!!

As a smug Berdly talks, Noelle looks stunned.

Kris is FORCING you to be on THEIR side!! Kidnapper!! Kris, our truce... is OVER!!!

Berdly summons his halberd.

Noelle!! This may hurt, but it's to help you!! W-wait, just listen to me---!!!

Berdly draws Kris and Noelle into an encounter.

Weird Route

Kris leads Noelle into the alleyway; suddenly, they pause, and the camera pans over to reveal Berdly standing further along the alleyway. He turns towards them and notices them.

Noelle! There you are! Just in time! We can both go back to Queen and--

He takes a step closer, but then stops.

... Noelle?

When Noelle speaks, Berdly stops smiling and his eyes widen.

Kris, it looks like another enemy. Should I freeze them? N... Noelle? Noelle, it's me, don't you recognize me!?

Noelle seems to finally recognize him, her eyes widening, looking alarmed and almost hunted. A brief pause - and then tense music begins playing.

...!?!? B... Berdly!? Noelle, are you okay...? What... what are you doing with Kris?

Noelle turns to the side, facing away from the viewer. A pause.

I'm just, we're just... ... Getting... stronger. Getting stronger... how? Kris, what are you doing!? Wh... what? Proceed... where? Noelle, what are they talking about!? ... Th-that's it, Kris! I don't know what you're doing... But if you're hurting my friend Noelle...

Berdly summons his halberd.

Then I have no choice but to stop you by force! Kris, ready yourself!!

Noelle looks terrified.

W-wait, Berdly, stop! Run away!

Berdly draws Kris and Noelle into his Weird Route encounter.

The tense music stops and the Weird Route abort SFX plays. Berdly looks shocked.

From... me!?

Noelle turns around, now facing the viewer.

(Th... that's right, what was I thinking just now?) (That's right... Kris is my friend, right?)

Berdly looks furious.

What absurdity!!! Kris!! Did you leave your IQ points at home!? Noelle is only going to be protected BY ME!

Berdly summons his halberd.

And I'll FIGHT HER to PROVE IT!

Noelle looks alarmed.

W-wait, just listen to me---!!!

Berdly draws Kris and Noelle into his normal route encounter.

After the fight

If Berdly was spared or defeated non-fatally

On the normal route, this always plays. On the Weird Route, if Berdly wasn't brought to 100% Mercy, this plays only if SnowGrave was not used successfully.

Berdly is left kneeling and looking pained.

D-damn it!

Noelle puts a hand over her mouth.

Berdly, are you okay...? Kris, maybe we should have gone easier on him...

Noelle lowers her hand as Berdly stands up and laughs.

Go easier!? Ha! Haha! Hahaha! The only one going easy mode, was me!

Noelle puts a hand over her mouth again.

You look... Exhausted.

Berdly puts on a smug face.

I'm only tired of HOLDING BACK MY TRUE POWER!

He turns, taking a step away from them.

Ah, Noelle. It'd be too simple to save you now. Kris! Go ahead and train your strength values. Try hard, and maybe someday you'll be a worthy rival.

He turns to the side, facing away from the viewer.

Heh! Sometimes I can't believe how cool I am...

Berdly leaves the alleyway, disappearing offscreen to the right. The camera pans back over to focus on Kris and Noelle.

If not sparing Berdly on the Weird Route

Plays on the normal route in all cases, and on the Weird Route if Berdly was defeated violently without using SnowGrave.

If Noelle is below max HP
(He hit me in the face with a tornado...)
If Noelle is at max HP
(If he would just listen to me...)

If sparing Berdly on the Weird Route

Plays when Berdly is brought to 100% Mercy on the Weird Route.

... ... Umm, about just now... ... (Seems like whatever was happening.) (They snapped themselves out of it...) (Proceed...) (Why... did they keep saying that...?) ... w-wait, I get it! You were just trying to make Berdly mad, weren't you!? Come on, that's not nice, Kris. ... Though, it's not like I don't understand the feeling. Fahahaha. I guess we both kind of got carried away...! ... (Really though, what... was I thinking?)

The Weird Route abort SFX plays.

Control returns to the player, who may heal the party at the newly-spawned SAVE point:

After defeating Berdly, a certain scent hangs in the air... You are filled with the power of chicken nuggets.

The player can now lead Kris and Noelle onwards to meet up with Queen.

If Berdly was defeated using SnowGrave

Only available on the Weird Route with the Thorn Ring equipped.

The traffic cones to the north have been knocked aside. The path to the right is blocked by the ice block encasing Berdly. After a moment, the camera pans back to focus on Kris, revealing that the path through which Kris and Noelle entered the alley is also blocked by traffic cones. A SAVE point without narration appears to the left of the knocked-over northern traffic cones.

Interacting with the block of ice gives only the following:

(It's hard to see into the ice.)

Kris can only enter the newly-opened northern alley, a long, winding path. At the end of the path lies an open manhole which can be interacted with:

(It's a manhole. There's a ladder leading inside.) (Enter it?) (You climbed into the manhole...)

The screen fades to black and the escaping SFX plays as Kris presumably heads down into the sewers. After several seconds, they emerge on the other side immediately outside Queen's mansion.

Nothing happens; dialogue ends.
