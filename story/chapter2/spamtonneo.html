Getting into the basement

On the first floor of the mansion, to the right of the door leading up to 2F, is a traffic puzzle. Past this puzzle is what appears to be a storage room of sorts, containing many Queen statues (posed like the Thinker), tables and chairs, blocky vases, stray teacups, and more.

Entering the secret room

The hidden room is largely empty. Towards the left is a switch that opens a shortcut back to the mansion's entrance hall (successive interactions make a beeping noise at increasingly higher pitches). Towards the right, the room narrows and is blocked by a shimmering holographic forcefield that can be interacted with.

Before purchasing the KEYGEN

It has a lock that seems impossible to open.

After purchasing the KEYGEN

(Kris used the KEYGEN.)

The KEYGEN is removed from the inventory, and the forcefield disappears with a mysterious jingle. The party can now walk into the second half of this room, where a door to B1F awaits.
