Annoying Mouse Room 2

Past the SAVE point room, a shopping area, and some traffic-filled streets, Kris and Noelle come upon another mouse puzzle.

Normal route

Puzzle introduction

Upon coming close to the puzzle, the party walks forward, Noelle stopping a few steps in front of Kris.

Looks like another electric wall.

She walks over to the "in" hole.

Huh? This hole has a switch inside of it... Maybe we could try to put something inside...?

She walks back over to Kris.

What do you think, Kris?

Control is returned to the player, who can work on solving the puzzle.

Scaring Noelle with the mice

By releasing mice when she's in the way.

First time

Noelle shrieks and jumps to the left side of the puzzle.

I... I wish they wouldn't jump out at me like that!
Subsequent times

Noelle shrieks and jumps to the left side of the puzzle.

(How many more times am I gonna get hit by mice...?)

Leaving to the west without Noelle

If Noelle has been scared out of the way while working on the puzzle, or after "solving" the puzzle while Noelle is still looking into the "in" hole.

Kris stops when Noelle speaks:

Kris, wait a second!

Kris turns back towards Noelle.

"Solving" the puzzle

By getting the mice to go into the "in" hole.

Noelle walks to a point a few steps away from the "out" hole, then stops and turns to the side towards Kris and the mouse hole.

Kris! You did it! We should be able to...

She turns towards the puzzle, looking shocked.

... huh? The forcefield's still up.

Noelle walks over to the "in" hole, looking inside.

D-did we miss something? H-hello?

She tilts her head slightly, and control is returned to the player.

Talking to Noelle after "solving" the puzzle

While she's looking into the mouse hole.

I wonder what we did wrong... The forcefield is still up.

Taking down the forcefield

Upon releasing mice into the hole again, Noelle shrieks and jumps out of the way, vaulting clear over the forcefield and landing on the switch on the other side. The forcefield goes down. After a brief pause, a visibly mad Noelle storms past the mouse hole wall until she can see Kris:

Kris, I can't believe you made those mice--

A slight pause. Noelle turns back to where the forcefield once was.

Huh?

Noelle turns back to Kris.

I... I guess landing on the switch turned off the forcefield...

A very slight pause.

Let's go, Kris.

The camera refocuses on Kris as control is returned to the player, who can proceed onwards into the next part of Cyber City.

Weird Route

Upon coming close to the puzzle, the party walks forward, Noelle stopping several steps in front of Kris. After a pause, she turns to Kris.

L... Look, Kris! Another puzzle! H... How should we solve it this time? Y... Yeah, you're right, Kris! (I thought Kris was acting strange, but...) (I guess it was just another mean joke...?)

The Weird Route is aborted with a chime. Dialogue ends here, and the puzzle can be solved as on the normal route.

Dialogue continues.

Kris walks several steps forward, and Noelle backs away as the music fades to silence.

H... how? There's nowhere to go. Kris, there's... the path is blocked, isn't it? Y... Yeah, you're right, Kris! (I thought Kris was acting strange, but...) (I guess it was just another mean joke...?)

The Weird Route is aborted with a chime. Dialogue ends here, and the puzzle can be solved as on the normal route.

Dialogue continues.

Kris walks forward and Noelle backs up further.

Kris... what are you doing? Kris, is... is everything okay? Y... Yeah, you're right, Kris! (I thought Kris was acting strange, but...) (I guess it was just another mean joke...?)

The Weird Route is aborted with a chime. Dialogue ends here, and the puzzle can be solved as on the normal route.

Dialogue continues.

Kris walks forward and Noelle backs up further.

Kris, there's lightning in the way... If... if we move any further... I'll be... I'll be... K... Kris, you didn't really hear what I said earlier, did you? You... you wouldn't... let me... Y... Yeah, you're right, Kris! (I thought Kris was acting strange, but...) (I guess it was just another mean joke...?)

The Weird Route is aborted with a chime. Dialogue ends here, and the puzzle can be solved as on the normal route.

Dialogue continues.

Kris walks forward, backing Noelle up against the forcefield.

K... Kris...? Y... Yeah, you're right, Kris! (I thought Kris was acting strange, but...) (I guess it was just another mean joke...?)

The Weird Route is aborted with a chime. Dialogue ends here, and the puzzle can be solved as on the normal route.

Dialogue continues.

The screen fades to black. After a moment, the chiming of Ice Shock sounds. The screen fades back to the puzzle, where the block rotators and switch are now frozen, and the forcefield is absent. A pause.

Th... there, looks like I... I did it? (I just... listened to Kris's command.) (And somehow, before I knew it...) (The puzzle was finished.) (What was I thinking? Kris wasn't trying to hurt me...) (They were just making me stronger.)

The Weird Route is continued with an ominous tune. The silence persists until the party proceeds to the next part of the city, where the cheese maze would normally be.
