Talking to Queen (and Berdly)

As the alleyway opens into a wider square, Queen's slow, hooting laugh makes Kris and an alarmed Noelle stop dead.

Kris, it's Queen... She's right over there! C-Can you distract her somehow!!?

Noelle hurries back the way they came as Kris moves forward, discovering Queen standing in the middle of the square with flushed cheeks and a glass in hand. The camera pans to focus on them, and Queen turns around, noticing Kris. After a pause, her theme begins playing.

Oh Lmao You're here

Queen gives a hoot of laughter, tipping her head back and spreading her hands. She looks at Kris again.

Sorry Kris While I've Been Looking For Noelle I've Had Like 4 Of These All-Ages Appropriate

She turns to the side facing the player.

Glasses of Pure Battery Acid

Queen laughs, then throws her glass to the ground; it smashes to pieces. She straightens back up and laughs again, then turns away from Kris.

Don't Worry That Was My Throwing Glass It's Safe

A pause.

Look Kris

Queen turns to Kris and takes a few steps towards them, the glass shards briefly crunching under her feet.

Kris Cross Applesauce

She turns to the side facing the player.

Now That We Are Trucies I Feel Like We Are On The Same

Queen walks up in front of Kris, glass still crunching underfoot.

Mathematical Wavelength

She takes another few steps and rests an arm on Kris's head, leaning on them and gesturing with a hand in the air.

You Get Me Kris

Queen flattens her raised hand, gesturing vaguely outwards.

You Do Not Do Crazy Things Like "Have Opinions" Or Scream When I Capture You

Her hand lowers slightly, her expression growing slightly morose.

Or Tell Me Horrible Plans For Smartboy Theme Park

Queen clenches her hand in a fist, looking grave.

I Just Cannot Compute It Why Everyone Else Is So... Ungrateful

Queen straightens up and takes a few quick steps away from Kris, who turns to watch as she produces another glass with a laugh. This glass, too, is flung to the ground, but with a cartoonish explosion that sends the shards of the first glass flying offscreen. Queen straightens up.

Whoops That Was My Extra Dangerous Glass

A pause. Queen turns to the side facing the player.

Kris

Queen turns away from Kris.

I Just Want To Make Everyone Smile

She walks away from Kris, stopping near the other side of the square.

And If I Become An Evil Villain To Accomplish That

A long pause.

Is That Bad?

Queen turns to the side away from the player. Another pause.

It's Okay You Do Not Have To Answer

Queen turns to the side facing the player and laughs for a moment, then stills, hand still raised beside her mouth.

You Would Be Wrong

She turns to face Kris.

But Kris... While We're Still Trucies Maybe We Could... Make The Most Of It

With a noise, an arcade machine drops down into the square, landing with a thud as both Kris and Queen turn to watch. The screen beeps on, displaying Queen's face, as she speaks.

We Could Play That Game Again Wasn't That... Fun, Kris

Kris and Queen walk up to the arcade machine, facing each other.

Just You And Me Having A Good Time No One Else To Get In The Way

A huge golden statue falls from above, squashing the arcade machine out of existence with a cartoonish explosion. Kris and Queen turn to look at it. It depicts a muscular, flexing Berdly wearing crocs and briefs labelled "IQ", flexing as a beaming Queen hangs from his bicep. A moment later, Berdly spins down from above to land beside Kris, smiling at Queen. They both turn to face him.

Queen!! I've been looking everywhere to show you this!!

He folds his arms and grins smugly.

A monument to our greatness... Together. Oh

Queen turns to the statue.

Um

She turns to Berdly and begins to speak rapidly while looking between him and the statue, dialogue proceeding without user input:

Oh Uh It It's Oh Well Um Mmm It's

Queen turns away from Berdly.

How Do I Put This Very... ...

She turns to Berdly.

Smart So Smart You Are I Love Tolerating You

Still grinning, Berdly raises a finger.

Haha!! I KNEW you'd be enamored. Such perfect taste.

Queen looks at the statue.

Very Cool Statues I Did Not Know You Had... Nipples

Berdly folds his arms again.

Haha... I will SOON... in our IDEAL WORLD.

Berdly lowers his arms and turns away from Kris.

Kris, feel free to admire our artisanship.

Queen turns to Berdly.

Queen and I have PLANs to consider. Haha Yes Oooh So Good Looking Forward To That MMmmm Yum Yum Yum Yum Yum

She turns away from Berdly and Kris.

Let's Go Burghley

Berdly and Queen both begin walking towards the path leading away to the right, but Queen walks slowly enough that Berdly quickly outpaces her. Once he's out of sight, she keeps "walking" without actually moving forward.

Keep Going I Am Right Behind You

"Walking" more slowly, she backs up a little.

Procedurally Lowering The Volume Of My Voice

She stops faking a walk. A pause. Then Queen turns and speeds over to stand in front of Kris.

Sorry Kris I Must Alt Tab Out Of Here

Walking very fast, Queen heads past Kris to the alley beyond; the camera pans to follow her. As she passes Noelle, standing at the side of the alley with her box-disguise over her head, she speaks:

Wow Cool Face

Queen heads back the way Kris and Noelle came, while the camera follows Noelle as she heads forward into the square, Kris walking to meet her. They both pause at the point where the alley opens up into the square.

Phew... Looks like my disguise worked.

Noelle takes the box off her head.

It's silly looking, but it beats hiding...

She puts the box back on her head.

Let's get going, Kris!

Control is returned to the player and the city music resumes. The moment Kris begins moving, however, Noelle walks forward in a diagonal line, eventually bumping into the statue and falling over its croc-clad foot; Kris turns to look when she falls.

Just realized... I can't see anything... haha...

Standing up, she takes the box off her head.

Guess I'll try not to wear it unless I have to. OK, let's go for real this time!!

She moves to stand behind Kris, and control is returned to the player once again, who can proceed properly this time, moving into the next area of Cyber City.
