Breaking out

Finding yourself trapped

The screen fades from black to a room in Queen's mansion, with a large screen and keyboard taking up the top wall and various objects scattered around the remaining walls. At the center of the room stands Kris in their cage.

After a moment, the monitor turns on and shows Queen grinning with spread hands against a background of blue skies and grassy hills.

How Do You Like Your New Room Units As You Can See They Are Perfectly Suited To Your Interests (Which I Gleaned From Your Internet Search Results) I Estimate You Will Have No Desire To Leave Please Tantilize Yourself With Your Surroundings And Wait Patiently While I Dominate The World Toodles

The monitor turns off. A long pause. Kris turns to the right when a voice comes out of the odd tube on that side:

K... Kris!? Hey!! Can you hear me!? Looks like there's some way to communicate between rooms.

The monitor turns on, showing Queen again.

Convenient Isn't It I Really Thought Of Everything

The monitor turns off.

Shut up!! Anyway, let's think of some way to get out of here!! Ralsei, you got any bright ideas...? ... Hey... where the hell's Ralsei!?

Susie is startled when a high-pitched wail sounds from outside. Kris turns back to the tube when Susie speaks.

Damn, that soft-yet-tender screaming - Kris, that's gotta be him!! Quick, we gotta think of some way to get out!! Anything you smuggled in that might help us!?

Control is returned to the player. Unfortunately, with Kris in the cage, there's not much to do.

Doing nothing

After 10 seconds, Lancer's voice is briefly heard.

5 seconds later, Susie speaks:

Hey, Kris... do you hear something...? Sounds like it's coming from your... pocket? Your phone going off or something?

For the next several seconds, Lancer's voice can be heard at brief intervals. Eventually, Susie speaks again:

Hey, Kris... uh... Does your phone usually, uh... yell...?

Lancer continues speaking at brief intervals. Eventually, Susie speaks again:

Hey, Kris, pick up your damn phone. Isn't it with your KEYs or whatever?

Checking Kris's pockets

As Susie hints, the only recourse is to check Kris's pockets, where Lancer awaits attention in the key item inventory:

Hoho! It's me, Lancer! Release me and I will release you!

Selecting Lancer causes the key item menu to close as Kris to take out the Jack of Spades card. With a mysterious noise, the card spins down to the ground outside the cage, where it turns into Lancer, also spinning, until he comes to a stop facing Kris. His theme begins playing as he speaks:

Missed me??? Because I missed you!! Ha ha ha!! Lancer!? That you!? I thought you ditched us!

Lancer turns away from Kris.

No!! I love ditches but I would never ditch you!!

He faces Kris once more.

I was simply relaxing in Kris's spacious Pants Hole. Uhh, "pocket."

Lancer moves towards the left side of the room, turning to face both Kris and the tube through which Susie is speaking.

Yes!! And I saw it all!! Pants. Lint. Various Items.

He turns to the side facing the player.

A wild Mom put you in some Shock Cages. Hahah, yeah. We're trapped, so, uh... Hey, wait didja see us on the roller coaster!?

Lancer takes a small jump closer to Kris's cage.

Yes!!! It was amazing!! My first coaster ride!!!

Lancer begins spinning around rapidly.

Ha ha!! I love getting nauseous with friends!!

Lancer and Susie laugh together, and as they go quiet, Lancer stops, facing the door. He then turns back to Kris and Susie's talking tube when Susie speaks.

Hey you should probably, uh, free us.

Lancer turns to the side facing the player.

Oh right!! Yes!!

He turns back to Kris and Susie's talking tube.

You can always rely on a friendly Me... To make you free!!!

Kris turns to watch Lancer as he goes up to the large monitor at the top of the room. It turns on, displaying a blue screen and blinking cursor. A pause. Lancer turns to Kris.

Haha!! I have no idea how to use this!!

Lancer jumps onto the keyboard, bracing himself on his hands and feet as he studies the screen.

"Request... new item for room"?? That sounds good.

Lancer begins crawling across the board to type several letters on the screen.

Item request: Shovel.

Lancer types a bit more.

Quantity: Let's say... 999.

Lancer jumps off the keyboard to the ground as the monitor begins flashing red, flashing the message "QUANTITY OVERLOAD". The electric cage around Kris splits apart and breaks away; Kris and Lancer both turn around once the cage is gone.

Hoho!! It worked!!

Lancer comes to stand in front of Kris.

Let's go, blue person whose name I know!

Spinning, he leaves the room; his theme fades along with his departure. Control is returned to the player, who can explore Kris's room (the last opportunity to do so) before following Lancer outside.

In the guest hall

The screen fades to black as Kris exits their room, then fades in on the hall outside, where Lancer has found Susie also freed from her room. Kris walks into the hall and turns to face the other two. Harpsichord music begins playing.

Kris, hey!!! Man... Lancer...! You're the best, dude!!

Susie and Lancer jump and high-five each other. Then Susie turns to look further down the hall.

C'mon, Noelle and Ralsei are waiting!!

Susie takes several quick steps forward.

Yes...! Let's put the pedal on the medal!!

Lancer loses his smile and shakes slightly.

Cough... cough*

Lancer's colors begin to fade, pulsing weakly. When he fails to move after a moment, Susie turns around.

...? Hey, Lancer, you ok, dude? I... I'm fine! Just... very... Cold...

Lancer falls to his back, shocking Susie.

H... hey, what the hell!? ... Hey, are you OK, dude...? Here, lemme heal you...!

Susie straightens up, smile gone, and shoots a healing blast at Lancer. Once it hits, she hurries over to him.

... th-thanks, Susie... but...

Susie turns to the side facing the player.

D... darn, it's not strong enough, huh...

She turns to Kris and Lancer.

Quick, Kris, let's find Ralsei! Maybe he can heal him!

Carrying Lancer on her back, Susie leads Kris further down the hall. They stop a short distance down and turn around, however, when the door beside Susie's opens and a serious-looking Berdly comes out.

Wait!!! WAIT!!! Y-you two aren't going to leave without ME, are you!? Uhhh... yeah we are. But Noelle - my damsel in distress - she must be...

Berdly droops, looking upset.

... in distress! And if I cannot help her, then...! Okay FINE you can come if you shut up.

Susie and Lancer hurry off, Berdly following; control is returned to the player. They can check the various rooms of the guest hall and stop by the SAVE point to the left:

You have escaped from your second personalized room of the day. You are filled with the power of vacation homes.

Eventually, the player must follow Susie and Berdly to the east.

Calm your skinless, boneless, thighs everyone. I'LL think of a solution! (Kris, just ignore him.) I'll just be over here where I can't hear you. And I'll be here, away from your IQ-debuffing aura!

I knew the answer, I just wanted to see Kris try, first! It's called SUSPENSE, Susan!! DRAMATIC, suspense!!

Arms still folded, Berdly's eyes dart to the side anxiously as Susie takes a step towards him.

Then YOU do the next one.

Berdly lowers his arms and walks past her into the next room as he responds:

Fine! WATCH me!!

Once he's gone:

(Also my name's not Susan?)
