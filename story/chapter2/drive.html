Running into Queen

After crossing a traffic-filled street, Kris and Noelle slow to a stop on a stretch of pavement as the music fades out. Queen speaks from somewhere offscreen:

Noelle puts on her box disguise as Queen's theme begins playing. Queen herself enters from the eastern street and stops in front of Kris, holding up a green pie.

Queen notices Kris.

Queen turns around and flings the pie to the ground, where it shatters with an explosive boom; she turns back to Kris.

Queen clutches her head, gasping in horror.

A car pulls up alongside the stretch of pavement everyone is standing on; Queen, Kris, and Noelle all get into the car. A moment later, Berdly walks in from the eastern road and stops on the same stretch of pavement, holding up a green pie. He turns around a few times, presumably looking for Queen.

My Queen!! Where are you!! I've baked you a Gamer's Delight!! Hmm... she's not here...

Berdly speeds off towards the west, on the path Kris and Noelle came from. The others exit the car, standing on the pavement again; Queen faces the other two, who are turned to the side facing the player.

A pause. When Queen speaks again, Kris and Noelle turn to her.

Queen steps forward, but Kris takes a step back towards Noelle, blocking Queen's path to her. A pause.

The trio gets back in the car, which begins driving; Queen's theme is replaced by her mixtape.

Noelle reminisces

Entering the next area, Kris and Noelle come upon a pair of narrow paths, dotted with small forcefields and switches. Some of the switches have letters on them, the first visible one bearing a letter D. On the wall beside the switch is a panel displaying a question mark with a small speaker underneath.

The party stops at the point where the second path begins.

Gosh, that was close, wasn't it, Kris...?

Noelle walks in front of Kris, stopping under the question mark-displaying panel and turning to Kris.

This... Strange world... this big city... It's so wild... It kind of makes my head spin.

Noelle turns to look at the panel on the wall, tilting her head.

...?

A pause. Noelle stops tilting her head.

Looks like another puzzle...

Noelle turns around, looking at the second path.

Maybe if I go... down... here?

Noelle backs up slightly, then jumps over the traffic cones separating the two paths; Kris turns to watch her. She proceeds down the path to step on the D switch and looks over to Kris's side of the path. Sure enough, the panel on the wall changes to read "D" (voiced aloud from the speaker), and the forcefield in Kris's way fades out of sight.

Control is returned to the player, who may guide Kris over to the blank switch on their side, removing the forcefield in Noelle's way. This pattern repeats through the rest of the room, with Kris using blank switches to take down forcefields in Noelle's way, while Noelle does the same for them with lettered switches.

Noelle presses a switch reading "E". As she and Kris walk forward, they pass behind a building in the foreground; their silhouettes can be seen "through" the building, in their associated yellow and blue respectively. Stopping at the next forcefield in her way, Noelle speaks to Kris while waiting for them to clear her way:

Noelle ...when I said this city makes my head spin... Noelle I didn't mean in a bad way... you know? Noelle I always wanted to go someplace new, you know?

Noelle presses a switch reading "C".

Noelle ... And Dess... always told me she'd take me. Noelle Somewhere like this. Noelle Somewhere with shining lights.

Noelle presses a switch reading "E".

Noelle ... don't you miss exploring, Kris? Noelle Back when the four of us were kids? Noelle Gosh, remember that night we explored the forest behind the graveyard?

Noelle presses a switch reading "M".

Noelle Ha, I know we never found anything interesting back there... Noelle I mostly remember... Crying because I was scared.

Noelle presses a switch reading "B". When she passes behind the next foreground building, her silhouette is that of a small, much younger Noelle, her hair up in pigtails instead of loose down her back.

Noelle But for some reason I feel... nostalgic thinking about it.

The duo pass behind another foreground building; Noelle's silhouette is back to her normal teenage form.

Noelle Dess wiping away my tears with Azzy's jacket... Noelle With warm sleeves that smelled like cinnamon.

Noelle presses a switch reading "E".

Noelle I guess I didn't mind being scared if it meant... Noelle Someone would comfort me.

Noelle presses a switch reading "R" (and finishes spelling the full message, "DECEMBER"). Kris turns to her as she jumps over the traffic cones and walks over to them.

(Sigh...)

She turns away from Kris.

(Kris is the only one who knows how weird I am.) (It's not fair, y'know!?)

She turns back to Kris.

(Everyone knows how weird YOU are. Fahahaha.)

Control is returned to the player, who can lead the party of two onwards to a carnival area.
