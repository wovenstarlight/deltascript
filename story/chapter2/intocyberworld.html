Into the new Dark World

Initial entrance

As Kris and Susie fall into the library's Dark World, a sparkle leaves Kris's pockets. Kris and Susie land on a green platform with blinking electronic-themed patterns around it. Susie looks behind herself.

... This is new.

She turns to Kris, and control returns to the player.

Exploring further

Past a SAVE point and cliffs that Kris and Susie can slide down are some distraught, trembling Plugboys who can be spoken with.

Meeting Queen

As Kris and Susie walk along the damaged platform past the third and fourth Plugboys, they hear a voice.

Noelle Somebody help me...

Kris and Susie, no longer smiling, walk forward. The camera pans over to show Noelle in Dark World clothes kneeling on the ground, face in shadow.

Noelle Somebody, please...

Noelle notices their presence and looks up, startled.

Susie!?

Noelle smiles awkwardly.

Cool that you're here, just...

She looks alarmed again.

Wait, get out of here before it's too late!

A cage floats in from the right and drops over Noelle, startling her and Susie. Kris and Susie rush forward, but aren't fast enough to stop the cage from flying away with Noelle.

NOELLE!

With a long, extended laugh, Queen rises from beyond the damaged platform, sitting in her rocket-powered throne. Her theme begins playing.

Hell Of A Study Session Who the hell are you!? I Am Known As Serial Number Q5U4EX7YY2E9N But You Foolish Children May Call Me

Queen holds up a finger and takes a drink from her glass of acid. Then, as she speaks, screens pop up to her left reading "Queen" with an arrow pointing towards her.

"Queen"

Susie jabs an accusing finger at her.

Heh, like we're gonna call YOU "Queen,"

Susie falters, looking to the side uncertainly.

"Q5U....3...7..." uh,

Another set of screens labelling her "Queen" pop up, this time to the right.

"Queen"

Susie points at her with renewed energy.

Well LOOK, "Queen." We're not "children!"

The screens disappear to be replaced by new ones on the left, showing a stick figure labelled "STUPID" turning into a bigger stick figure labelled "BIG STUPID".

Teens Are Merely Big Children And Adults Are Even Bigger Children

Susie briefly looks away, confused.

... doesn't that make you a child too then?

Susie looks back at Queen as more screens pop up to the right, with a simplistic computer labelled "SMART" turning intoâ€¦ the same thing, a computer labelled "SMART".

No I Am: A Computer (Smart)

Susie looks confused.

... computer?

Queen's chair flies up and away and her screens disappear.

Well Thank You For The Stimuli But I Must Leave Now (Goodbye)

Susie steps closer to the edge of the platform and points accusingly at her again.

Wait! WAIT!! Why'd you capture Noelle!?

Queen floats back down in front of them, with Susie backing away as she approaches.

I Would Have Captured You Too But I Ran Out Of Cages

Susie looks exasperated.

Why the CAPTURING!?

Susie looks over when a screen depicting Noelle appears beside Queen.

So That She May Become My Willing Peon

Several more screens appear, these ones in red and showing globes with smiley faces above them, as Queen spreads her hands in demonstration.

In My Quest For World Domination

Another green screen pops up beside the one depicting Noelle, with an arrow showing her face replaced by a robotic face.

Also Maybe I Will Make Her Face Into A Robot One?

Susie looks alarmed.

What!? Why!?

Queen laughs.

Seems Cool Well, FORGET it, dude.

Queen returns to swishing her wine glass as Susie points at her.

Nobody's turning anyone's face into a robot! Could That Be A Statement Of Animous Dissension Huh? You Wanna Fight, Loser

Susie draws her axe and begins repeatedly striking the ground with it.

YES!!! YES ALREADY, YES!!!

Queen's screens all disappear and she flies away again, this time to the right, with Kris turning to watch her go.

Oh Then Bye

Susie's axe strikes get much faster.

WAIT! A GODDAMN! SECOND!! I Have No Time For Such Frivolities (And Would Kick Your Ass) But Perhaps Someone Else Could Entertain You

Susie stops wielding her axe; she and Kris turn around as the camera pans back over to the Plugboys. Susie and the Plugboys look alarmed and frightened as wires descend from the sky and latch onto the Plugboys' faces, lifting them up into the sky.

The camera pans back to the right, where Queen floats at the end of the platform. Susie and Kris turn to watch as the Plugboys are lowered down, quivering at the ends of the wires for a moment before transforming into Werewires with an electronic crackle.

Enjoy: Your Assimilation

Kris and Susie are drawn into battle with the Werewires.

After the fight

Upon leaving the encounter, Susie turns to Kris.

Phew, nice fighting again after so long!

She turns around, facing the path Queen was floating by.

Now let's go catch up to Queen!

At that moment, two more Werewires drop down from above, startling Susie.

... or not!

Before the encounter can begin, however, the Werewires are pacified and fall asleep, turning back into Plugboys and disappearing.

Huh? They calmed down...?

Kris and Susie turn around to see Ralsei walking up to them.

I just used my PACIFY spell to put them into "Sleep Mode"! Ralsei! You're here!

They all huddle closer together, forming a semicircle with Ralsei and Susie at either end.

I felt a dark presence and hurried over!

Ralsei turns to Kris.

It seems that a new Dark Fountain has appeared...

Ralsei turns back to Susie when she turns away and speaks, looking unhappy.

Ralsei... they got Noelle!

She looks briefly exasperated.

Damn, what was she THINKING messing in our Dark World!?

Susie turns aside.

If we don't do something, she might be...

Ralsei speaks, drawing her attention.

Worry not, Susie! All we have to do is seal the Fountain! ... yeah. Yeah!

Susie smiles again.

Can't be mad about another adventure, right!?

She and Ralsei turn to Kris.

C'mon, Kris!

Kris, Susie, and Ralsei jump into a line, spin, and strike poses.

Let's go!

Control returns to the player. They can proceed past the damaged platform to a slide, where Susie and Ralsei quickly outpace Kris. Sweet flies next to the slide on rocket-powered turntables and sends bullets down the slide in time to the cheery music that starts playing. After the intro, Sweet flies out of sight as the screen flashes white, and the party finds themselves at the bottom of the slide near a SAVE point. They enter Cyber Field, where they eventually run into Queen.
