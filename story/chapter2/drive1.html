Running into Queen

After crossing a traffic-filled street, Kris and Noelle slow to a stop on a stretch of pavement as the music fades out. Queen speaks from somewhere offscreen:

Noeeeeelle Honey Where Are You (Oh no, here she comes...)

Noelle puts on her box disguise as Queen's theme begins playing. Queen herself enters from the eastern street and stops in front of Kris, holding up a green pie.

Noelle Come Home I Made You A Battery Acid Pie

Queen notices Kris.

Oh, Kris Perfect Timing

Queen turns around and flings the pie to the ground, where it shatters with an explosive boom; she turns back to Kris.

Have You Seen -- Oh my Luxurious Quuuueeeeeenn!!

Queen clutches her head, gasping in horror.

Oh My Circuits Kris Get In The Car

A car pulls up alongside the stretch of pavement everyone is standing on; Queen, Kris, and Noelle all get into the car. A moment later, Berdly walks in from the eastern road and stops on the same stretch of pavement, holding up a green pie. He turns around a few times, presumably looking for Queen.

My Queen!! Where are you!! I've baked you a Gamer's Delight!! Hmm... she's not here...

Berdly speeds off towards the west, on the path Kris and Noelle came from. The others exit the car, standing on the pavement again; Queen faces the other two, who are turned to the side facing the player.

That Was Close

A pause. When Queen speaks again, Kris and Noelle turn to her.

Kris Who Is Your Friend

Queen steps forward, but Kris takes a step back towards Noelle, blocking Queen's path to her. A pause.

My Face Recognition Software Does Not Compute Them .... And Yet Something About Them Seems

A pause. When Queen speaks again, Noelle turns to the side facing the player.

Cool Robotic Even (...)

Queen turns to her car. Meanwhile, Noelle collapses to the ground in apparent relief.

Kris We Must Drive To Find Noelle Everyone Get In The Car

The trio gets back in the car, which begins driving; Queen's theme is replaced by her mixtape.

Driving the car

As soon as the car passes the crosswalk, Queen gives instructions on how to drive:

On PC and PlayStation consoles:

Kris Press Directions To Steer

On non-PlayStation consoles:

Kris Press Directional Buttons To Steer

As cars start appearing:

Kris Don't Hit The Cars!

If a car is hit, it flies offscreen with a yelp:

Haha Okay Actually Hit All The Cars

(After 4 cars are hit, newly-spawned cars begin fleeing from the player's car.)

After several seconds:

So Much Traffic Isn't It Wonderful Lightners Love Traffic They Look It Up All The Time Poor Noelle She Would Love Hitting All These Cars Umm, actually, I... she, um, might not, um... Stop Everything Kris Get The Banana

As Queen speaks, a rotating banana appears in the middle of the street.

If you get the banana:

Potassium

If you don't get the banana:

Kris You Are Going To Get Sick
Here Comes A Crossing Get Ready To Stop

The car comes upon a crosswalk and stops immediately after it.

Traffic jam

With the car stopped safely beside a road packed with cars, the music fades to silence as Queen speaks:

Oh My Circuits It's A Traffic Jam A Dog Must Have Wandered In The Road Again Kris Be A Dear And Go Press The Walk Button It's Just Over On The Other Side Of The Road

The camera pans to show a full 10 lanes of cars filling the road bumper-to-bumper, with the eastern road also filled with traffic. On the other side, there is indeed a traffic light post. The camera then pans back to the car.

Umm... I'll go, too... No You Must Stay !? Until You Hear All Of My Mixtape

Queen's mixtape fades back in.

O-okay... (Kris... hurry up, please...)

Kris gets out of the driver's seat. Traffic pulls up to fill the road they came from, and control is returned to the player.

Checking the car

Next Up Is My CD Of Vintage Hard Disk Noises (Kris, please hurry...)

Kris may proceed into the alley that will take them across the road, but not without encountering a Cyber World denizen.
