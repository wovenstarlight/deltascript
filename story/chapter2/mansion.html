Discovering the paintings

The first corridor the party enters has a knock-off Mona Lisa painting on the wall, using Queen's face and making the OK hand gesture. Past it is a wide monitor; as the player leads the party past it, they stop in front of the monitor as it turns on, showing Queen.

I See You Have: Escaped From Your Room Units

Kris turns to the monitor as Susie and Ralsei crowd behind them.

Who Can Blame You For Wanting To See My Glorious Mansion You Won't Want To Take Your Eyes Off This Beautiful Art

Susie points aggressively at Queen on the monitor.

We're not here to look at your stupid pictures! Oh It Was Not A Recommendation I Was Just Warning You LMAO

The monitor turns off, and the entire segment of wall it's on spins around to reveal three more portraits of Queen. From this point onwards, the portraits begin spitting fire at regular intervals.

Respecting the pottery

In the next room is a single Queen-themed pot, with a monitor on the wall behind it. A short distance into the room, the party stops as the monitor turns on to show Queen.

Now I Know You Are All Loving My Mansion But We Have A Few Rules Around Here You Have To Follow One Wipe Your Shoes Two Have Fun Three Respect The Pottery Four Have Fun Five List Entry Duplication Error

The monitor turns off. Control is returned to the player, who can lead the party members onward while giving the pot a wide berth. Alternatively, walking into the pottery causes it to break. The monitor begins flashing the text "POTTERY NOT RESPECTED", and two hostile red Swatchlings bearing brooms enter from either side of the room, hurrying towards the party to draw them into an encounter.

Balancing pottery

The next room has more pots that must be avoided for fear of angering the Swatchlings present in the room. Partway through the room is a starting line; walking up to this line causes a pot to fall onto Kris's head. Walking left or right causes the pot to tip to one side. The pot must be kept balanced until the finish line further in the room, or it will fall off and break, again angering the Swatchlings in the room (and leaving the challenge area empty after the resulting encounter ends).

Making it to the finish line

A victory jingle plays and the Swatchlings begin clapping. Susie and Ralsei walk in front of Kris and turn to face them.

Heh, not bad, Kris!

She punches Kris playfully on the shoulder. Unfortunately, this makes the pot on their head wobble and fall to the ground, angering all nearby Swatchlings. Susie and Ralsei look alarmed for a moment. Then Susie speeds off down the path ahead. Kris and Ralsei are free to move a second later and can follow after her, or risk an encounter with the Swatchlings who begin chasing after them with brooms.

End of 1F

The area past Tasque Manager's room contains a door leading up to the second floor via a teacup ride; this progresses the main plot.

To the right of the 2F door is a traffic puzzle, past which is what appears to be a storage room of sorts, containing many Queen statues (posed like the Thinker), tables and chairs, blocky vases, stray teacups, and more.

Finding the hidden creature

As the party approaches one of the tables, it will shift as something hides under it; the table can be identified because its legs will not be visible after the creature hides underneath. Interacting with it causes the party to notice the creature:

Hey, there's something under this one!

The party lines up in front of the table, and the creature underneath is alerted. The table lifts up as the creature stands; its legs are visible underneath, resembling those of a small white dog. The dog backs into the east wall, knocking the platter on its own table as well as the whole of the adjacent table-and-platter offscreen. The party members rush to stand in its way, headed by Susie.

Don't try to run away, you little...

The dog darts forward; the party members all jump out of its way, but end up landing onto the table it's carrying, and Susie and Ralsei look stunned.

HEY, WAIT A SEC!

The dog begins racing back and forth between the north and south walls, bouncing off them and knocking all the tables in its way offscreen. The player can guide it left and right. Eventually, one of the platters it knocks into will fly open to reveal the key to the doors. The key hangs in the air for a second with a sparkling chime, and Ralsei turns to it.

There's the key!!! Now we just have to--

A pause. Then the dog darts forward, shattering the key to pieces.

It BROKE IT?!

The dog moves to the center of the room. Then it darts forward again, this time blasting clear through the doors. The screen fades to black, then fades to show the next room, where Swatchlings run frantically out of the dog's way as it charges to the end of a long hall. When it knocks into the far wall, the party is thrown off the table and to the ground; the dog then runs off towards the right, while platters fall from above to block the way down the long hall.

Susie grins, posing.

Hahah, was that awesome or what?! Umm, maybe minus the blatant destruction...

Susie and Ralsei turn to Kris.

Let's go, Kris! We're almost at the third floor!

Kris turns around to face the player, and control is returned to the player, who can head left past a corridor overlooking the torso and grin of GIGA Queen towards the teacup ride to floor 3.
