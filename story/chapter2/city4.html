Cheese maze

Exclusive to the normal route. Replaced by two Maus encounters on the Weird Route, both towards the right side of this area.

Discovering the first cheese

Past the Annoying Mouse Room 2, Kris and Noelle enter an alley where a lone slice of cheese stands to one side. Interacting with it gives the following dialogue:

Kris... You aren't going to touch that cheese, are you? (You rubbed the cheese down like a beloved pet.) (Suddenly...!)

A squeak sounds and Noelle looks briefly panicked as a Maus appears from further down the alley, drawing the party into an encounter.

After the encounter ends, Noelle speaks:

I... I never want to see cheese ever again...

Unused variant, if Noelle is not in the party

Impossible to get, as this area can't be accessed when Noelle is no longer in the party. Would have played instead of Noelle's post-encounter dialogue above.

(The cheese was destroyed in the heat of battle...) (You realized you lost sight of what was important...)

Dialogue ends.

Interacting with the destroyed cheese

The lone slice of cheese is destroyed after its linked Maus encounter ends, or after entering the cheese maze (even if the second Maus encounter isn't triggered).

The full cheese maze is destroyed after its linked Maus encounter ends.

(The cheese was destroyed in the heat of battle.) (Feels like you lost sight of what was important.)

Unused variant, if Noelle wasn't in the party when destroying the lone cheese

Impossible to get, as this area can't be accessed when Noelle is no longer in the party.

(The cheese was destroyed in the heat of battle.) (This marks a cruel victory for those who hate cheese.)

Discovering the cheese maze

Plays only if the first Maus was encountered.

Upon spotting the maze, Noelle stops to yell:

KRIS!!!!!!!!!!!!!!!!!!!!!!!!!

Touching the cheese walls will result in a dual-Maus encounter. The cheese maze can be traversed to find a treasure chest containing a CD Bagel on the left, or a path leading to the Annoying Mouse Room 3 on the right.

Annoying Mouse Room 3

Normal route

Puzzle introduction

The music fades to silence as Kris and Noelle approach the broken path, overlooking the gap.

It's a dead end...?

Noelle walks down the nearby stairs to the left mouse puzzle platform. Kris follows her, stopping at the foot of the stairs and turning to watch Noelle.

When Noelle steps on the switch at the right side of the platform, a display towards the top right switches from reading "??????????" to "I?????????" with a robotic voice speaking the letter aloud (as with all following letters). Nothing else happens, though, and Noelle turns to the side facing the player, then away from the player, seeking a change.

Hmm... the switch didn't seem to work?

As she finishes speaking, however, the narrow path leading to the platform she's standing on disappears with a thud. Noelle, having turned to look, grows panicked when the platform itself begins shaking shortly after and tense music starts playing.

Kris!! The platform's falling!! H-help me!!

The Annoying Mouse Room 3 sign fades in and a (noninteractable) forcefield blocks Kris from climbing the stairs to leave. Control is returned to the player, who can use the block rotator or wait things out.

Solving the puzzle

When the player releases mice at Noelle, she shrieks and jumps onto the switch of the right platform, adding an L to the message above her, so that it now reads "IL????????".

The music fades out, and Noelle turns to Kris, looking mad.

Kris!! What are you doing!! You made me --

Noelle calms down, still facing Kris.

H... huh?

She turns away from them, smiling sheepishly.

S-sorry, Kris, you just saved me, didn't you...?

Noelle looks alarmed as the right platform shifts towards the left, replaced by yet another puzzle platform to its right. The platform she's standing on quakes as fast-paced music begins playing.

Th... this one's falling, too! Kris, whatever you just did, keep doing it!!

Failing to solve the puzzle

After a fair bit of time passes with Kris standing at the block rotator but not solving the puzzle, the shaking platform falls, taking Noelle with it. Noelle is brought back up a moment later by a stack of Maice, and jumps with a shriek onto the right platform, which has now shifted to the left. The music fades out and Noelle looks panicked as she speaks:

KRIS!!!!!!! Gosh, you're lucky Dess isn't here! Otherwise YOU'D be facedown in a mousepile! ... S... sorry, I didn't mean to get so mad. Sure... okay, we can try again.

Control is returned to the player, who can begin working on the puzzle, with each additional solved platform resulting in a letter added to the message above. Alternatively, they can simply wait for each platform to fall, and let Noelle switch between platforms and spell out the message on her own.

Berdly's monologue

While the puzzle is in progress, Berdly appears from the path Kris and Noelle entered the room through. He stops halfway down the steps and addresses Kris, seemingly not having spotted Noelle. His dialogue proceeds through the following lines automatically without needing the player's input:

Ahh, Kris. Stuck on this puzzle, I see? I thought I heard Noelle calling for help, but... I get what that sound was now. Kris. Your brain must be crying. Poor, sweet, Noelle. Where could she be now. Stuck on a puzzle just like this one, no doubt. If only I was there to do it for her. She's but a powerless doe without my help... (Powerless!?) Though being alone might be better than being with you! Kris, you would only get in the way! After all, you can't understand Noelle like I can. Yes, you may have been her lifelong neighbor. But. I am a maestro of emotional intelligence. I can read her feelings like a strategy guide. Which, I have NEVER needed by the way! You may see her as the simpleton girl next door. SIMPLETON? But the reality. Is that she is. A lovestruck girl with her heart on her sleeve. ... H-huh? Berdly? And... Kris... The target of her affection... May be surprising to you. BERDLY??? BERDLY KNOWS??? It's... Truly sad. Such love... May never fruit. ... But still, I have to support her, Kris. She's my friend. Berdly... Also, my only worthy opponent at racing games. WHY THAT!?

A two-second pause.

(Is that really what you're gonna end it on!?)

Solving the puzzle

After Noelle has jumped between enough platforms, the switches she lands on eventually spell out the message "ILOVEMOUSE" (the message turns green the moment the last letter is entered). The music fades as Berdly climbs to the top of the stairs and looks out over the now-unbroken path.

What. How did you...

Berdly looks mad, raising clenched fists.

HOW DID YOU SOLVE THAT??

Berdly seems to realize something, straightening up and smiling as per normal.

Even a broken clock is right once a day.

Berdly walks down the path, stopping near the middle of the screen.

And Kris, if anyone asks...

Berdly looks mad again, shouting down towards Kris.

I solved the puzzle!! It's not fair for YOU to get extra credit for a fluke!! Get it!? Let NO ONE think otherwise! ESPECIALLY Susie! You KNOW she'd just... lord it over me!

Berdly walks off to the east, no longer smiling. Once he's gone, the path leading from Noelle's platform back to the main path reappears with a thud. Noelle crosses it, stopping at the base of the stairs and turning around, and Kris turns to face her. To the right, the platforms sink into the dark as Noelle speaks:

Gosh. ... Y'know, I don't think I'm afraid of mice anymore. Actually, I think they might be kind of... cute? Anyways, let's keep going.

Noelle turns to face the player.

(Noelle is now immune to mice.)

The city music resumes. Control is returned to the player, who can proceed past a newly-formed SAVE point and a trashcan-littered street into another alleyway where the party confronts Berdly.

Weird Route

The music fades to silence as Kris and Noelle approach the broken path, overlooking the gap.

I'll... go ahead and do the puzzle!

Noelle walks over to the mouse hole on the left platform, inside which something is glinting. She tilts her head, and the scene fades to black. A pause.

Noelle Isn't it a good thing? Noelle I'm solving things by myself. Taking new things for myself. Defeating enemies by myself. Noelle And every time I do it... Noelle I'm getting stronger. Noelle ... what did I do when we got that ring? ... when I solved that puzzle? Noelle ... It... It doesn't matter. It doesn't matter if I can't remember. Noelle It doesn't matter if the battles are blurring together. Noelle If Kris tells me to do it... Noelle I can do things I could never do before. Noelle Isn't it a good thing? Noelle ... I'm getting stronger. Noelle ... Noelle As long as I... just do what they say. Noelle As long as I...

After a slight pause, the chiming of Ice Shock is heard. The scene fades back to show both mouse platforms absent; Noelle is now standing just behind Kris. The alley is unbroken.

All finished, Kris. Should we find some more enemies?

The Weird Route is continued with an ominous tune. Control is returned to the player, who can proceed to freeze the encounters in the trashcan-littered street ahead. They can then backtrack to Spamton's dumpster and purchase the Thorn Ring before proceeding into another alleyway where the party confronts Berdly.
