Mouse balloon puzzles

The next area contains a broken pathway, which can be passed through by solving puzzles involving mice tied to balloons. Kris can pop the balloons using forcefield switches, dropping the mice into a basket they find along the way and carry on their head. The gathered mice can then be deposited at mouseholes along the way, which require a certain number of mice to open up the path leading forward.
